Class 9
Receivers for satellite-based positioning and time synchronization, in particular for global satellite-based navigation (GNSS) and regional satellite-based navigation (RNSS); antennas for receiving radio signals for satellite-based positioning and time synchronization, in particular for global satellite-based navigation (GNSS) and regional satellite-based navigation (RNSS); software for satellite-based positioning and time synchronization; software for satellite-based positioning and time synchronization systems based on a differential global positioning system (DGPS); software for satellite-based positioning and time synchronization systems based on Precise Point Positioning (PPP); computer chips; recorded or downloadable data concerning topographies of microelectronic semiconductor products; radio frequency jammers; electrical and electronic equipment to interfere with the electromagnetic spectrum, in particular to interfere with global satellite-based navigation systems (GNSS), regional satellite-based navigation systems (RNSS), and ISM frequency band-based systems, as well as equipment and components specifically designed for this purpose; electronic signal spoofing transmitters; electrical and electronic devices for deception of the electromagnetic spectrum, in particular for deception of global satellite-based navigation systems (GNSS), regional satellite-based navigation systems (RNSS) and ISM frequency band-based systems, as well as equipment and components specifically designed therefor; electrical and electronic equipment for electronic countermeasures (ECM) and electronic protective measures (ECCM), including electronic jamming and deception equipment, in particular equipment designed to generate jamming or spoofing signals in receiving systems or to otherwise disrupt the reception, operation or effectiveness of electronic receivers, including countermeasure equipment.
