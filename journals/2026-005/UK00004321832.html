The mark consists of a stylised, full-colour logo. The logo features a cartoon-style puppy holding a rectangular chew in its mouth, positioned within a circular badge design. The background of the badge depicts mountain scenery and countryside elements, and includes a stylised representation of a yak. Beneath the design appears the wording “OZA CHEWS” in bold, stylised lettering, with the word “OZA” presented prominently and the word “CHEWS” in a contrasting stylised font. Below this wording appears the phrase “100 Natural Yak Chew” in smaller stylised text. The mark is depicted in colour, including orange, blue, white, yellow, green, brown, and black.

The applicant disclaims any exclusive right to the wording “100 Natural Yak Chew” apart from the mark as shown.
