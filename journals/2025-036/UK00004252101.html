DTEX RISK-ADAPTIVE FRAMEWORK

International priority date claimed: 19 August 2025 (Australia) (2578581)
Class 9
Downloadable computer software and recorded software for data loss prevention (DLP), insider risk management (IRM), user activity monitoring (UAM), and cyber threat detection; downloadable software featuring behavioral analytics and artificial intelligence for identifying, managing, and reducing cybersecurity and data risks; Downloadable computer software for implementing risk assessment frameworks based on behavioral science and proprietary research; software utilizing behavioral analytics and large language models (LLMs) to detect escalating user risk and address insider threats, data misuse, and compliance issues. .
Class 41
Training and Education; Educational services, namely, providing training programs and workshops in the fields of cybersecurity, insider risk management, AI governance, and data protection; Advisory services in the field of security education, namely, advising organizations on best practices for proactive security strategies, risk governance, and compliance awareness.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) featuring software for data loss prevention (DLP), insider risk management (IRM), user activity monitoring (UAM), and cyber threat detection; SaaS incorporating behavioral analytics and artificial intelligence for managing cybersecurity and data protection risks; Software as a service (SaaS) featuring software platforms that provide risk assessment frameworks based on behavioral science and proprietary research; SaaS using behavioral analytics and large language models (LLMs) to proactively identify insider threats, data misuse, and compliance challenges; Consulting services in the field of cybersecurity technology, namely, deployment, configuration, and optimization of security and risk management platforms; technical support services, namely, troubleshooting of computer software problems related to cybersecurity and risk management solutions.
Class 45
Consulting services in the field of regulatory compliance and corporate governance, particularly relating to cybersecurity, insider threats, and AI policy and risk management.
