QUADREAL RESIDENTIAL PROPERTIES

Class 9
Downloadable application for mobile phones for residential, commercial and industrial tenants that provides building access control and allows tenants to arrange maintenance and repair services, make rental payments, reserve building amenity spaces, communicate via an electronic message system with building facilities management teams, access a tenant only online marketplace, receive package delivery notifications, search for and redeem loyalty reward offers and take part in online contests.
Class 35
Administrative services relating to real estate; customer loyalty rewards program for real estate tenants; providing a real estate tenant reward program that allows participants to earn rewards, prizes and free gifts.
Class 36
Financial services, namely investment management, capital investment, administration of investment plans, private equity fund investment services, investment of pension plan funds, administration of pension plans, financial asset management, financial placement of private equity funds for others, hedge fund services; Real estate property and asset management; real estate investment services; mortgage services; asset based lending services; real estate financing services; financial services, namely construction loans, mezzanine loans and loan syndication services.
Class 37
Maintenance and repair of kitchen appliances, hot water heaters, HVAC systems, residential plumbing and electrical systems and door locks; real estate development.
Class 38
Providing access to an online portal for tenants to access information in the field of maintenance and repair requests and rental payments, to arrange maintenance and repair services and to make rental payments.
