UK00004304852 3 December 2025 (35,36,42,45)

Hybrid World Commission

Class 35
Business administration and management of sporting organisations, motorsport series and governing bodies; administration of regulatory frameworks and compliance processes; organisation, supervision and management of membership programmes, accreditation schemes and licensing systems; commercial administration of motorsport teams, franchises and participation rights; negotiation, procurement and management of sponsorship, advertising and commercial partnerships; business consultancy relating to sport, motorsport governance and competition structure; management and administration of databases, records and registration systems relating to drivers, teams and technical entities; provision of business information, analysis and advisory services relating to motorsport, sporting competitions and governing bodies.
Class 36
Financial sponsorship services; financial management of sporting organisations and governing bodies; administration of membership schemes, subscription-based programmes and fan engagement platforms; collection and processing of payments, fees, registration charges and licensing revenue relating to motorsport competitions and participant accreditation; issuance, brokerage and management of digital tokens, membership tokens, fan tokens and non-fungible tokens (NFTs); crowdfunding and fundraising services; brokerage, valuation, appraisal and financial consultancy relating to motorsport teams, franchises, drivers, vehicles and sporting assets; financial services relating to the sale, licensing and transfer of team rights, franchise rights and participation licences; provision of financial information and analysis relating to sporting events, competitions, teams and governing bodies.
Class 42
Accreditation, testing and certification services relating to motorsport vehicles, components, equipment, teams and participants; development, assessment, enforcement and auditing of technical regulations, standards and specifications for motorsport competitions; design, operation and maintenance of digital licensing systems, driver passport systems, scoring frameworks and compliance databases; provision of technical consultancy relating to vehicle safety, engineering, performance analysis and regulatory compliance; development and management of software, data platforms and digital tools for officiating, timing, telemetry, scrutineering and technical governance; scientific and technological services relating to motorsport engineering, safety analysis and performance evaluation.
Class 45
Regulatory, disciplinary, investigative and arbitration services relating to motorsport; governance, enforcement and administration of sporting codes, rules and conduct standards; stewarding, adjudication and dispute resolution services; issuing of licences, permits, accreditations and regulatory authorisations for participation in motorsport competitions; oversight and enforcement of compliance by teams, drivers, officials and participants; provision of regulatory information, guidance and advisory services relating to motorsport governance and ethics.
