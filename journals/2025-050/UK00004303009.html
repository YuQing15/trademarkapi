Trade Mark Journal No.2025/050 12 December 2025

UK00004303009 30 November 2025 (41,43,45)

The mark consists of a circular design featuring a stylised Christian cross and musical motif in the centre. The wording “Brave Moves Christian Ministries & Music Trust” is used in association with the logo. The colours purple and white are claimed as distinctive features of the mark.

Class 41
Education, Music, Worship & Community Events; Organisation and hosting of Christian music festivals, worship gatherings, prayer events, and faith-based community celebrations; provision of live musical performances, concerts, and creative arts activities; educational services including workshops, teaching, training, mentoring, and seminars relating to Christian faith, music, wellbeing, and community development; production of audio, video, and online content for educational and worship purposes; arranging and conducting retreats, youth programmes, and outreach events promoting spiritual growth and community cohesion; All of the above services being provided within the context of Christian ministry and charitable community work.
Class 43
Food, Hospitality & Community Feeding Programmes; Provision of food and drink as part of charitable and community events; operation of community cafés, soup kitchens, and foodbanks; preparation and distribution of meals to individuals in need, including the homeless and vulnerable; temporary accommodation services in connection with Christian outreach and charitable relief activities; hospitality services provided during festivals, community gatherings, and ministry events; All of the aforesaid solely for charitable community support, outreach programmes, and Christian ministry.
Class 45
Christian Ministry, Charitable Outreach & Social Support; Christian ministry services; pastoral care, prayer support, and spiritual counselling; organisation and delivery of charitable outreach programmes for homeless, vulnerable, and disadvantaged individuals; provision of emotional, spiritual, and practical support within a Christian framework; coordination of volunteer programmes for community improvement, crisis response, and humanitarian aid; faith-based community advocacy and social action initiatives; All of the above services being provided for charitable, pastoral, humanitarian, and Christian ministry purposes.
