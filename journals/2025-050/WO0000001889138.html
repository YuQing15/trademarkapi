The mark contains the colours dark blue, blue, turquoise and white.

The letters “MS” appear in white and are placed within a dark blue circular background. A white droplet shape appears below the letter “M”. To the right, the letters “MS” appear in blue. The words “FUEL SAVER” are displayed in white and are placed over a rectangular background that transitions from blue on the left to turquoise on the right, creating a gradient effect.
