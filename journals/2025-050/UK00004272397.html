UK00004272397 1 October 2025 (41,42,45)

Class 41
Training and educational services; continuing professional development (CPD) seminars and workshops in rights of light, daylight/sunlight, wind microclimate, party wall and neighbourly matters, measured surveys and building surveying.
Class 42
Scientific and technical consultancy in the built environment; building surveying; inspection of buildings (surveying); land and measured building surveys; topographical surveys; area referencing (IPMS, GIA, GEA, NIA); boundary and title plan preparation; digital twins and scan-to-BIM/CAD/REVIT; reality capture including point cloud and photogrammetry; drone surveying; preparation of technical drawings, CAD, BIM and 3D computer models; daylight, sunlight, overshadowing and sun-hours-on-ground assessments; climate-based daylight modelling; radiance studies; light-pollution, solar glare and solar-convergence assessments; tree-impact daylight/sunlight assessments; planning and EIA daylight/sunlight chapters; peer review; wind microclimate assessments including computational fluid dynamics (CFD) analysis and wind-tunnel testing; pedestrian comfort and safety assessments; outdoor thermal-comfort analysis; wind-loading and roof/terrace uplift assessments; ventilation and internal overheating CFD; solar studies including solar-access and shading analysis; solar photovoltaic (PV) potential and yield modelling; portfolio and city-scale PV potential assessments; payback and system-sizing estimates; simulation and visualisation of PV glint and glare impacts; monitoring of building structures; vibration monitoring; project monitoring and independent technical reviews; technical due diligence and pre-acquisition surveys; development massing optimisation and feasibility studies; advisory and consultancy relating to land, property and construction; party wall surveying services, including acting as building owner’s surveyor, adjoining owner’s surveyor or agreed surveyor under the Party Wall etc. Act 1996.
Class 45
Neighbourly-matters dispute-resolution services relating to land and property; preparation and service of party wall notices and awards; negotiation of access, boundary and oversailing (crane and scaffold) licences and rights-of-way and easements; neighbourly liaison; expert-witness services in disputes concerning rights of light, daylight/sunlight and party wall matters; advice regarding Light Obstruction Notices and the prevention of prescriptive rights of light; advice under Sections 203–206 of the Housing and Planning Act 2016.

GIA Surveyors Ltd
