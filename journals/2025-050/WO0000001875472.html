The mark contains the colours blue, white, grey, yellow, green, red, and black.

The mark consists of three non-Latin characters presented in blue with a white outline and grey shading, positioned above the words “POKKÉN TOURNAMENT” presented in a stacked manner and depicted in stylized yellow font with a black outline. To the left and the right of the term “POKKÉN” are rectangles with slanted edges featuring a black outline and containing three horizontal lines depicted in green, red, and blue. The white background is not claimed as a feature of the mark.
