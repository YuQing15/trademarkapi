Global Advisors for Global Citizens

Class 35
Business management, business administration; accountancy, accounting, bookkeeping, and auditing services; financial auditing services; consultancy and advisory services relating to administration services relating to business transactions and financial records, for example, book-keeping, drawing up of statements of accounts, business and financial auditing, business appraisals, tax preparation and filing services; Business Management Consultancy; book-keeping; tax research; administrative services and consultation related thereto; consultation related to secretarial services; incorporation of companies; commercial business consultation for companies; consultation in the field of business efficiency.
Class 45
Legal and consultancy services, namely, consultation, representation, and advisory services in the fields of corporate, commercial, business, tax, and financial law; legal advisory and consultancy services relating to mergers, acquisitions, joint ventures, corporate restructuring, corporate governance, and compliance ;  drafting, negotiation, and review of legal documents and commercial contracts; legal services relating to intellectual property, namely, registration, protection, enforcement, licensing, and portfolio management of trademarks, copyrights, patents, and trade secrets; advisory services relating to arbitration, mediation, and alternative dispute resolution; litigation support services; administrative and regulatory legal consultancy services; Private client legal services, namely, estate and succession planning, wills and inheritance advisory, trust and fiduciary structuring, and family governance solutions; legal and strategic advisory services for high-net-worth individuals, family offices, and private investment structures; Legal advisory and consultancy services relating to citizenship and residency planning, immigration law, and cross-border compliance; legal services relating to visas, residence, and citizenship, namely, advice and assistance in the preparation and filing of applications for residence permits, permanent residence, naturalization, and citizenship by investment; legal services relating to the formulation, development, and implementation of immigration, residence, and citizenship programs; Legal consultancy and advisory services provided to governments and public authorities in connection with immigration, residence, and citizenship programs; legal research and analysis concerning international visas, immigration, and citizenship law and policy, namely, research into visa policy, visa-free access regimes, and comparative studies of governmental programs in the area of international visas, immigration, residence, and citizenship; Advisory and consultancy services relating to compliance with domestic and international laws and regulations, including anti-money laundering (AML), counter-terrorist financing (CTF), know-your-client (KYC), sanctions, and economic substance obligations; legal services relating to regulatory compliance, risk management, and internal governance; Legal and consultancy services relating to asset protection, private wealth management, global mobility, and cross-border succession planning; representation before governmental, regulatory, and judicial authorities; and all related legal advisory, documentation, and research services ancillary thereto.
