Class 9
Downloadable mobile applications; downloadable computer application software for mobile phones and wearable devices for tracking, monitoring and analysing health, wellness, fitness and hormonal data; downloadable software for integrating, viewing and displaying data from wearable activity trackers, laboratory test results and user-inputted lifestyle information; downloadable software for providing personalised recommendations in the fields of health, wellness and lifestyle optimisation; downloadable software for managing user health programmes, progress tracking, coaching interactions and community engagement; none of the aforesaid being computer game software or electronic wallet software.
Class 42
Software as a service [SaaS] featuring software for the collection, monitoring, analysis and visualisation of health, wellness, fitness and hormonal data; software as a service [SaaS] featuring software for integrating data from wearable devices, laboratory tests and user-inputted in for motion and generating personalised health and wellness insights; providing temporary use of online non-downloadable software for managing health optimisation programmes, tracking user progress and delivering personalised recommendations in the fields of health, wellness and lifestyle optimisation; design and development of software in the field of health and wellness; all of the aforesaid not relating to computer game software or platforms.
Class 44
Providing information and advice in the fields of health, wellness, men’s health, hormonal health and lifestyle optimisation; health counselling and lifestyle counselling relating to nutrition, exercise, sleep, stress management and hormonal health; consultancy and advisory services relating to health and wellness; provision of personalised lifestyle and wellness plans based on analysis of user data and laboratory test results; providing online information, reports and assessments in the fields of health, wellness, men’s health and hormonal health; all of the aforesaid services being non-medical in nature and excluding the provision of medical diagnostic or medical treatment services.
