WILD KITE

Class 33
Alcoholic beverages (except beer); wines; sparkling wines; fortified wines; alcoholic preparations for making beverages; alcoholic fruit beverages; alcoholic cocktails; alcoholic cider.
Class 35
Retail services, wholesale services and online retail services in relation to alcoholic beverages (except beer), wines, sparkling wines, fortified wines, alcoholic preparations for making beverages, alcoholic fruit beverages, alcoholic cocktails, alcoholic cider, non-alcoholic beverages, and corkscrews, bottle openers, glassware, coasters, ice buckets, wine racks and decanters; the bringing together, for the benefit of others, of a variety of goods, namely alcoholic beverages (except beer), non-alcoholic beverages and corkscrews, bottle openers, glassware, coasters, ice buckets, wine racks and decanters, enabling customers to conveniently view and purchase those goods from a retail store, on-site retail outlet/shop, catalogue, internet site, mobile application, by mail order or by means of telecommunications; administration, organisation, operation and supervision of customer loyalty, sales, incentive and promotional schemes; procurement services for others (purchasing goods and services for other businesses); sales promotion for others; advertising services including direct mail advertising, publicity column preparation, shop-window dressing, demonstration of goods and presentation of goods (wines, sparkling wines and related products) on communication media for retail purposes; business management and administration services; business consultancy and advisory services relating to the retail of alcoholic beverages and corkscrews, bottle openers, glassware, coasters, ice buckets, wine racks and decanters; data collection and management for business purposes; organisation of commercial or advertising events, public relations campaigns and exhibitions; organisation of wine tastings, wine tourism events and vineyard tours for commercial or advertising purposes; auctioneering of wines; business advice and information to consumers; administrative processing and tracking of orders for wines and related goods.
