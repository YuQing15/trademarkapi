Dart Setups

Class 35
Advertising services; promotional services; affiliate marketing; dissemination of advertising for others via the internet; online promotion of goods and services of others; providing consumer information and advice relating to sporting goods and equipment; comparison and recommendation services relating to sporting goods; business management and administration of online communities; provision of online marketplaces for buyers and sellers of sporting goods; advertising and marketing services provided via online and digital platforms.
Class 41
Providing information relating to sports; providing online publications, blogs, videos, and non-downloadable digital content relating to darts, professional players, and sporting equipment; educational and instructional information relating to sporting equipment and player statistics; providing online non-downloadable videos featuring equipment demonstrations and analyses; providing online user-generated content, rankings, and voting features for community-submitted designs.
Class 42
Providing online non-downloadable software for configuring, customising, designing, and visualising sporting equipment in 2D and 3D; software as a service (SaaS) featuring software for selecting, displaying, comparing, saving, downloading, and sharing customised equipment setups; hosting of online tools enabling users to create, publish, and share customised equipment designs; hosting of platforms enabling online communities to interact with and view user-generated designs; providing temporary use of online non-downloadable configurator and design tools.
