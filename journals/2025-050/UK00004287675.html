Class 35
Business certification and accreditation of artisans, studios, and workshops; verification of professional credentials, quality standards, and sustainability practices; business networking and online marketplace services for bespoke and handcrafted goods; consultancy and advisory services relating to craftsmanship standards, production ethics, and supply chain transparency.
Class 40
Custom manufacture, fabrication, and finishing of furniture, materials, and bespoke objects; application of craftsmanship standards to material treatment and production; quality control, inspection, and verification services relating to manufacturing, fabrication, and artisan processes; consultancy services in the field of bespoke fabrication and craftsmanship quality assurance.
Class 41
Education, training, and workshops relating to craftsmanship, fabrication, innovation, and sustainability; development and delivery of accreditation programs, online courses, and publications promoting excellence in design, bespoke fabrication, and artisan skills; organisation of awards, exhibitions, and events recognising craftsmanship excellence and innovation.
Class 42
Software as a Service (SaaS) for verifying artisan credentials and issuing certifications; blockchain-based systems for provenance tracking, secure contracting, and quality assurance; design and development of digital platforms and AI tools for managing craftsmanship standards, verified collaborations, and data-driven innovation in bespoke fabrication.
