ChargeBat

Class 9
Portable power chargers; rechargeable batteries; power banks; charging stations for electronic devices; battery charging devices; mobile charging kiosks; automated rental stations; vending machines; NFC-enabled devices for access and payment; contactless payment terminals; downloadable mobile applications for managing power bank rentals; downloadable software for locating, reserving, and unlocking portable chargers; electronic devices for wireless charging; USB cables; charging cables.
Class 37
Rental of portable power chargers; rental of power banks; rental of rechargeable batteries; rental of charging cables;   rental of mobile charging devices via kiosks and vending machines.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) featuring software for managing the rental and sale of portable charging devices, charging accessories, and vending machines; development and maintenance of mobile applications for locating, reserving, unlocking, and paying for portable power chargers and accessories; operation of online platforms and websites for managing rental stations, vending machines, and usage tracking; provision of web-based software tools for monitoring the status and availability of portable charging devices, charging cables, mobile charging kiosks, and vending machines; design and development of APIs for integrating rental operations, inventory tracking, vending machine control systems, and payment processing; design and development of websites and web portals for portable device charging services, power bank rentals, and the operation of vending machines and automated rental machines.
