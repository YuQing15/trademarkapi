The Savvy Marketing Guy

Class 35
Marketing consultancy; digital marketing services; business development consultancy; provision of marketing strategy and implementation advice; consultancy relating to brand positioning, personal brand development and online visibility; advertising and promotional services; creation and execution of marketing campaigns; consultancy and support services relating to lead generation, sales funnels, and conversion optimisation; assistance with CRM setup, automation workflows and email marketing strategy; social media marketing consultancy; copywriting and content strategy for marketing purposes; analysis and optimisation of marketing performance; consultancy relating to the use of technology and software to grow small businesses.

Education and training services in the fields of marketing, business development, personal branding and automation; provision of in-person and online training workshops, seminars, coaching sessions and strategic business events; providing non-downloadable webinars, tutorials, instructional videos and podcasts; organisation and facilitation of group training, live Q&A calls and mentoring sessions; creation and delivery of online courses and learning programmes; publishing of digital and physical educational content; production of video and audio content for educational and promotional use; provision of business mentoring and strategic coaching for entrepreneurs and small business owners; hosting of in-person and virtual events for educational purposes; production and dissemination of training materials in the form of guides, workbooks, templates and printed handouts. .
Class 42
