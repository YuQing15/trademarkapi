Voucho

Class 35
Provision of an online marketplace for buyers and sellers of digital and physical gift vouchers; advertising of discounted vouchers; promotion of third-party brands through voucher resale; facilitating commercial transactions between private individuals via a website platform. .
Class 36
Payment processing services; facilitation of electronic funds transfer between buyers and sellers; provision of escrow services for digital voucher transactions; financial transaction services related to the resale of prepaid gift cards and vouchers. .
Class 42
Providing temporary use of online non-downloadable software for operating a voucher resale marketplace; platform as a service (PaaS) featuring software for listing, managing and processing gift vouchers; hosting of e-commerce platforms; design and development of computer software for online marketplaces. .
