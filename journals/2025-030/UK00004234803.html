The word ‘Brent’ does not form part of the mark; it will be replaced according to the name of the borough being accredited and licensed to use the mark. The name ‘Brent’ is also being used to illustrate the colour combinations featured in the mark and to provide the full impression of the mark used by licensees (accredited boroughs). In addition, the black coloured backgrounds in Mark 2 and Mark 4 do not form part of the mark, they have been used merely to provide a contrast so that the words ‘Brent: A Nature Borough’ are visible.

Class 42
Certification and accreditation services relating to business management, business administration, business advisory, business information and business consultancy services, including relating to public sector businesses, commercial enterprises, government, non-governmental organisations and industrial enterprises, including in the conduct of their business relating to nature, conservation, the environment, bio-diversity, ecology, planning, land management, and transport policy; certification and accreditation services relating to the development and execution of green infrastructure strategies and green space improvement and maintenance plans; certification and accreditation services relating to community empowerment and participation, effective leadership, cross-sector delivery partnerships, and coherent improvement and investment plans in the green space sector; certification and accreditation services relating to funding, financial management, governance and workforce development; accreditation services; quality assurance services; quality audits; testing, analysis and evaluation of the services of others for the purpose of certification; process monitoring for quality assurance; certification [quality control] services.
