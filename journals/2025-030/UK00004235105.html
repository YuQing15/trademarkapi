The mark consists of a stylised square emblem bearing the words ‘SPRATTS PATENT’ in capital letters, arranged above and below a pair of decorative crossed strokes at the centre, which resemble curved ribbons intersecting over a small central circular node. The interior background features ornamental symbols including dots, crown-like marks, chevrons, and circular shapes. Around the perimeter of the design appears the phrase ‘NONE ARE GENUINE UNLESS SO STAMPED.’ in a stylised arrangement. The mark is presented in black and white and evokes the appearance of a historic biscuit seal from the Victorian era.
