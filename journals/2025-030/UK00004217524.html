DROP-IN

Class 9
Downloadable mobile application software for coordinating carpooling and ridesharing for participants in surfing and other sporting activities; software for scheduling shared transportation to beaches, sporting venues, and recreational locations; GPS-enabled software for matching users based on activity destinations and timing; mobile software for managing user profiles, trip scheduling, route planning, and pickup coordination for sport-related transport; software to connect people who take part in the same type of sporting activities.
Class 39
Provision of carpooling and ridesharing services for participants in surfing and other sporting activities; transportation arrangement services enabling individuals to share rides to beaches, sporting venues, and recreational locations; coordination of shared vehicle journeys based on activity destination and timing preferences; providing information relating to sport-based travel routes, estimated arrival times, and ride availability.
