Class 1
Chemicals; Chemicals for use in industry and science; chemical preparations for use in the manufacture of semiconductors, optical coatings, aerospace coatings, protective coatings, OLED displays, quantum dots and other specialised functional coatings; chemical reagents in liquid, powder, crystalline or solid form, including emulsions, dispersions and suspensions, for use in laboratory and industrial processes; coating compositions [chemicals] other than paint, requiring dissolution, mixing, dispersion, homogenisation or reconstitution before use, for terrestrial and non-terrestrial use.
Class 7
Machinery; Machines and apparatus for mixing, dissolving, dispersing, homogenising and reconstituting chemical, pharmaceutical and biological substances in liquid, powder, crystalline or solid form, including emulsions, dispersions and suspensions; industrial mixers for use in semiconductor, optical, aerospace, protective and specialised coating manufacture; automated laboratory mixing apparatus not for medical purposes.
Class 10
Medical Devices; Medical and laboratory devices for the mixing, dispersion, dissolution and homogenisation of pharmaceutical preparations in liquid, powder, crystalline or solid form, including emulsions, dispersions and suspensions; automated medical apparatus for preparing drug formulations for infusion or injection; sterile mixing systems for clinical and hospital use.
