AOTC MindSafe

Class 41
Provision of accredited education and training services in the fields of mental health, wellbeing, safeguarding, and welfare; delivery of training programmes and workshops for the creative industries including film, television, theatre, music, and sports; design, development and delivery of bespoke training courses; online and in-person instruction; certification of facilitators, instructors, and trainers; operation of a training academy; provision of qualifications aligned with national and international regulatory standards.
Class 44
Provision of mental health services, counselling, psychotherapy, and wellbeing support; consultancy in workplace wellbeing, resilience, and mental health strategy; delivery of wellbeing programmes for organisations in the creative, media, and sports sectors; provision of supervision, aftercare, and psychological support connected to training programmes and professional practice.
Class 45
Safeguarding services; provision of safeguarding consultancy, advice, and training relating to children, young people, and vulnerable adults; safeguarding and welfare risk assessment services for organisations in the film, television, music, theatre, and sports industries; consultancy on welfare, protection, and wellbeing policies and practices; provision of agency services for safeguarding and wellbeing professionals.
