The mark consists of three pictorial elements, which looks like puzzle pieces, located vertically one under the other. Inside each of them is a letter or a number. Inside the top puzzle is the letter E, inside the middle one is the number 2, inside the bottom one is the letter A. Around the vertically located elements are depicted lines diverging from them in the form of rays.

Registration of this mark shall give no right to the exclusive right to the individual letters E and A and individually to the numeral 2.
