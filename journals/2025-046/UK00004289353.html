Beauty Intelligence

Class 9
Downloadable software and artificial intelligence systems for delivering personalised content, product recommendations, beauty profile analysis, and aesthetic mapping; downloadable software for operating AI-powered discovery engines, virtual assistants, and curated commerce platforms in the field of beauty and personal care; including tools for classification, skin type profiling, tone matching, and interactive consultation.
Class 35
Retail services, including online and offline sale of cosmetics, skincare, haircare, and beauty tools; operation of digital marketplaces and curated commerce platforms in the beauty sector; provision of consumer product information, personalised recommendations, and profile-driven shopping experiences; merchandising and curation services in the field of beauty discovery; marketing and promotional services for beauty brands via digital, social, and offline media; consultancy services relating to AI-powered personalisation and retail models in the beauty industry.
Class 38
Transmission and streaming of beauty-related content, product recommendations, and multimedia experiences; provision of digital communication and messaging services relating to AI-enabled beauty discovery; broadcasting of editorial content and product information in the field of beauty via digital and traditional media; transmission of data for delivering personalised beauty-related experiences; provision of interactive platforms focused on beauty discovery and community engagement.
Class 41
Publishing and distribution of editorial content, beauty product guides, and recommendation tools; provision of online non-downloadable content including articles, videos, audio, and interactive media relating to skincare, cosmetics, haircare, and aesthetic identity; editorial content generated by artificial intelligence and human contributors; development and syndication of multimedia content via websites, applications, virtual assistants, and social platforms; including AI-powered editorial tools and collaborative publishing systems.
Class 42
Development, deployment, and training of artificial intelligence systems for personalised content, beauty product recommendations, aesthetic classifications, and skincare profiling; design and maintenance of AI infrastructure for beauty-based recommendation engines and assistants; provision of AI services for modelling user preferences, skin and hair type affinity, and behavioural data patterns; generation of insights and classifications using large language and vision models; provision of predictive and adaptive tools for beauty discovery and personalised commerce.
Class 44
Provision of personalised beauty consultation services powered by artificial intelligence; advisory services relating to skincare, makeup selection, haircare products, and aesthetic identity; digital tools for assessing individual needs and recommending beauty products; services matching personal characteristics to beauty product profiles for lifestyle and wellbeing.
Class 45
Licensing of artificial intelligence systems, recommendation engines, and data modelling technologies related to beauty and personal care; provision of intellectual property rights for integration of beauty AI into third-party platforms; personal advisory services powered by artificial intelligence relating to beauty routines, product selection, and aesthetic expression; provision of virtual assistants and interactive tools offering beauty-related guidance and support.
