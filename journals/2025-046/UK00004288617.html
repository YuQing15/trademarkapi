holomatrix

Class 9
Downloadable computer software and mobile applications for creating, converting, streaming, and interacting with holographic video content; software using artificial intelligence to transform standard video into holographic or volumetric 3D formats; augmented and virtual reality software for rendering, visualising, and manipulating holographic video and 3D data in real time; software for 3D graphics processing, animation, compression, and visualisation in the fields of entertainment, education, and immersive media experiences.
Class 42
Providing temporary use of non-downloadable software and online platforms for generating, streaming, and interacting with holographic, volumetric, and 3D content using generative artificial intelligence; design and development of computer software for converting and enhancing video, audio, and interactive media into immersive holographic or augmented-reality experiences; Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS) and Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) featuring tools for creation, visualization, compression, and manipulation of 3D and AR/VR data; IT consultancy and design services in the fields of holographic media, 3D animation, and immersive digital environments.
