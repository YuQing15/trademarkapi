Fashion Intelligence

Class 9
Downloadable software and artificial intelligence systems for delivering personalised fashion content, product recommendations, style profile analysis, and wardrobe mapping; downloadable software for operating AI-powered style discovery engines, virtual stylists, and curated commerce platforms in the field of fashion, accessories, and lifestyle products; including tools for silhouette classification, styling algorithms, aesthetic clustering, and interactive fashion consultation.
Class 35
Retail services, including online and offline sale of apparel, footwear, accessories, and fashion-related products; operation of digital marketplaces and curated commerce platforms in the fashion sector; provision of consumer product information, personalised style recommendations, and profile-driven shopping experiences; merchandising and fashion curation services; marketing and promotional services for fashion brands via digital, social, and offline media; consultancy services relating to AI-powered styling, personalisation, and retail models in the fashion industry.
Class 38
Transmission and streaming of fashion-related content, product recommendations, and multimedia experiences; provision of digital communication and messaging services relating to AI-enabled style discovery and fashion consultation; broadcasting of editorial content and fashion product information via digital and traditional media; transmission of data to deliver personalised style experiences and wardrobe guidance; provision of interactive platforms focused on fashion discovery and community engagement.
Class 41
Publishing and distribution of editorial content, fashion guides, and recommendation tools; provision of online non-downloadable content including articles, videos, lookbooks, and interactive media relating to style, fashion identity, and aesthetic interpretation; editorial content generated by artificial intelligence and human stylists; development and syndication of multimedia fashion content via websites, applications, virtual assistants, and social platforms; including AI-powered editorial engines and collaborative publishing systems in the fashion category.
Class 42
Development, deployment, and training of artificial intelligence systems for personalised content, style recommendation engines, aesthetic classification, and wardrobe building; design and maintenance of AI infrastructure for fashion-based personalisation tools and assistants; provision of AI services for modelling individual preferences, body types, colour theory, and behavioural data; generation of fashion-related insights and classifications using large language, vision, and multimodal models; provision of predictive and adaptive tools for fashion discovery and commerce navigation.
Class 44
Provision of personalised fashion consultation and wardrobe services powered by artificial intelligence; advisory services relating to individual style identity, occasion dressing, personal branding, and aesthetic development; digital tools for assessing individual preferences and recommending clothing, accessories, and style edits; services for matching personal characteristics to fashion products for lifestyle, wellbeing, and professional alignment.
Class 45
Licensing of artificial intelligence systems, fashion recommendation engines, and style data modelling technologies; provision of intellectual property rights for integration of fashion AI into third-party platforms; personal advisory services powered by artificial intelligence relating to clothing selection, fashion identity, and personal presentation; provision of virtual assistants and interactive tools offering wardrobe guidance and style expression.
