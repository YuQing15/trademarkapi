The trademark consists of an imprint depicting the wording REGINA TECHNOLOGY in fancy characters and arranged on two lines, the expression REGINA, light green in color and larger in size, being followed underneath by the expression TECHNOLOGY, black in color, said wording taken overall being flanked on the left by a highly stylized letter R, light green in color, with the central eye constituted by strokes that define a hexagonal figure, inscribed within a black hexagonal frame which is interrupted on the left.

The mark contains the colours black and light green.

The color light green appears in the expression REGINA, of a larger size, of the wording REGINA TECHNOLOGY in fancy characters arranged on two lines and in the highly stylized letter R, with the central eye constituted by strokes that define a hexagonal figure, flanked on the right side by the wording REGINA TECHNOLOGY; the color black appears in the expression TECHNOLOGY of the wording REGINA TECHNOLOGY in fancy characters arranged on two lines and in the hexagonal frame which is interrupted on the left, flanked on the right side by the wording REGINA TECHNOLOGY.
