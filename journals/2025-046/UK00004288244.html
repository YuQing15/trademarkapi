Scientific and technological services, and research and design relating thereto, particularly in relation to identifying, locating, monitoring and diagnosing faults in electrical equipment, electrical cables and electricity supply and distribution networks; online provision of web-based applications (non-downloadable) for data collection, analysis and reporting faults in electrical cables and electricity supply and distribution networks; non-downloadable software (software as a service) in relation to data collection, analysis and reporting faults in electrical cables and electricity supply and distribution networks; non-downloadable software (software as a service) in relation to provision of data analytics services in relation to energy supply infrastructure; non-downloadable software (software as a service) in relation to managing and controlling electricity supply and distribution networks; non-downloadable software (software as a service) in relation to data collection, analysis and reporting faults in railway networks; non-downloadable software (software as a service) in relation to monitoring and management of assets in railway network infrastructure; non-downloadable software (software as a service) in relation to monitoring of railway networks; providing on-line non-downloadable software for business decision-making; information, advisory and consultancy services in relation to the aforementioned.
