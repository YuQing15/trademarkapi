Peaknode

Class 35
Business intermediary services for connecting energy project developers with energy demand customers; Business services facilitating business connections and partnership opportunities in relation to energy development projects and energy user site selection via an online platform; all included in Class 35.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) and platform as a service (PaaS) enabling users to assess the viability of energy development sites; providing online non-downloadable software for site scoring, comparison, network-cost assessment and infrastructure evaluation; providing artificial-intelligence-based analysis and ranking of development sites for energy developers and energy-intensive industries; hosting interactive dashboards for infrastructure visualisation, investment scoring and viability assessment; providing temporary use of non-downloadable software for generating, modelling, visualising and exporting electronic reports, ratings, maps and geospatial data; design and development of software for processing infrastructure and energy-related data to support siting, feasibility evaluation and decision-making for energy developments and high-demand electricity users; providing online comparison tools and decision-support systems for evaluating multiple development sites; providing cloud-computing services for geospatial and infrastructure analysis; technical data-analysis services relating to energy systems and infrastructure; all included in Class 42.
