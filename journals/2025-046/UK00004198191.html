Class 9
Wearable electronic payment devices, namely artificial fingernails, smart nails, rings, bracelets, wristbands, spectacles, tags and keyrings incorporating electronic chips for contactless financial transactions; wearable electronic payment devices incorporating NFC or RFID chips; waterproof wearable payment devices; Near Field Communication (NFC) and Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) enabled devices for processing contactless payments; contactless payment devices in the nature of smart nails, smart rings, key fobs and bracelets; encoded smart cards, chips and microprocessors for use in wearable payment technology; mobile applications and downloadable software for managing and processing transactions via wearable devices; downloadable software for linking wearable payment devices to financial accounts; authentication software for secure financial transactions via wearable devices; electronic security devices for encrypting and protecting financial data linked to wearable technology; point-of-sale terminals and readers for use with contactless payment wearables; smart payment devices that enable wireless transactions via integration with banking systems.
Class 14
Jewellery and imitation jewellery incorporating digital and electronic payment technology; smart jewellery, including artificial nails, rings, bracelets, and keyrings, enabled for electronic payments;   customised artificial fingernails made of precious metals or coated with precious metals, for use as decorative jewellery; precious and semi-precious metal accessories adapted for contactless payments.
Class 35
Retail services, including online retail, relating to wearable electronic payment devices, smart jewellery, and smart jewellery accessories; retail and wholesale services relating to artificial fingernails incorporating payment functionality; subscription services for wearable payment technology; business consultancy services in the field of wearable financial technology and digital payments;  wholesale services for wearable payment devices and financial transaction technology via a distributorship; online retail services for wearable payment-enabled accessories.
Class 36
Financial transaction services, namely electronic payment processing via wearable technology; issuing of debit, prepaid, and charge cards linked to wearable payment devices; banking and financial management services relating to wearable payment devices; electronic funds transfer and payment authentication services for wearable payment devices; facilitation of contactless and digital payments via wearable payment devices; payment authentication and transaction verification services; financial account management services provided through wearable-linked applications; mobile payment services linked to wearable technology; provision of secure financial services through digital authentication of payment transactions; cryptocurrency and digital currency transaction services via wearable payment devices.
Class 38
Telecommunication services for facilitating financial transactions via wearable payment devices; electronic transmission of financial data between wearable devices and banking institutions; secure data transfer services for enabling authentication and encryption in wearable financial technology; wireless communication services for linking wearable devices with banking systems; transmission of encrypted financial data via wearable technology.
Class 41
Educational and training services relating to the use and operation of wearable payment devices; conducting workshops, seminars and training sessions on financial technology, wearable payment devices and secure transactions; organisation of exhibitions, conferences and summits in the field of wearable financial technology and digital payments; providing non-downloadable training materials, videos and tutorials on wearable payment devices and digital banking.
Class 42
Software as a Service (SaaS) for managing and processing electronic payments through wearable devices; development, design and maintenance of software for facilitating financial transactions via wearable technology; development of encryption and security software for wearable financial technology; cloud computing services for storing payment credentials on wearable devices; authentication and security services for wearable payment devices, including biometric authentication; design and development of electronic encryption and security solutions for contactless payment devices; technological consultancy relating to wearable financial technology.
Class 45
Identification verification services; licensing of intellectual property; security services for the protection of property and individuals; digital identity verification relating to wearable payment devices; fraud prevention services; access control and user authentication services. .
