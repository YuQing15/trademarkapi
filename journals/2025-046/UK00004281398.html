The mark consists of the word “HiiTcentive” in a modern, bold sans-serif font, with the letters “HiiT” stylised in uppercase and “centive” in lowercase. The logo features a gradient colour scheme using shades of purple and pink, symbolising energy, motivation, and movement. On top of the text, there is an abstract icon combining a heart and a flexed arm — representing fitness, wellness, and reward.
