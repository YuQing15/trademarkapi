The color designation being submitted for registration uses pink (Pantone 226) color and relates to combined marks. The designation consists of one word in Latin letters arranged in a single line centered. The line contains the word "MimiBon". The inscription is made in Latin letters using an original font, where the first letter "M" and the letter "B" are executed in a larger size compared to other letters, which are smaller in size. The letter "o" is executed in the form of a spiral. The verbal part "MimiBon" is executed in pink (Pantone 226) color. The overall background color is white.

The mark contains the colours white and pink (Pantone 226).

The background of the logo is white, the word "MimiBon" is pink (pantone 226).
