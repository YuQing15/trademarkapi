Class 1
Chemical products for commercial use; chemical additives for industrial oils and greases, for lubricants and for fuels (including motor fuels); antifreeze; chemical cleaning agents for commercial use (as far as included in this class); fluids for hydraulic circuits (as far as included in this class); brake fluids (as far as included in this class); power transmission fluids (as far as included in this class); cooling fluids (as far as included in this class); heat transfer fluids (as far as included in this class); mordants for metal; silicones, namely, silicone spray.
Class 2
Paints; varnishes; lacquers; anti-rust preparations for preservation; preparations for the preservation of metal surfaces, lacquer surfaces and plastics surfaces (as far as included in this class); undercoating for vehicles; surface sealing compositions in the nature of coatings (as far as included in this class); anti-ageing products in form of lacquers and paints; solvents for thinning lacquers and paints; anti-corrosive preparations; rust solvents in form of anti-rust preparations for preservation.
Class 3
Cleaning agents; cleaning preparations, namely, cold cleaning preparations; turpentine for degreasing; cleaning agents in form of working fluids having cleaning and rinsing properties for surfaces of metal, glass, lacquer and plastic; laundry preparations, cleaning preparations, polishing preparations, grease removers and abrasives, especially for car maintenance; cleaning fluids, polishing fluids and abrasive fluids for metal treating.
Class 4
Industrial oils and greases, lubricants; motor oils; lubricants being gear oils; lubricating oils being hydraulic oils; anti-seize substances, namely, lubricants and preparations for reducing sliding friction between two surfaces in the form of sliding lacquers; manufactured solid fuels, liquid fuels, fuel gas (including motor spirit); industrial wax; fluids for metal working in form of industrial oils, greases and further petrochemical fluids (as far as included in this class); petroleum (raw or refined).
