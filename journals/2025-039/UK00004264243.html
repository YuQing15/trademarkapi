GRAVEPAGE

Class 35
Providing online directory services featuring information about deceased persons and memorial services; online advertising services for funeral homes, memorial service providers, and grief counselling services; business administration services relating to memorial websites; providing an online marketplace for memorial-related goods and services; online business networking services for families and friends of deceased persons; compilation and management of computerised databases containing memorial information; advertising services, namely, promoting the services of funeral directors and memorial service providers through online directories.
Class 42
Providing a website featuring technology that enables users to create online memorial pages for deceased persons; hosting of websites on computer servers for a global computer network; computer services, namely, creating and maintaining websites for others; software as a service (SAAS) services featuring software for creating memorial websites; platform as a service (PAAS) featuring computer software platforms for creating and sharing memorial content; design and development of computer software for memorial website creation; providing temporary use of online non-downloadable software for creating memorial pages and sharing photographs and information about deceased persons.
