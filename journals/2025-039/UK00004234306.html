Alteam

Class 9
Recorded and downloadable media; downloadable computer software for talent sourcing, candidate matching, fractional-team assembly, project management and human-resource allocation; downloadable mobile applications for collaboration, task tracking and time recording; downloadable artificial-intelligence software for automated product ownership, user-feedback capture, quality-assurance testing and virtual chief technology officer (CTO) guidance; downloadable software development kits (SDKs) and application programming interfaces (APIs) for integrating workforce-management functionality into third-party platforms; downloadable data-analytics software for monitoring performance indicators of distributed or fractional teams; downloadable cloud-based project dashboards; downloadable encryption software for securing remote-team communications; downloadable electronic data files and publications, namely pitch-deck templates, product-strategy worksheets, due-diligence checklists, onboarding guides, e-books, white papers, newsletters and manuals in the fields of product development, fundraising, software engineering, design and remote-team leadership; downloadable audio and video recordings, podcasts and webinars featuring business and technology instruction; downloadable templates for automated reporting and invoicing.
Class 41
Education and training services, namely arranging and conducting workshops, boot camps, hackathons, masterclasses, webinars, coaching sessions and mentoring programmes in the fields of software engineering, product management, user-experience design, entrepreneurship, fundraising, remote-team leadership and fractional CTO practice; provision of non-downloadable online videos, audio recordings and interactive courses for professional development; career coaching for chief technology officers, chief product officers and other executives, including fractional and interim roles; organisation of conferences, exhibitions and networking events for educational purposes in the technology and start-up sectors; publication of electronic books and journals online; provision of online non-downloadable written articles, worksheets and instructional materials; arranging and conducting business-innovation competitions and coding challenges for educational or entertainment purposes; provision of certification programmes and issuing of certificates of completion for training courses.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) featuring software for artificial-intelligence-driven talent matching, fractional-team orchestration, project scoping, resource allocation and human-resource-system integration; platform as a service (PaaS) featuring computer platforms for assembling, managing and analysing performance of fractional or distributed teams; design and development of computer software, mobile applications, cloud-native architectures, prototypes and digital products for others; user-experience and user-interface design services; consultancy in the fields of software engineering, artificial intelligence, machine learning, cloud computing, DevOps, cyber-security and product scalability; provision of technical consultancy and advisory services acting as fractional chief technology officer (CTO) for start-ups and scale-ups; provision of engineering, product-management, design and quality-assurance teams on a fractional or interim basis for technology-development projects; SaaS featuring AI-based virtual CTO guidance and technology-strategy recommendation engines; conducting technological due-diligence, code-review audits and software quality-assurance assessments for third parties; hosting of non-downloadable software that enables experts to create public profiles, schedule paid sessions, conduct video calls and process payments; application service provider (ASP) services featuring project-management and collaboration software; provision of virtual development environments and sandboxes for rapid prototyping; data-encryption and data-security services; computer-software rental, maintenance, updating and integration; monitoring of computer systems by remote access to ensure proper functioning; technological research and advisory services in the field of digital-product innovation.
