Class 9
Scientific apparatus and instruments for measuring, controlling, managing and monitering water flow, flooding and infiltration/inflow in sewer networks and wastewater systems; apparatus for recording, collecting, storing, transmitting and/or disseminating images and data from the monitering, management and control of water flow, flooding and infiltration/inflow in sewer networks and wastewater systems; computer software and applications relating to the aforesaid monitering, and for the management and control of water flow, flooding and infiltration/inflow in sewer networks and wastewater systems; meteorological instruments and forecasting instruments for monitering water flow, flooding and infiltration/inflow in sewer networks and wastewater systems; recorded computer software for optimizing the management of entire sewer networks and wastewater systems.
Class 35
Data and information collection for use in the preparation of business plans for preventing flooding and infiltration/inflow in sewer and wastewater systems.
Class 42
Scientific and technical consultancy regarding monitoring, management and notification in connection with water flow, flooding and infiltration/inflow in sewer networks and wastewater systems; technical advice based on data and information collection for the prevention and management of water flow, flooding and infiltration/inflow in sewer networks and wastewater systems; industrial analysis and research relating to monitoring, management, notification, data and information collection, data and information storage and control of water flow, flooding and infiltration/inflow in sewer and wastewater systems; scientific, engineering and technological services, namely, monitoring, analysis, management, notification, data and information collection, data and information storage and control of water flow, flooding and infiltration/inflow in sewer networks and wastewater systems via computer software and computer software applications; meteorological forecasting for use in monitoring, management and control of water flow, flooding and infiltration/inflow in sewer and wastewater systems; forecasts, including meteorological predictions, regarding the monitoring, management and control of flooding and infiltration/inflow in sewer and wastewater systems; technical and scientific engineering consultancy services relating to water storage facilities and water distribution systems and hydraulic constructions; technical consulting on water quality and water quality assessments, as well as water quality monitoring and modelling; data and information storage for use in the preparation of business plans for preventing flooding and infiltration/inflow in sewer and wastewater systems.
