Class 11
Apparatus and installations for the purification of flue gases and the production of carbon dioxide from flue gases; flue gas scrubbers for the cooling and the purification of flue gases, in particular direct contact coolers; flue gas scrubbers for the removal of acidic components from flue gas, in particular for the removal of sulphur dioxide, hydrogen chloride or hydrogen fluoride; flue gas scrubbers for the removal of solid particles or aerosols; flue gas filters for the removal of solid particles or aerosols; flue gas purification apparatus for the separation of carbon dioxide with the aid of a liquid absorption agent, in particular absorption columns, in particular with intermediate coolers for storing absorption heat; heat exchangers for heating purified flue gas in heat exchange with flue gas or a heat transfer fluid, in particular water steam; apparatus for the desorption of carbon dioxide from an absorption agent, in particular strip columns with water steam generators; vaporizers for the production of water steam; installations for the purification of flue gases, and if required for the production of carbon dioxide, with one or more of the aforementioned apparatuses; installations for the purification of flue gases, in particular for the production of carbon dioxide, in combination with heat pumps for energy regeneration for steam production or district heating production.
Class 42
Engineering services, in particular research and development services, test executions, technical consultancy, technical planning and construction work, conducting technical project planning, security checking of apparatus and installations for the purification of flue gases, and in particular for the production of carbon dioxide from flue gases, and for components of the aforementioned installations; technical analyses, in particular evaluation of process data, of thermal or chemical processes, and technical appraisal of apparatus and installations for the purification of flue gases, and in particular for the production of carbon dioxide from flue gases, and for components of the aforementioned installations.
