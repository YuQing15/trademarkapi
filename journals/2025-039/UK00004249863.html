HALALICIOUS

Class 9
Mobile and web application software for ordering, tracking, and delivering halal home-cooked meals and halal restaurant meals, integrating AI-driven personalised recommendations and geo-location services; software for processing halal home-cooked meal and halal restaurant meal orders, including secure payment processing, order management, and customer feedback; downloadable software for halal home cooks and halal restaurants to manage menus, pricing, bookings, and delivery logistics; augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR) applications offering immersive halal dining experiences and virtual restaurant tours; AI-powered software for automating order suggestions, customer preference analysis, and dietary compliance management in the halal food context; IoT-enabled software solutions for halal food providers to optimize inventory, kitchen operations, and smart device integrations; blockchain-based software for secure transaction tracking and halal certification verification.
Class 35
Providing a global online marketplace for the promotion, sale, and delivery of halal home-cooked meals and halal restaurant meals; advertising and promotional services for halal home cooks, halal restaurants, and meal delivery providers; business management services for halal home-cooked meal providers and halal restaurants, including menu management, pricing, and customer relations; online food ordering services specialising in halal home-cooked meals and halal restaurant meals, including options for delivery, pick-up, and subscription plans; retail services connected with the sale of halal home-cooked meals, prepared halal meals, meal kits, and ingredients from halal restaurants and home cooks; facilitating online reservations and booking systems for consumers to secure tables at halal restaurants or arrange private home dining experiences with halal home cooks; providing food pick-up services allowing customers to collect orders directly from halal restaurants, home cooks, or smart lockers; subscription services for halal home-cooked meal plans and halal restaurant offerings; advertising and marketing services using AI-driven personalised targeting and virtual or immersive dining experience promotions.
Class 39
Transportation, delivery, and last-mile logistics services for halal home-cooked meals and halal restaurant meals, using advanced route optimisation, drone delivery, autonomous vehicles, and robotic fulfillment centers; packaging, handling, and secure delivery services ensuring food quality and safety for halal home-cooked meals and halal restaurant meals; real-time order tracking and customer notification services for halal food deliveries; food pick-up services providing consumers with flexible options to collect halal home-cooked meals and halal restaurant meals directly from providers, smart lockers, or designated collection points; sustainable and eco-friendly delivery methods for halal meals; providing information and support services related to halal food transportation and delivery.
Class 42
Design, development, and maintenance of an advanced online platform for ordering, payment, and delivery of halal home-cooked meals and halal restaurant meals; providing software as a service (SaaS) enabling halal home cooks and halal restaurants to manage menus, customer interactions, dynamic pricing, order processing, and reservations; platform hosting services for a digital marketplace connecting halal home cooks, halal restaurants, and consumers; integration of AI and machine learning for personalized meal recommendations, demand forecasting, and dynamic availability management; development of mobile applications and web-based software with features for table bookings, immersive dining experiences using AR/VR, home dining arrangements, and pick-up services; provision of data analytics services to optimize halal food providers’ operations and customer engagement; API services for seamless integration with payment gateways, delivery logistics, reservation systems, loyalty programs, and emerging technologies such as autonomous deliveries or blockchain transaction verification.
