Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) featuring non-downloadable software for restaurants and hospitality businesses, namely, for creating and hosting online ordering websites and web-based storefronts for customer orders, for receiving, managing, processing, and fulfilling in-store, take-away, and delivery orders for food and beverages through online merchant interfaces, kitchen display systems, and driver dispatch tools, for processing electronic, contactless, and card-present payments, for operating customer loyalty and rewards programs for promotional purposes, for sending marketing and transactional communications to customers via SMS, email, push notifications, or other electronic channels, namely confirmations, status updates, and promotional offers, for dispatching and coordinating delivery drivers, namely route planning, navigation, real-time tracking, and delivery management, for integrating the software with third-party websites and e-commerce platforms, point-of-sale (POS) systems, electronic payment terminals, on-demand courier services, accounting software, Internet-of-Things (IoT) devices, and voice-activated digital assistants, for utilizing artificial intelligence, machine learning, and predictive analytics to optimize menus, analyze customer preferences, forecast demand, and improve operational efficiency, for generating and displaying sales, customer, and operations analytics and reports, and for performing other related restaurant and hospitality management functions; platform as a service (PaaS) featuring application programming interfaces (APIs) and integration tools for enabling all the foregoing services.
