Class 1
Bioengineered chemical preparations for colouring, dyeing and pigmenting; chemicals for the manufacture of pigments; natural, biosynthesized, or synthesized additives for dyeing; natural, biosynthesized or synthesized chemical agents for manufacturing dyestuffs; natural, biosynthesized or synthesized chemicals, additives, agents and microorganisms for the application of dyes that confer functional colour properties onto materials for scientific, research, or industrial purposes; enzymes for use in colouring; cultures of microorganisms for industrial use; cultures of microorganisms for industrial purposes; living microorganisms, other than for medical or veterinary use; microorganisms for the production, depositing and fixing of colour pigments; natural, biosynthesized or synthesized chemical compositions for use in the manufacture of colours; all of the aforementioned goods deriving from living organisms only.
Class 2
Bioengineered colourants, pigments and dyes for general industrial use, namely, use in the industrial manufacture and treatment of goods; dyestuff for textiles; dyestuff for cosmetics; dyestuff for food and beverage; dyestuff for plastic; dyestuff for pharmaceutical applications; non-toxic dyes; biosynthetic dyes; natural dyes; synthetic dyes.
Class 40
Treatment of materials by colouring, dyeing and pigmenting; treatment of materials by colouring, dyeing and pigmenting using bioengineered and natural colourants, pigments and dyes; dyeing services; textile dyeing; services for the dyeing of fabrics, plastics, food and beverage, cosmetics, or pharmaceutical products; biomanufacturing of custom gene sequences and microorganisms for use in scientific, research, industrial and commercial applications for coloring materials; rental or leasing of dyeing machines and apparatus; information, advisory and consultancy services relating to all of the aforesaid.
Class 42
Product research, scientific research, product design and product testing in the field of bioengineered, biosynthesized, natural, or synthesized colourants, pigments and dyes; checking and assessing the environmental impacts of manufacturing colorants and use of colorants in industries; design, development, and maintenance of software and computer database relating to biomanufacturing; design, development, and maintenance of software and computer database relating to coloring, dying, and pigmenting materials; providing information on the origin, quality and environmental impact of colorant production; providing information on the origin, quality and environmental impact of coloring materials.
