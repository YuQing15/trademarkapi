Class 35
The bringing together, for the benefit of others, of a variety of energy, electricity, gas, solar energy, telecommunications services, and internet services, enabling consumers to conveniently compare and purchase those services; Business advisory services in the nature of providing purchase advisory and consulting services to consumers for the purchase of electricity, energy, gas, power, solar energy, telecommunications services and internet services; Billing services in the field of individual and commercial energy consumers; Providing usage and billing information in the nature of utility bill management services, namely, tracking, reporting, analyzing and delivering information in the form of utility bills and utility meter data rate schedules for energy, and telecommunications; Assistance, advisory services, and consultancy with regard to business planning, business analysis, business management, and business organization relating to energy production, energy efficiency, and energy products; Energy conservation management, namely the provision of audit services for tracking and monitoring energy usage, and the provision of information and advice regarding strategies and plans for the purpose of increasing energy efficiency; Preparation of reports on energy consumption of buildings, namely, tracking and monitoring energy consumption for other for cost auditing purposes; Advisory, consultancy and information services relating to all the aforesaid services.
Class 39
Public utility services, namely, the supply of renewable and alternative energy sources; supply services in the nature of electricity distribution, gas supplying, distribution of energy power, and solar energy distribution; Distribution and transmission of electricity; Distribution and supply of energy; Distribution, storage and delivery of electricity, gas, power, and solar energy.
Class 40
Generation of power; Generation of energy; Generation of electricity; Production of energy by power plants.
Class 42
Consultation in the field of research and development of renewable energy technologies; Consultation in the field of research and development of renewable energy technologies related to reducing and offsetting emissions of greenhouse gases and carbon; Consulting services in the field of environmental assessment, compliance and planning, namely, analyzing and evaluating environmental effects of credits, benefits, reductions, offsets and allowances of others resulting from the purchase, generation, or use of energy, or the avoidance of emissions of any gas, chemical or other substance to the air, soil or water; Consulting services in the field of analyzing and evaluating the environmental characteristics, attributes, credits, benefits, reductions, offsets and allowances of others resulting from the purchase, generation, or use of energy, or the avoidance of emissions of any gas; Consultation in the field of design of energy production facilities.
