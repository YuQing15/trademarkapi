Freight International is a trademark used for services related to the transportation and logistics industry. This includes: Freight forwarding services: Arranging the transportation of goods by air, sea, and land. Warehousing services: Storage and handling of goods in warehouses. Customs clearance services: Assisting with the documentation and procedures required for importing and exporting goods. Supply chain management services: Coordinating and optimising the flow of goods from suppliers to customers. Limitations Territorial Limitation: The trademark protection is limited to the United Kingdom. Any use of the trademark outside the UK will not be covered by this registration. Functionality Limitation: The trademark does not cover any functional aspects of the services provided. For example, it does not protect the specific methods or technologies used in freight forwarding or warehousing. Fair Use Limitation: The trademark allows for fair use by others in descriptive contexts, such as in comparative advertising or commentary. This means that competitors can mention "Freight International" in a descriptive manner without infringing on the trademark rights.
