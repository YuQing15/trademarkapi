The mark consists of the wording, in special graphic font, white in color, placed on two lines, "ALFIERI ITALIAN FOOD STORIES". On the first line, wording "ALFIERI" of larger size, having capital letter "A". On the second line, in a central position, wording located within space bounded by two parallel white-colored segments "ITALIAN FOOD STORIES". Wording placed within dark blue oval shape shaded to light blue at upper left and lower right ends and black inner border.

The mark contains the colours white, light blue, dark blue and black.

White: wording "ALFIERI ITALIAN FOOD STORIES" and segments; dark blue: oval shade; light blue: upper left and lower right part of the oval imprint; black: inner border of the oval imprint.

Class 30
Pasta sauce; condiments; chocolate; rice; gnocchi; honey; ice cream; coffee; teas and cocoa and substitutes therefor; bread, pastries, confectionery; flour; preparations made from cereals; fresh pasta; dried pasta.
Class 43
Serving food and drinks; serving food and drink in restaurants and bars.
