Class 9
Downloadable and recorded computer software for validating, checking and pre-processing electrophysiological data, namely EEG, qEEG and related biomedical signal recordings, including automatic verification of file integrity, patient identifiers, electrode placement, signal quality, artifact detection and format compliance, for clinical, research and medico-legal use.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) featuring software for automated quality control and validation of EEG, qEEG and other electrophysiological recordings, namely real-time assessment of data quality, artifact detection, electrode configuration checking, standards compliance monitoring and generation of quality reports, including artificial intelligence based analysis and decision support, and the design and development of software and cloud platforms for the foregoing.
Class 44
Provision of medical data quality assurance services in the field of neurophysiology and neurology, namely validation of EEG and qEEG recordings for conformity with clinical and international standards prior to diagnostic interpretation or neuromodulation treatment planning, and provision of information and advisory services relating to quality standards for neurophysiological recordings.
