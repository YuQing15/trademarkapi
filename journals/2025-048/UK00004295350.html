Trade Mark Journal No.2025/048 28 November 2025

UK00004295350 15 November 2025 (9,35,36,42)

Pare

Class 9
Downloadable mobile applications for comparing financial products and services; downloadable software for financial data analysis; downloadable software for accessing, viewing and managing financial information; downloadable software for receiving personalised financial recommendations; downloadable software for linking to open banking and credit data sources.
Class 35
Comparison services relating to financial products and services; provision of consumer information relating to financial service providers; compilation and systematisation of information relating to financial products; bringing together of financial service providers to enable consumers to compare products and services; advertising and marketing services for financial product providers.
Class 36
Provision of financial information; financial data analysis; comparison of financial products and services; advice and information relating to loans, credit cards, insurance, savings accounts and other financial products; providing information relating to financial products via websites and mobile applications; financial evaluation, assessment and reporting services; providing financial insights based on consumer data; provision of information relating to the terms, pricing and suitability of financial products; brokerage of financial products via online platforms.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) for comparing financial products; platform as a service (PaaS) featuring software for financial data analysis; hosting of applications and websites for financial comparison; providing temporary use of online non-downloadable software for financial insights, recommendations and analytics; development of software using machine learning and artificial intelligence for financial analysis; data aggregation services (non-financial); open banking API integration services.
