DELTA DIVING SERVICES

Class 37
Commercial diving services for underwater cleaning, maintenance, repair, construction and installation; underwater hull cleaning; underwater maintenance and repair of boats, ships and marine vessels; underwater cleaning and maintenance of yacht hulls, propellers, intakes and underwater fittings; replacement of sacrificial anodes; underwater propeller cleaning and polishing; underwater cleaning, maintenance, inspection, repair, construction and installation of marine structures, quays, pilings, jetties, pontoons, slipways, marinas, docks, bridges, seawalls, drainage outlets, moorings and other marine assets; underwater construction, removal and installation of marine fittings, components and structures; underwater search and recovery of lost property, anchors, equipment and vessel components; underwater removal of fouling, debris and obstructions; emergency commercial diving services for maintenance, repair and construction projects; provision of ROV (Remotely Operated Vehicle) services for cleaning, inspection, maintenance, repair and construction support relating to subsea and marine structures.
Class 42
Underwater engineering services; commercial diving services relating to engineering, civil works and infrastructure projects; technical inspection of marine vessels, hulls, propellers, underwater components and marine structures; engineering inspection, surveying and assessment of quays, pilings, jetties, pontoons, bridges, docks, slipways, seawalls, drainage outlets and marine infrastructure; technical condition reporting and risk assessment relating to underwater engineering and marine structures; preparation of technical engineering reports for underwater construction, installation, maintenance and infrastructure projects; testing, evaluation and reporting relating to underwater engineering, marine safety, vessel integrity and underwater structures; provision of ROV (Remotely Operated Vehicle) inspection, surveying, mapping and technical assessment services for marine structures, vessels, subsea infrastructure and underwater engineering projects.
