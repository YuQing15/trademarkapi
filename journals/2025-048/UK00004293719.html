UK00004293719 12 November 2025 (35,39)

VIIA

Class 35
Online marketplace and networking services facilitating introductions between drivers and passengers for peer-to-peer, event-based car sharing; business intermediary services in the field of ride-sharing and event travel; advertising, marketing and promotional services for shared travel listings and event communities; organisation of promotional campaigns and customer referral programmes; provision of consumer information and comparison services relating to peer-to-peer ride-sharing options; dissemination of advertising via mobile applications and the Internet; compilation and systemisation of information into computer databases for business purposes; business data analysis and market research services relating to travel behaviour, mobility and shared transport; consultancy and advisory services relating to marketing and promotion of shared travel platforms; none of the foregoing relating to the operation of professional transport fleets, courier/postal services, or rail/logistics supply-chain management.
Class 39
Provision of an online peer-to-peer platform enabling users to arrange and book shared car journeys for pre-planned, event-based travel; provision of information relating to the planning, scheduling and coordination of such journeys via mobile applications and the Internet; providing journey information, route details and travel updates to users; consultancy and advisory services relating to shared passenger travel; none of the foregoing being taxi or private-hire vehicle services, chauffeur services, public transport services, bus or coach operation, courier or postal delivery, rail freight or warehousing logistics, and not the operation of transportation services.
