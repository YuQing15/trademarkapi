SCANWOW

Class 9
Computer software [downloadable]; downloadable mobile application software; downloadable e-commerce software to allow users to conduct electronic business transactions in online marketplaces; downloadable software for an online marketplace for buyers and sellers of downloadable 3D data; downloadable software enabling users to provide evaluative feedback and ratings of sellers' content, the value and prices of sellers' content, buyers' and sellers' performance, and overall trading experience in connection therewith.
Class 35
Retail services provided online and via software relating to 3D data; online retail services connected with the sale of downloadable 3D data; provision of an online marketplace for buyers and sellers of downloadable 3D data; online trading services, namely, operating on-line marketplaces for sellers and buyers of 3D data; providing evaluative feedback and ratings of sellers' content, the value and prices of sellers' content, buyers' and sellers' performance, and overall trading experience in connection therewith; advertising, marketing and promotional services; business intermediary services relating to the matching of licensors of 3D data with licensees of 3D data; commercial administration of the licensing of 3D data of others; commercial intermediation services; negotiation and conclusion of commercial transactions for third parties; negotiation of business contracts for others; online advertising on a computer network; presentation of 3D data on communication media, for retail purposes; sales promotion for others; advisory, consultancy and information services relating to the aforesaid services.
Class 42
Software as a Service [SaaS]; Platform as a Service [PaaS]; providing computer software [non-downloadable]; providing non-downloadable e-commerce software to allow users to conduct electronic business transactions in online marketplaces; providing non-downloadable software for an online marketplace for buyers and sellers of downloadable 3D data; providing non-downloadable software enabling users to provide evaluative feedback and ratings of sellers' content, the value and prices of sellers' content, buyers' and sellers' performance, and overall trading experience in connection therewith; advisory, consultancy and information services relating to the aforesaid services.
Class 45
Licensing of intellectual property; licensing of 3D data; advisory, consultancy and information services relating to the aforesaid services.
