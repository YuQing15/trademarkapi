The mark consists of the word CIRRUS in stylized letters and to the left of the word CIRRUS, a stylized representation of an airplane, as seen from above, with one front window, the airplane is rotated 45 degrees to the right, superimposed over and bisecting a vertical rectangle with rounded corners, with the airplane tail positioned at the lower, left-hand corner of the rectangle, the airplane nose positioned at the upper, right-hand corner of the rectangle, and the left airplane wing extending over the upper, left corner of the rectangle, and the right airplane wing extending over the lower, right corner of the rectangle.
