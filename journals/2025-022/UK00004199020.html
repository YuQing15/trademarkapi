KIBIOME

Class 42
Scientific and technological services and research and design relating thereto; Scientific, technological, research services for collecting, analysing, monitoring, reporting or tracking data and information in the fields of microbiome, biology and genetics; Scientific research and analysis in the field of human microbiome and immunology; Scientific and technological services, namely, testing, analysis and research in the field of human gut microbiome; Microbiome profiling and analysis services; Laboratory research services relating to the microbiome; Provision of scientific reports and data relating to microbiome testing; development of personalised health recommendations based on microbiome data; preparation of scientific reports for the purpose of health assessment and prevention based on gut microbiota analysis; development of diagnostic methods and tools for use in microbiome analysis; advisory, consultancy, counselling and information services in relation to the aforementioned services, including such services provided over the Internet.
Class 44
Healthcare advisory services; healthcare information services; nutrition and dietetic consultancy; Medical and health consultancy services relating to the microbiome; Medical analysis services for diagnostic and treatment purposes provided by medical laboratories; Pathological testing for diagnostic and treatment purposes; Medical testing services relating to the gut microbiome; health and wellness services relating to microbiome analysis; Healthcare services, including such services relating to nutrition and diet; health management services, including such services relating to nutrition and diet; provision of information and advice in relation to gut health based on microbiome testing; preparation of personalised health and dietary plans based on microbiome data; consultancy in the field of diet and nutrition for the prevention and treatment of health conditions through microbiome modulation; advisory services for improving immune resilience through dietary and microbiome-based interventions; creation of tailored dietary plans for microbiome balance and protection against pathogens, including viruses; advisory, consultancy, counselling and information services relating to the aforementioned services, including such services provided over the Internet.
