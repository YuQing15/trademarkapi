The mark consists of the verbal elements and the figurative element. The figurative element is an image of a knight in armor and chain mail, with a crown above his head, and a sword in his hands, pointing the blade downward (gray and black on a white background). The knight's elbows and sword rest against a large open book. The verbal element "EXCALIBUR HOLDING" is written in Latin characters in green and grey under the book. The verbal element "since" is written in Latin characters to the left of the knight. The verbal element "MMIV" is written in Latin characters to the right of the knight.

Registration of this mark shall give no rights to the exclusive use of the words 'HOLDING, since, MMIV.'

The mark contains the colours grey, black, green and white.
