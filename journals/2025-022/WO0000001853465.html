The mark consists of a single line circle. Within the circle are two horizontal lines beginning at the center point on either side of the circle and traveling less than half of the diameter of the circle from either end, leaving a space between them. There is a vertical line at the end of each horizontal line inside the circle, the center of the vertical line where the horizontal line ends. At the top and bottom of each vertical line are shorter horizontal lines that travel back towards the edge of the circle.
