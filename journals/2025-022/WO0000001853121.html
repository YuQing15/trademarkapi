The mark consists of a geometric design consisting of five fanned out triangular shapes sharing a common center and of gradient shades and angles with the leftmost shape in the darkest shade, pointed at a 145 degree angle, and featuring a rounded bottom with a hole in the center and the rightmost shape being the lightest shade and at a 0 degree angle. To the right of the design is the stylized wording "METAVIA" with "META" in a darker shade than "VIA".
