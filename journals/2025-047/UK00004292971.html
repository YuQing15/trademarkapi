battery chargers for electric vehicles; modular electric vehicle (EV) charging units; rapid and ultra-rapid direct current (DC) chargers for electric vehicles; electric charging stations for vehicles; downloadable and recorded computer software and applications for the management, monitoring, control, optimisation, and remote operation of electric vehicle charging apparatus, installations and networks; human machine interfaces (HMI), touch screens, displays, control panels and RFID readers for use in EV charging infrastructure; data processing and transmission equipment for operating, monitoring and managing electric vehicle charging systems; electronic payment terminals and systems for use with EV charging posts and devices; communication devices and networking equipment for connecting electric vehicle charging stations to cloud-based and remote management systems; identification, authentication and security apparatus for electric vehicle charging installations; surge protection devices, circuit breakers, safety cut-off devices and fuses for use in electric vehicle charging hardware; electrical power distribution boards and control units for EV charging installations; enclosures, housings, cabinets, mounting frames, stands and structural parts and fittings for electric vehicle charging equipment and systems; signal processing apparatus, voltage surge suppressors, residual current devices (RCDs), and monitoring sensors for electric vehicle charging posts and modules; internal electronic circuitry and printed circuit boards (PCBs) for electric vehicle charging apparatus; parts and fittings for all of the aforesaid. .
