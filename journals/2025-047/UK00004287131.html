UK00004287131 31 October 2025 (9,36,42)

The mark consists of a stylised logo featuring the word “TradeEasy” presented in a specific graphic design. The element “Trade” appears in dark blue (Pantone 7463 C) and the element “Easy” appears in light blue (Pantone 299 C). To the left of the wording is a geometric device composed of a stylised dark blue (Pantone 7463 C) hexagonal shape enclosing angular elements that form a partial square motif with a small light blue (Pantone 299 C) square positioned near the upper left area. The mark is shown in colour as part of the design. The colours dark blue (Pantone 7463 C) and light blue (Pantone 299 C) are claimed as features of the mark. The white background is not claimed as part of the mark. No claim is made to the exclusive right to use the words “Trade Easy” separately from the logo as shown.
