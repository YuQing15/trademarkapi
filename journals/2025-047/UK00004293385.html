Class 9

Software as a Service (SaaS); software as a Service (SaaS) featuring software for managing rental agreements, financial transactions and deposit alternatives; provision of non-downloadable software for processing rental applications, tenant referencing and facilitating financial arrangements for tenants and landlords; provision of temporary use of online non-downloadable software for arranging and managing deposit-free rental arrangements; hosting of online platforms for property letting transactions and communications; Platform as a Service (PaaS) for use in the administration of rental agreements and payments; electronic data storage for rental and financial records; design and development of proprietary software for property letting agencies and landlords; providing online tools for identification verification and risk assessment in the letting sector; software development services for financial technology (FinTech) in the field of property rental; and advisory and consultancy services relating to the use of software in property letting; information, advisory and consultancy services relating to all of the aforesaid.
