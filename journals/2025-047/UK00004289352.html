The mark consists of the stylised head of a bull wearing a white chef’s hat with a black outline and band. The bull’s face is primarily blue (Pantone 286 C) with orange stripes (Pantone 1585 C) running vertically from the forehead down to the muzzle and on the inner parts of the horns. The horns are outlined in black with orange inner colouring, and the ears have white inner details. The bull’s nose is white, and the eyes are black and white. Below the bull’s head appears the wording “CHEF-OX” in bold, uppercase, sans-serif white letters, placed inside a black horizontal banner with pointed ends on each side. The mark is presented on a transparent background.
