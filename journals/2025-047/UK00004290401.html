Fitness monitoring devices; personal wearable activity trackers; multifunctional electronic devices featuring software, sensors and transmitters for monitoring, measuring, detecting, transmitting and displaying information and data relating to fitness, exercise, health and well-being, and for displaying the date and time, and also with connectivity to smart phones, computers, tablet computers, portable computers and mobile computer devices;
