ROYAL PAPWORTH

The rights conferred by this registration are personal to the Royal Papworth Hospital NHS Foundation Trust and may not be enforced or acquired by any licensee or assignee/transferee of the said Royal Papworth Hospital NHS Foundation Trust. This limitation shall not restrict the Royal Papworth Hospital NHS Foundation Trust from permitting third parties to use the mark under contract.
