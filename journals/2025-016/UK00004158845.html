VERNISOL

Class 2
Paint; binding agents for paints; traffic marking, namely, pavement marking paint; plastics based surface marking compositions (in the nature of paints); thermoplastic paint materials formulated for spray or extrusion application for use in pavement marking; road marking paint; thermoplastic paint materials for use in marking road surfaces; temperature resistant coatings in the nature of paint for the protection of steel and other surfaces; non-metallic materials for treatment, repair, construction, and marking of roads, paths, runways, bridges, carparks, internal and external flooring, and other like surfaces, namely, synthetic resin paints, resin based coatings for use on pavements, reflective paints and exterior surface protective coatings for marking asphalt and concrete roads; exterior surface protective coatings.
Class 19
Building materials (non-metallic); binding materials made of plastics, and material included in Class 19 for use in the marking of roads, runways and other like surfaces; bituminous coatings (not in the nature of paint) for use in road construction, road repair and jointing materials, the maintenance and repair of cracks and holes in carriageways and other like surfaces; sheets and strips of synthetic material for marking roads and other like surfaces; materials for the surface treatment of roads, pavements, pedestrian areas, landscape design schemes and other like surfaces.
