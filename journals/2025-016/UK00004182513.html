GREENHOUSE

International priority date claimed: 27 March 2025 (United States of America) (99107501)
Class 35
Providing digital marketplace services in the field of employment, namely connecting and matching job openings, candidates, and employer companies via a global computer network; Providing digital databases, directories, portals, and notifications featuring information in the field of job openings, employer company intelligence, candidate profiles and resumes, application and hiring status, employment, and careers, via a global computer network; Human resources consulting services, namely, using specialized software to provide assessment and analytics to prospective employer companies and candidates regarding job openings, recruiting, hiring, employment, and careers.
Class 42
Computer services, namely, providing temporary use of online software for sourcing, searching, tracking, managing, recommending, and analyzing information regarding job openings, employer company intelligence, candidate profiles and resumes, application and hiring status, employment, and careers; Providing temporary use of online software for facilitating and streamlining the communication, evaluation and assessment, application submission and review, and dissemination of information between candidates and employer companies.
