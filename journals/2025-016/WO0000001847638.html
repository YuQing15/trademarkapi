The mark contains the colours orange (Pantone 1585C), green (Pantone 575C), and yellow (Pantone 136C; Pantone 602C and Pantone 607C).

The mark consists of a stylized design of an imaginary piece of fruit loosely resembling a yellow strawberry, with a crown of leaves, the fruit is in orange (Pantone 1585C) and the crown of leaves is in green (Pantone 575C), shades of yellow are used on both the body of the fruit (Pantone 607C and Pantone 136C) and on the leaves (Pantone 602C) to create an impression of mass.
