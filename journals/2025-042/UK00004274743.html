ONDABOOTH

Class 9
Downloadable digital media including videos, music, performances and freestyle recordings; downloadable and recorded media featuring content captured in 360 booths, photo booths, and branded video installations; downloadable software for operating 360 video booths, photo booths, and branded content platforms; computer programs for creating, editing and sharing audiovisual content captured in booth environments; recorded media and downloadable content relating to entertainment, music, live events and brand activations.
Class 35
Advertising, marketing and promotional services; arranging and conducting brand activation events using 360 video booths and photo booth platforms; rental and operation of branded 360 booth systems for promotional campaigns; production of branded digital content and interactive experiences; consultancy relating to experiential marketing, audience engagement, and influencer activations; promotion of artists, brands, and events through live and digital 360 booth experiences.
Class 38
Broadcasting and streaming of audio and video content via the internet; transmission of music performances, freestyles, interviews, and entertainment content recorded in 360 booths or photo booths; live streaming and video-on-demand services featuring booth-based performances; podcasting and digital broadcasting services; transmission of downloadable and interactive content through mobile applications and online platforms linked to 360 booth experiences; creating and hosting virtual communities centred around music, freestyle expression, entertainment, and brand activations.
Class 41
Entertainment services; production, recording, filming and editing of live performances, music freestyles, and creative content captured within 360 booths and photo booth platforms; organisation of entertainment events, competitions, and brand activations featuring interactive booth performances; providing facilities and platforms for artists, influencers, and creators to record and share performances; production of audiovisual and social media content for entertainment and promotional purposes.
Class 45
Online social networking services provided through photo booths, 360 video booths, and branded content capture installations; providing online social introduction and interaction services via mobile applications and websites linked to physical booth experiences; enabling users to connect, collaborate, and build creative profiles through interactive booth recordings and events.
