Figurative mark consisting of the image of a square with rounded corners rotated about 30° framing a stylized blue letter “F”. The letter consists of two rectangles perpendicular to each other and partially overlapping. Along the diagonal of the square starting from the upper left corner, there are three small squares, two of which are white like the background and the third blue. The latter, with the two rectangles, goes to form the letter “F”.

The mark contains the colours white, blue and black.

Blue: the blue areas form the main body of the stylized "F" and consist of three distinct geometric shapes—a large top-right rectangle, a vertical rectangle on the left, and a smaller square toward the lower right; white: the white space sits centrally within the composition and acts as the negative space that shapes the horizontal bar of the "F"; black: the black is used to outline the tilted square that frames the entire design.
