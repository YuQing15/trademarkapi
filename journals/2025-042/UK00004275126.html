VISION-AS-A-SERVICE

International priority date claimed: 11 April 2025 (United States of America) (99133561)
Class 9
Downloadable software for use with onboard hardware namely, cameras, sensors, processors, memory, and storage devices, for capturing, processing, and transmitting live and recorded video, along with metadata including geolocation and vehicle performance data in commercial motor vehicles; downloadable software for facilitating communication between onboard systems and vehicle-based systems to support safety programs, fuel economy optimization, operational analysis, and identification of maintenance anomalies; downloadable applications for configuring, managing, and monitoring systems and integrated third-party applications; computer hardware and pre-installed or recorded software for edge computing and onboard application execution; downloadable software development tools and APIs for use by fleet operators, OEMs, and third-party developers to create compatible applications for vehicle-based computing.
Class 12
Vehicles and apparatus for locomotion by land, namely, commercial motor vehicles equipped with integrated electronic vision systems comprised of cameras, sensors, and digital displays for enhanced situational awareness, driver assistance, and collision avoidance; structural parts for vehicles, namely, externally and internally mounted vision modules designed to replace or supplement traditional mirrors, incorporating digital imaging, edge computing, and wireless data transmission technologies.
Class 38
Telecommunications services, namely, the electronic transmission of data, video, images, and telemetry from commercial motor vehicles to cloud-based processing platforms and remote monitoring systems; transmission of live and recorded video feeds from vehicle-mounted vision systems for real-time driver support, operational oversight, and safety compliance; providing access to a centralized communications network enabling the secure exchange of telematics, sensor data, and visual intelligence between vehicles, fleet operators, and third-party applications.
Class 39
Providing information services in the field of commercial vehicle transportation and fleet logistics, enabled by onboard hardware and software; namely, collecting, transmitting, and analyzing location data, video feeds, and vehicle performance metrics to support routing optimization, driver behavior monitoring, fuel efficiency improvement, and predictive maintenance; providing telematics-based services including vehicle tracking, driver status updates, vehicle health monitoring, and operational visibility for fleet management; providing logistics data analytics services to fleet operators to improve vehicle safety, regulatory compliance, delivery performance, and route efficiency.
Class 42
Providing a cloud-based platform integrating software and hardware components, namely, onboard cameras, sensors, processors, memory, storage devices, and edge computing technologies, for enabling third-party, customer, and proprietary application development and deployment in commercial motor vehicles; providing temporary use of non-downloadable software accessed via the internet for vehicle safety enhancement, fuel economy optimization, operational efficiency, and predictive maintenance support; providing Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS) featuring software development tools, application programming interfaces (APIs), and a development sandbox environment for creating and testing vehicle-based applications; providing a web-based platform functioning as a marketplace for applications developed for commercial vehicle platforms; providing backend infrastructure and software services to support third-party application integration, compliance enforcement, performance monitoring, and revenue sharing; hosting of software applications and digital content developed by fleet operators, and third-party developers; providing backend tools for application promotion, monetization, billing, and reconciliation; providing technical support services relating to application hosting, data access, system configuration, and platform compliance.

VisionWise Systems, Inc.
