NEURICODE

Class 9
Computer software; downloadable and non-downloadable mobile and web applications for personal finance management, savings tracking and investment analysis; software for generating QR codes linked to accessibility information; encrypted data storage software for education and Special Educational Needs (SEN); wearable electronic devices including wristbands incorporating sensory regulation features; smart devices enabling stimming support, contactless payments, NFC/QR login and carer-controlled financial limits; smart watches; wearable smart devices in the nature of wristbands and bracelets with integrated sensory support functions.
Class 10
Medical and therapeutic devices; sensory aids for neurodiverse individuals; wearable devices providing rhythmic pulses for stimming and sensory regulation; devices for monitoring environmental sensory input including light and sound, for use in sensory support and therapy.
Class 14
Watches; jewellery incorporating electronic sensory regulation or financial management features.
Class 36
Financial information services; provision of online financial calculators, Universal Credit estimators and investment portfolio tracking tools; financial advisory and planning services provided via a web-based platform; Provision of financial account management, budgeting, and payment-authorisation services for vulnerable or assisted users under carer or guardian supervision; financial management services provided via secure digital platforms enabling carers to monitor, approve, and control financial transactions of dependent individuals; provision of information, consultancy, and advisory services relating thereto.
Class 41
Education and training services; provision of online educational resources in the field of personal finance, financial literacy and budgeting; provision of online forums, discussion boards and training materials relating to Special Educational Needs (SEN); publishing of accessible educational content for neurodiverse learners.
Class 42
Software as a Service (SaaS); Platform as a Service (PaaS); provision of online non-downloadable software for finance management, accessibility settings and SEN support; secure hosting of accessibility profiles and encrypted SEN documents; design and development of customised neurodiverse-friendly software interfaces; data encryption, secure communications and identity verification services.
Class 45
Providing information on legal rights relating to Special Educational Needs (SEN); advocacy and campaigning services in the field of neurodiversity and accessibility; online social networking services for neurodiverse individuals; licensing of computer software in the field of accessibility and finance.
