NEOTECH

Class 15
Straps, harnesses, slings and supports specially adapted for musical instruments; shoulder straps for musical instruments; neck straps for musical instruments; instrument harnesses and support systems for woodwind and brass instruments, namely saxophones, clarinets, oboes, bassoons, flutes, piccolos, recorders, English horns, bass clarinets, contrabass clarinets, baritone horns, trumpets, cornets, trombones, French horns, euphoniums, tubas, sousaphone and related wind instruments; padded carrying straps for musical instruments; instrument harnesses with quick-disconnect fittings for use with handheld or wearable wind instruments; playing supports, cradles and shoulder/neck rests adapted for wind instruments; bags, cases and pouches adapted for musical instruments and their accessories; accessories for carrying and supporting musical instruments, namely adapters, connectors, and attachment fittings; replacement parts and fittings for all the aforesaid goods.
