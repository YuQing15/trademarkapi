SPARK CARE

Class 9
Sensors and monitoring apparatus; computer software and hardware for use with sensors and monitoring apparatus; sensors and monitoring apparatus for use in health and social care environments; sensors for detecting and monitoring presence, occupancy, falls, movements, vital signs and wellbeing indicators; computer software and hardware modules enabling Internet of Things connectivity within care settings; downloadable software and mobile applications for monitoring safety and wellbeing, generating alerts, dashboards, analytics and reports, excluding those relating to internet connectivity and internet solutions; handheld computing devices for carers and nurses; downloadable software for monitoring safety and wellbeing; downloadable software for detecting and monitoring falls, movements, vital signs and wellbeing indicators; computer software and hardware for controlling lighting equipment and apparatus; computer software and hardware to enable nurses and healthcare staff to communicate with one another and with patients, in a care setting; safety alert systems and alarms; parts and fittings for the aforesaid goods.
Class 41
Training services relating to the use and implementation of computer hardware, computer software and technology in health and social care settings; training relating to falls prevention and technology-enabled care; sales and marketing training; information, advisory and consultancy services in relation to all of the aforementioned services.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) and platform as a service (PaaS) featuring software for monitoring safety and wellbeing in health and social care environments, generating alerts, dashboards, analytics and reports; scientific and technological services in the field of health and social care, excluding those relating to internet connectivity and internet solutions; software consultancy relating to health and social care technologies, excluding those relating to internet connectivity and internet solutions; design and integration of Internet of Things systems within care environments; technical support services for social care technology solutions, excluding those relating to internet connectivity and internet solutions; digital transformation consultancy and advisory services; integration of software with nurse call systems and digital care planning platforms; providing temporary use of non-downloadable software for the foregoing; information, advisory and consultancy services in relation to all of the aforementioned services.
Class 44
Consultancy and advisory services in the field of health and social care; consultancy and advisory services to care homes; health risk assessment services in relation to falls and wellbeing risks derived from sensor data; information, advisory and consultancy services in relation to all of the aforementioned services.
