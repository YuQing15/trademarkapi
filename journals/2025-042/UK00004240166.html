Build Armour

Class 17
Modular laminated plastic surface protection materials; modular plastic films and sheets for temporary surface protection in residential, commercial, trade, DIY, or building environments; modular cushioning foams and non-metallic edge protectors; modular extruded plastic sheeting for impact resistance or insulation; all for use on stairs, floors, worktops, handrails, furniture, radiators, appliances, internal and external doors, window sills, windows, pipes and pipe protrusions, driveways, patios, paths, pavements, concrete, shingle, grass, turf, and artificial grass.
Class 19
Modular non-metallic protective sheeting for use on all surfaces in residential, commercial, trade, DIY, or building environments, including but not limited to stairs, floors, worktops, handrails, furniture, radiators, appliances, internal and external doors, window sills, windows, pipes and pipe protrusions, hard surfaces such as driveways, patios, paths, pavements, concrete, shingle, grass, turf, and artificial grass and surfaces; modular surface protection panels and coverings for internal and external applications.
Class 20
Modular storage units for tools and building equipment; modular stackable containers; modular docking plates and mounting systems for vehicles, including those made of metal or plastic; modular van-mounted storage systems; modular tool holders and storage fixtures; modular portable storage containers and vehicle-mounted storage solutions for use in residential, commercial, trade, DIY, or building environments, including protection or storage related to stairs, floors, worktops, handrails, furniture, radiators, appliances, internal and external doors, window sills, windows, pipes and pipe protrusions, driveways, patios, paths, pavements, concrete, shingle, grass, turf, and artificial grass.
