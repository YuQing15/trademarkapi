Class 1
Chemical products for industrial, scientific, and photographic purposes, as well as agricultural, horticultural and forestry purposes, in particular chemical preparations containing carbon black for use in the rubber industry, the printing industry, the plastics industry, the paint, lacquer and coatings industry as well as the sealants and adhesives industry; fillers for rubber; carbon black for industrial or agricultural purposes; carbon; carbon black for industrial purposes, in particular for use as a reinforcing filler for natural and synthetic rubber and plastics, as a coloring agent, a rheology modifier, a protective agent against ultraviolet radiation; fillers for synthetic resins; an agent for absorbing infrared light, an antistatic agent, an electrically conductive agent, a dispersant, an antioxidant and/or a reducing agent; carbon black for use in the manufacture of industrial products such as tires, plastic articles, fibres, films and coatings, textiles, papers, paints, coatings, varnishes, lacquers, printing inks, toners, printing media, sealants, adhesives, electrode materials, catalysts and technical rubber articles and for use in metallurgical processes; technical gases being gases and gas mixtures for use in industry, research and science; gaseous and liquid hydrocarbons, not for use as fuel; gases and oils for industrial purposes, in particular gases and oils from the processing of waste products such as used tires, for example pyrolysis gas and pyrolysis oil being preparations for use in producing carbon black; unprocessed synthetic resins, unprocessed plastics, in particular resins and plastics containing carbon black; adhesives for industrial purposes, in particular adhesives containing carbon black for industrial purposes; all of the afore mentioned goods preferably at least partly produced from renewable and/or biogenic sources.
Class 2
Paints; varnishes; lacquers; coatings [paints or lacquers]; colorants; dyes; blacks [dyes, or paints]; pigments; carbon black as pigment, in particular for use in printer inks, toners, for contactless printing, in sealants, adhesives, paints, coatings, varnishes, lacquers, cement, concrete, rubber as well as plastics; carbon black as colorant; all of the afore mentioned goods preferably at least partly produced from renewable and/or biogenic sources.
Class 42
Scientific and technological services and related research and design services as well as industrial analysis and research services, in particular chemical analysis, chemical research, chemical engineering services, engineering services, industrial design services, physical research, scientific laboratory services, scientific research, engineering research, technical project studies, engineering project studies; material testing; environmental protection research; preparation of technical reports; development of industrial processes; development of new products; development of new technologies for third parties; development of measurement and testing methods.
