L.I.F.E. Programme

Mark 2

The L.I.F.E. Programme

Class 41
Education, training, coaching, mentoring and tutoring services; educational consultancy; provision of academic, vocational and personal development programmes; provision of education and skills-based training for children, young people and adults in a range of settings including schools, residential homes, foster care, supported accommodation and semi-independence services; provision of post-16 education and boarding school services; employability training; delivery of life skills training including personal care, domestic skills, cooking, hygiene, safety, financial literacy, travel training, and time management; digital, IT and online training; training in entrepreneurship, business, leadership, and career development; coaching in emotional intelligence, wellbeing, resilience, communication and behavioural development; education and support for individuals with learning difficulties, neurodevelopmental conditions, mental health needs and other vulnerabilities; training of staff, coaches, carers, mentors and educators; organisation and delivery of workshops, seminars, conferences, group training sessions and community learning events; provision of educational resources and facilities; organising and facilitating educational, cultural, recreational and enrichment activities, including community events, outings, and social clubs; organisation of competitions, sporting events and training camps; sports and fitness instruction; personal coaching services; yoga, fitness and meditation training; online education services and non-downloadable electronic publications; publishing and editing of educational content; provision of online and telephone information services relating to the aforesaid. .
Class 44
Provision of wellbeing services; coaching and mentoring focused on emotional wellbeing, mental resilience and personal growth; trauma-informed support services; psychological care, counselling, consultation and treatment services; psychological assessment, profiling, testing and examination services; preparation of psychological reports and profiles; personality testing for psychological purposes; individual and group psychology services; psychological counselling for staff and in the field of sports; wellbeing support for vulnerable individuals, including those with emotional, behavioural, neurodevelopmental and mental health needs; delivery of structured one-to-one and group coaching and mentoring programmes in educational, residential, supported living, fostering and community settings; services relating to stress management, emotional regulation, confidence building, relationship development, goal setting and behavioural change; therapeutic and non-therapeutic wellbeing interventions delivered by trained professionals with clinical oversight where appropriate; advisory and consultancy services relating to emotional health, trauma recovery, psychology and trauma-informed practice; provision of information, resources and support materials in the field of psychological and emotional wellbeing; development and implementation of programmes to support the psychological, social and emotional needs of vulnerable or at-risk individuals.
