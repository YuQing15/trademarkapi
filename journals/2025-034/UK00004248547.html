ValuCert

Class 9
Downloadable and recorded computer software; downloadable mobile applications; downloadable web applications; downloadable software for creating, managing, storing, and sharing jewellery and watch valuation reports; downloadable software for data analysis, image processing, and optical character recognition; downloadable software for integration with point-of-sale and stock control systems; downloadable electronic reports and certificates; downloadable databases; downloadable software for secure document storage and retrieval; downloadable software for providing market value updates for jewellery, watches, and other luxury goods; downloadable software for creating and verifying digital certificates of authenticity.
Class 36
Valuation services; appraisal of jewellery, watches, gemstones, and luxury goods; financial valuation reporting; preparation of valuation reports for insurance, probate, family division, sale between parties, open market value, loan security, and capital gains purposes; providing financial information in relation to market values of jewellery, watches, gemstones, and other luxury goods; providing information and consultancy in the field of valuations; provision of valuation data to insurers, law enforcement, and related parties; financial risk assessment relating to luxury goods.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) featuring software for creating, managing, storing, and sharing jewellery and watch valuation reports; providing temporary use of online non-downloadable software for creating, managing, storing, and sharing valuation reports; providing temporary use of online non-downloadable software for data analysis, image processing, and optical character recognition; platform as a service (PaaS) for integration with point-of-sale and stock control systems; providing temporary use of online non-downloadable software for secure document storage and retrieval; providing temporary use of online non-downloadable software for providing market value updates for jewellery, watches, and other luxury goods; design, development, and maintenance of computer software; hosting platforms on the internet; providing non-downloadable databases of jewellery and watch information, valuations, and related data.
