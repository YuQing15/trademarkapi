RECASTLE

Class 9
Downloadable software for semantic compression, validation, and regeneration of digital content; downloadable toolkits and software development kits (SDKs) for creating, reading, writing, converting and validating proprietary computer file formats [including the .rclx format and its variants]; downloadable software libraries for data encoding, decoding, and validation of structured digital content; artificial intelligence software for processing, scoring, and reconstructing content using semantic anchors; middleware software for runtime verification, behaviour-based gating, and regeneration control; software for distributed certification and cryptographic validation within AI and machine learning systems; software utilities for managing data quality metrics, certification scores, and content regeneration parameters; downloadable applications for file compression testing and certification enforcement; software for enforcing certification protocols across IoT, SaaS, LLM, and embedded systems; data wrappers and scoring modules for cognitive model integration and semantic traceability.
Class 35
Business consultancy services relating to artificial intelligence, data compression, and digital governance; business consultancy relating to the implementation of certification systems for digital products and services; strategic consultancy in the fields of trust-marking, semantic scoring, and autonomous decision systems; business management services relating to trust frameworks and compliance monitoring; consultancy services for lifecycle oversight of certified digital formats and compression protocols; commercial assistance relating to the licensing and promotion of proprietary software and digital file formats; business project management in support of regulatory adoption, ethical audits, and digital certification enforcement; promotion of interoperable standards and certification systems for AI, LLMs, IoT, and semantic infrastructure; advisory services relating to compliance and governance frameworks in the digital sector; development of regulatory-aligned business strategies for digital trust, regeneration control, and cognitive scoring implementation.
Class 41
Educational services relating to artificial intelligence, data compression, and digital governance; provision of training in the use and implementation of semantic file formats and trust-based certification systems; publication of white papers, articles, and technical manuals in the fields of AI scoring, regeneration fidelity, and autonomous compliance architectures; delivery of online seminars, tutorials, and courses on the RECASTLE protocol, semantic compression, and digital certification methods; provision of digital content and audiovisual media in the field of certified computer file systems [including the .rclx and .rclx3 formats] for educational purposes; creation and distribution of multimedia content for public education on AI trust frameworks, including animated series and explainer videos; dissemination of learning materials via websites, SaaS platforms, and digital publishing systems related to ethical AI and file integrity standards.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) featuring tools for semantic compression, file regeneration, and trust-scored data processing; cloud-based platforms for the encoding, decoding, and certification of proprietary computer file formats [including the .rclx format and its variants]; provision of non-downloadable software for enforcing digital trust frameworks and regeneration controls; development and maintenance of cryptographic validation protocols for use in artificial intelligence and machine learning environments; hosting of digital tools for anchor-based scoring, fidelity verification, and behavioral compliance gating; provision of certification services for AI output and content regeneration through trust-bound semantic validation systems; technical support for implementing digital compliance engines across autonomous platforms, IoT, and web environments; design and maintenance of algorithms for content scoring, file format structure, and execution control in software systems; provision of software infrastructure for certification, content traceability, and data authentication in semantically compressed file systems; development of interactive toolkits and games to teach semantic regeneration and cognitive scoring principles.
