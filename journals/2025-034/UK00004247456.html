Soldering and welding chemicals; chemical preparations for welding; separating agents to prevent welding spatter; cooling preparations for welding.
Class 7
Installations, machines, apparatus and equipment for welding and cutting, in particular electric arc welding machines, plasma welding machines, laser welding machines and autogenous welding and cutting machines, equipment and burners; parts, wearing parts and accessories for the aforesaid goods, including electrode holders, gas nozzles, current contact tips, welding gas burner connections, protective gas connecting hoses, nozzle sets, gas diffusers, gas lenses, clamping sleeves, clamping sleeve housings, central connectors for protective gas connecting hoses with plugs and bushings and vortex rings or turbulence rings, back caps, welding electrodes, in particular tungsten and carbon electrodes, plasma electrodes, plasma nozzles, spirals and/or bores for soldering and welding wire; wire feeding systems for welding and laser applications; apparatus for cutting soldering and welding wire; suction apparatus for electric or gas welding and cutting; cleaning equipment and apparatus for welding torches and blow torches, in particular blower outlets and spraying units for automated welding; tool changing systems for automated welding and cutting, in particular torch neck changing systems; mountings for burners, adjustment devices for robot welding and welding burners; mechanical positioning aids for programming welding robots; gas economising equipment for welding and cutting equipment; gouging blowpipes and parts therefor for gouging, seam preparation and preparation of metals; abrading instruments (mechanical), namely machines for grinding welding electrodes; robot mountings; condensate separators.
Class 9
Electric and electronic controls and regulators for installations, in particular microprocessor controllers; optical laser beam systems and optical sensor systems for welding and cutting technology, in particular optical seam tracking sensors; optical positioning aids for programming welding robots; power sources for welding and cutting installations; computer software and programs therefor; gloves for protection against accidents, in particular gloves for welders; welding shields, in particular welding helmets.
Class 11
Cooling apparatus for welding.
