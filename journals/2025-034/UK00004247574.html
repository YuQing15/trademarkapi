Perfect Piste

Class 39
Travel arrangement services; transportation booking and reservation services for travellers; provision of travel information and travel planning services, including via the Internet and mobile applications; arranging and coordinating transportation for ski resorts, mountain destinations, and winter sports events; organization of ski transfers and shuttle services; transportation of passengers by car, bus, coach, train, cable car, and aerial lift; arrangement of guided tours, excursions, and adventure trips in ski and mountain regions; providing information relating to transportation conditions, routes, weather, and accessibility for ski areas and mountain destinations; rental of vehicles for travel to and within ski and mountain regions; baggage transportation and handling services; travel guide and escort services; arranging transport for sports equipment, including skis and snowboards.
Class 42
Provision of temporary use of online non-downloadable software and mobile applications for planning, booking, and managing ski trips, mountain holidays, and travel arrangements; provision of temporary use of online non-downloadable software for providing information on ski conditions, snow forecasts, weather, and mountain safety; hosting of platforms for booking accommodation, transportation, and dining in ski and mountain destinations; design, development, and maintenance of software and mobile applications relating to travel, transportation, accommodation, and snow sports; creation and hosting of interactive maps for ski resorts and mountain areas; provision of temporary use of non-downloadable software for GPS navigation and route planning for ski resorts and mountain travel; data aggregation and analysis services relating to ski conditions, travel logistics, and hospitality trends; development of computer software enabling communication and coordination between travellers, guides, and service providers; provision of online platforms featuring virtual tours of ski resorts, mountain lodges, and related facilities; consultancy and advisory services relating to the design and development of computer software and web platforms in the travel and hospitality sectors. Application service provider (ASP) services relating to artificial intelligence and artificial intelligence applications; providing temporary use of non-downloadable software relating to artificial intelligence and artificial intelligence applications; providing temporary use of non-downloadable software to enable the viewing of website content via personal and handheld computers and mobile devices relating to artificial intelligence and artificial intelligence applications; provision of non-downloadable software applications through a website, virtual community, social networking site, and shared use of text, images, and audio-visual content relating to artificial intelligence and artificial intelligence applications; creation, design, and maintenance of the aforesaid; advice, consultancy, and information services relating to artificial intelligence, artificial intelligence applications, machine learning, and machine learning applications; advisory, consultancy, and information services relating to all of the aforesaid.
Class 43
Provision of temporary accommodation services; reservation and booking of accommodation for travellers, including hotels, chalets, apartments, hostels, lodges, cabins, and mountain huts; provision of information and advice relating to accommodation, hospitality, and lodging in ski and mountain destinations; provision of food and drink; restaurant, café, bar, and catering services; arranging dining reservations; booking of tables at restaurants and après-ski venues; provision of information about dining, catering, and hospitality services; operation of online platforms for booking accommodation, dining, and hospitality experiences; provision of holiday camp services and mountain lodge retreats; consultancy and advisory services in relation to the provision of temporary accommodation and food services.
