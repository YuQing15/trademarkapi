Spectrometers for measuring lubricating and hydraulic fluid health; electronic fluid condition monitoring systems comprising electronic sensors, fluorescence spectroscopy sensors and monitors for real-time analysis and diagnostics of lubricating oils and other machinery fluids; electronic system for installation in industrial equipment, engines, turbines, and other machinery for monitoring, analysis, diagnostics, and for condition-based and predictive-based maintenance of lubricating oils and other machinery fluids; electronic sensors for analyzing the properties of fluids and for detecting and quantifying debris particles in fluids; integrated electronic signal processing units for interpreting fluorescence and electrical data and providing digital or analog outputs; predictive maintenance devices for assessing fluid quality and other properties in engines, turbines, and industrial machinery for use in a wide range of industrial and commercial sectors, including marine, aerospace, automotive, oil and gas, and energy industries; software for monitoring, analyzing and collecting data from a fluid health, properties and contamination detection system.
