Collective Mark

Class 45
Advocacy services promoting accessibility and inclusivity in public policy, legislation, and organisational practices; consultancy on accessibility standards, laws, and compliance; representation of member interests before governmental, regulatory, and professional bodies; establishing, monitoring, and enforcing professional standards in the field of accessibility and inclusive design; mediation and dispute resolution services related to accessibility issues; legal consultancy regarding accessibility compliance and inclusive practices; providing ethical guidance and professional advice to organisations and professionals in the field of accessibility..

Inst. Of Accessibility Limited

The Regulations provisionally accepted by the Registrar for governing the use of this Collective trade mark are open to inspection at the Trade Marks Registry, from where copies may be obtained. If no opposition arises the Registrar will approve the Regulations and the mark will proceed to registration.
