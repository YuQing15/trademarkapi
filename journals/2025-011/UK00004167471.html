The Harvecaster is a scraper system designed to remove the bottom layer of worm castings from a composting worm composting bin. The central icon showcases a blade or scraper, representing the tool’s primary function. The inclusion of soil and compost imagery highlights the harvesting process of worm castings Dark Brown, Grey and Green, creating a balance between industrial strength and natural sustainability
