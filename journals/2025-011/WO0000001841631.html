Date of designation in the UK: 21 November 2024

The mark contains the colours blue, light blue and white.

The mark consists of the stylized word "HyBEKSTER" where y is in lowercase letters, H and BEKSTER is in uppercase letters. A thin white horizontal line runs through the middle of the word "HyBEKSTER", with the portion of the word above the horizontal line in blue and the portion of the word below the horizontal line in light blue.
