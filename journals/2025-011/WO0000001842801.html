Providing online non-downloadable software for the creation, editing, reviewing and sharing of environmental risk assessment and regulatory compliance reports; providing online non-downloadable software for analyzing data in connection with environmental laws and regulations and the generation of environmental compliance reports; providing online non-downloadable software for use by professionals in the environmental risk information industry for the creation, editing, reviewing and sharing of property and parcel-specific reports and data; software as a service (SaaS) services featuring computer software for uploading, downloading, searching and accessing real property reports, maps, photographs, and data; software as a service (SaaS) featuring software for use in project planning, project management, financial management, environmental risk assessment, engineering, asset and data management in the fields of environmental science and real property remediation; providing online non-downloadable software for the drafting, preparing, reviewing and sharing of technical reports and environmental surveys; providing online non-downloadable software for use by professionals in the environmental risk industry for collaboration among users; providing online non-downloadable software for use by professionals in the environmental risk industry for collaboration, project management, time management, calendaring, information management, organization, and workflows; providing online non-downloadable software for the drafting and preparing of environmental inspection reports for use in performing phase I and phase II environmental risk assessments on land assets; software as a service (SaaS) services featuring software for measuring distances or areas between geographical points on a topographical map; software as a service (SaaS) services featuring software for vapor screening for the identification of vapor encroachment conditions; software as a service (SaaS) services for the creation of templates for use in drafting and preparing environmental and engineering reports, letters and work proposals in the fields of environmental science and real property remediation;
