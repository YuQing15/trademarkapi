" The mark consists of the word "WASBY" in a stylized font, accompanied by a faith-based design featuring a dove, a cross and rays of sunshine.

The applicant does not claim exclusive rights over the individual elements of the mark, including the dove, cross and rays of sunshine, except when used in the specific arrangement and stylization represented in the WASBY trademark. The applicant also does not claim exclusive rights over the phrase "Walk in Faith, Shine With Purpose" outside of its specific branding context".
