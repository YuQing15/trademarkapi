Combined (figurative) mark consisting of a stylized graphic image and a word element. The graphic part represents the silhouette of darts feathers, with one of the feathers fragmented into small square elements resembling data. This graphic design forms a stylized letter "D". The word element "dartitai" is written in Latin letters. The colors in the mark are in a dark blue-green range.

The mark contains the colour dark green.

The logotype and the text.
