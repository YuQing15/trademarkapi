Downloadable software that enables musical artists and entertainers to engage with fans through product giveaways, contests, and promotions; optical glasses, sunglasses, eye glass cases.

Advertising services, namely, providing exclusive access to sweepstakes, merchandise, live music events, and vinyl records; arranging and conducting incentive reward programs to promote musicians and entertainers and their music and musical services; providing customer loyalty, appreciation, incentive awards, rewards, and discount programs, for commercial, promotional and/or advertising purposes; advertising, promotion, and marketing services in the nature of promotional contests for products and services in the music and entertainment industry; promoting the goods and services of others through product giveaways, promotions, sweepstakes, and contests; organizing and arranging sweepstakes featuring games and customer competitions for advertising, marketing, marketing research and business purposes; administration of a customer loyalty program which provides customers with access to exclusive items; customer loyalty services, namely, providing a loyalty program promoting the sale of products and services of others by providing members exclusive advantages and benefits that incorporates gift incentives from participating businesses and musical artists; retail store services in the field of music, apparel, vinyl records, blankets, mats, games, and other merchandise; retail store services in the field of entertainment featuring musical works and audiovisual works; providing online retail store services featuring music recordings, comedy recordings, spoken word recordings, video recordings, and music related products; subscription-based and online retail store services featuring downloadable prerecorded music and video; online ordering services featuring prerecorded music and video; providing online retail store services featuring clothing, footwear, headwear, optical glasses, sunglasses, eye glass cases, backpacks, bags, and water bottles.
