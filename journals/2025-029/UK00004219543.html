Business management services, namely, providing customer relationship management services and business consulting and advisory services relating to the development, implementation, and administration of contests; promoting the goods and services of others through the development, implementation, and organizing of contests, incentive award programs, and competitions in the fields of business competitions, educational competitions, video game competitions, civic competitions, coding competitions, hackathons, autonomous technology, robotics, artificial intelligence, and science competitions; business networking; online professional networking services; matching small businesses with professionals in the fields of technology and innovation; business project management services; marketing services; business education and workforce development through experiential learning programs and challenge-based training initiatives; franchise licensing services for universities, associations, and organizations to operate branded innovation challenges.
