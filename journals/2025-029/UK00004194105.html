Trade Mark Journal No.2025/029 18 July 2025

UK00004194105 24 April 2025 (35,38,41)

PLUME

Class 35
Provision of digital advertising space within a mobile and web-based social networking platform; promotion of third-party goods, services, events and campaigns through targeted advertising, branded content, and in-app sponsorships; facilitating collaborations between businesses, creators and users based on shared values, lifestyle traits and interests; enabling community-aligned brand partnerships and audience-specific marketing through user-generated content streams, recommendations and personalised engagement features.
Class 38
Provision of online forums and virtual communities for users to create profiles, connect with others, and engage in social discovery based on shared values, interests, and personal traits; transmission of user-generated content including text, images, audio, and video via the internet; enabling online messaging, commenting, and content sharing within a social networking platform; provision of access to a platform for personal expression, interest-based matching, and community engagement.
Class 41
Provision of a digital platform, accessible via mobile and web applications, for creative expression, community sharing and the dissemination of non-downloadable user-generated multimedia content;  providing online content including journals, blogs, creative projects and audio-visual material in the fields of lifestyle, identity, and culture; organising and facilitating virtual workshops, showcases, and community-led events that support personal storytelling, collaboration and expressive engagement.
