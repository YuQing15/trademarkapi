Class 9
Recorded computer application software used for identifying, classifying searching and retrieval of information according to consumer preferences, including flavor and taste profiles; recorded computer software to enable the digital visualization of flavor profiles; recorded computer software for use as an application programming interface (API); downloadable computer software for use as an application programming interface (API); pre-recorded media in the nature of downloadable video; software used for analyzing and reviewing market research, information, reports, news and analysis; downloadable computer application software for delivering personalized recommendations via an application programming interface (API); Downloadable mobile applications software for selecting and identifying flavor and taste profiles; downloadable interactive databases in the field of consumer preferences, including flavour and taste profiles; recorded computer software for searching and retrieval of data.
Class 35
Promoting the goods and services of others by providing a website featuring coupons, rebates, price-comparison information, links to the retail websites of others, and discount information; advertising services; advertising services provided via the Internet; electronic business data processing services; provision of business information; retail store services in relation to alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages; providing personalized recommendations for use in the food and beverage industry to consumers for commercial purposes; provision of information relating to the food and beverage industry, specifically information about consumer flavor profiles for commercial purposes, for use on a customer engagement services platform.
Class 43
Provision of information relating to food products, flavor profiles relating to consumable food and beverage products, taste profiles relating to consumable food and beverage products, beverages and alcoholic beverages, via an interactive website; providing information in the nature of digital visualization of flavor profiles relating to consumable food and beverage products, analytics relating to consumable food and beverage products, recipes, information relating to consumable food and beverage products and recommendations according to consumer preferences, consumer flavor profiles and consumer taste profiles relating to consumable food and beverage products, all for use in the food and beverage industry and all provided via a global communications network; providing personalized recommendations for menu planning for use in the food and beverage industry; providing information relating to taste profiles for consumable food products and beverages, flavor profiles for consumable food products and beverages, food products, beverages and alcoholic beverages, via an interactive website; providing an interactive questionnaire regarding taste and flavor preferences for food products, beverages and alcoholic beverages, via an interactive website; providing information in the field of food products, beverages and alcoholic beverages, via an interactive website.
