RENAISSANCE DEFENCE

Class 35
Business consultancy services in the field of defense, security, and government procurement; Business administration and project management services for the coordination and oversight of multi-stakeholder government programs; Strategic sourcing services and vendor selection for government contracts; Contract management services, namely, holding and administering prime contracts and sub-awards on behalf of government clients; Advisory services relating to the structuring, implementation, and delivery of complex defense programs; Supply chain management and analysis services for defense and national security sectors; Providing business information and data analytics relating to procurement and vendor performance in the public sector; Business risk assessment and efficiency optimisation services in relation to government contracting and defense supply chains; Providing an online marketplace for government entities and contractors to evaluate, compare, and select third-party suppliers and service providers; Business services providing transparency and traceability in defense and security-related supply chains, including monitoring supplier qualifications, contract fulfilment status, and compliance credentials.
Class 36
Financial services for businesses and entrepreneurs; Fundraising services for commercial ventures; Investment consultancy and financial analysis; Venture capital funding services; Angel investment services; Financial sponsorship of entrepreneurial and innovation programmes; Real estate services relating to leasing, rental, and management of commercial properties; Financial transaction services relating to business investments; Financial risk assessment and management services; Financial planning and budgeting services; Financial consultancy in the field of venture capital; Financial services relating to the provision of venture capital; Financial services for the support of start-up businesses; Financial services relating to investment funding; Financial services provided to businesses, namely, providing working capital; Financial services for facilitating the investment of funds.
Class 42
Platform as a service (PaaS) featuring computer software platforms for managing, coordinating, and tracking defense-related government procurement programs; Software as a service (SaaS) services featuring software for hosting, visualising, and comparing supplier and subcontractor data across defense supply chains; SaaS services featuring software for facilitating marketplace functions, namely, the evaluation, scoring, and selection of government contractors and vendors based on performance metrics, compliance status, and capability benchmarks; SaaS services featuring software incorporating artificial intelligence (AI) for risk modelling, procurement optimisation, and predictive analytics relating to contract delivery; SaaS services for real-time tracking of supply chain events, contract milestones, and compliance verification; SaaS services for integrating procurement workflows with compliance management systems and third-party verification services.
Class 45
Advisory services in the field of regulatory compliance for government and defense procurement programs; Legal consultancy relating to public contract law, export control, and international compliance standards; Monitoring of subcontractor compliance with statutory and contractual obligations; Risk mitigation services in the field of multi-jurisdictional defense projects; Providing legal information and procedural guidance concerning cross-border defense procurement and program delivery; Compliance auditing services using automated tools and third-party data integrations; Consultancy in the standardisation and harmonisation of vendor due diligence procedures, credential verification, and supply chain certification; Providing transparency and governance services by publishing supplier compliance status and contractual risk indicators via online interfaces.
