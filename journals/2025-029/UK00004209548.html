Class 9
Computer software platforms for social networking, matchmaking and dating services; Application software for social networking, matchmaking and dating services via the internet; Downloadable software in the nature of a mobile application for internet-based introduction, dating, matchmaking and social networking; Communication, networking and social networking software; downloadable software in the nature of a mobile application in the field of social media, namely, for sending status updates to subscribers of web feeds, uploading and downloading electronic files to share with others; computer software for the collection, storage and transmission of digital files, data and information; computer software to enable uploading, downloading, accessing, posting, displaying, streaming, linking, sharing or otherwise providing electronic or digital media or information via computer and communication networks; downloadable software in the nature of a mobile application for social networking, matchmaking and dating.
Class 38
Chat room services for social networking and dating; Forums [chat rooms] for social networking; providing access to computer databases in the fields of social networking, social introduction and dating; telecommunications services, namely electronic transmission of data, messages, graphics, images and information; photo sharing services, namely, electronic transmission of digital photo files among internet users; providing access to computer, electronic and online databases; providing online forums for communication on topics of general interest; Communication services; internet telephony and video-conference services; messaging and instant messaging services via the Internet; telecommunications, namely, transmission of voice, data, images, audio, video, and information via cloud-based communication networks; email and instant messaging services; chat services; operating chat rooms; provision of online chat rooms; providing access to online services relating to social networking, social introduction and dating.
Class 41

Class 42

Class 45
