Class 1
Industrial chemicals; chemical reagents for use in industry; oxidation reagents; oxidising agents for industrial purposes; non-aqueous or non-polar sources of hydrogen peroxide; peroxide based chemical preparations for industrial use; chemical complexes comprising hydrogen peroxide and 2,2,5,5-tetramethyltetrahydrofuran (TMO); non-aqueous solvent systems for industrial use; chemical intermediates for use in industry; chemical additives for industrial processes; chemical preparations for use in fine chemical synthesis, depolymerisation, material recycling, waste processing and surface treatment; chemical agents for depolymerising polymers or composite materials; chemical agents for improving oxidation efficiency, degradation behaviour or processing performance; chemical preparations for use in sustainable chemistry, green manufacturing and circular material technologies; chemicals for use in manufacture.
Class 3
Cleaning, degreasing and surface treatment preparations for industrial and commercial use; oxidative cleaning agents; non-aqueous hydrogen peroxide based cleaning or sterilising preparations for non-medical use; solvent based cleaning preparations for industrial use; industrial degreasing preparations; surface activating preparations for industrial use; preparations for removing organic contaminants, coatings or residues from surfaces, equipment, textiles, polymers or composite materials; cleaning preparations for polymeric or composite substrates.

Sterilising, disinfecting and antimicrobial preparations for medical, surgical, dental or veterinary use; non-aqueous hydrogen peroxide based sterilising preparations; chemical sterilant for instruments, surfaces and equipment; disinfecting preparations for clinical, laboratory or healthcare environments; sterilising agents for use in medical device reprocessing.

Machines and apparatus for producing chemical reagents; equipment for generating oxidation systems or hydrogen peroxide based complexes on demand; industrial machines for chemical processing, material treatment, depolymerisation, textile recycling or surface cleaning; machines for treating polymers, textiles, composite materials or wastes; reactors and processing units for carrying out oxidative chemical transformations; parts and fittings for all the aforesaid goods.

Recycling, waste processing and material treatment services; chemical treatment of materials using oxidation processes; depolymerisation of polymers, textiles, rubbers and composite materials; chemical recycling services; treatment of cotton polyester or polycotton blends; services involving dissolution, oxidation or degradation of materials for recovery, reuse or purification purposes; separation, purification or recovery of components from mixed material waste streams.
