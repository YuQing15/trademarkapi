Hybrid World Commission

Class 41
Educational and training services relating to motorsport governance, safety, officiating, stewarding, technical regulations and sporting conduct; Provision of training courses, workshops, seminars and certification programmes relating to motorsport rules, standards, regulatory procedures and driver development; Publication of rulebooks, regulatory materials, guidance documents and educational content relating to motorsport governance; Organisation of examinations, assessments and accreditation activities for officials, stewards, marshals and other motorsport personnel; Organisation of non-commercial sporting events and demonstration activities for the purpose of training, education or regulatory evaluation.
Class 42
Provision of online platforms, software and digital systems for the administration, management and verification of licensing, accreditation and certification schemes in motorsport; Design, development and maintenance of databases, electronic record-keeping systems and digital identity systems for drivers, officials and participants; Technological services relating to the monitoring, auditing, verification and management of compliance with sporting, technical and licensing standards; Development of software for incident reporting, regulatory case management, adjudication workflows and safety compliance; Provision of temporary use of non-downloadable software for governance, rule management, licensing and regulatory oversight.
Class 45
Regulatory compliance and governance services relating to motorsport; Establishment, enforcement and oversight of sporting, safety, technical and ethical standards in motorsport; Arbitration, adjudication and dispute-resolution services relating to sporting regulations, competitions and disciplinary matters; Provision of advisory, consultancy and information services relating to governance frameworks, regulatory standards, licensing criteria and ethical conduct in motorsport; Investigation and disciplinary review services relating to breaches of sporting or technical regulations .
