The mark consists of an upward-facing arrow whose point intersects and ends within a circle appearing to the left of the wording "WORLD FOREST ID" followed by a horizontal line.

Class 35
Association services, namely, promoting the interests of those concerned with illegal logging, illicit natural resource trade, and deforestation; promoting public awareness of the issues surrounding illegal logging, and illicit natural resource trade; promoting public awareness of illegal logging, forest degradation, and deforestation; business consultation services in the fields of natural resource supply chain analysis, regulation compliance, and counterfeit detection; charitable services, namely, organizing and developing projects to promote awareness of illegal logging; promoting public awareness campaigns, all in the field of science-based traceability of commodities.
Class 41
Conducting seminars and conferences in the field of combating illegal logging and deforestation through the use of science; educational services, namely, providing classes and instruction in the field of testing methods for identifying natural resources originating from illegal logging, deforestation, and illicit trade; educational services, namely, conducting programs in the field of supply chain verification, natural resource verification, natural resource traceability, and prevention of illicit trade; education services, namely, conducting classes, seminars, conferences, organizing exhibitions, providing field training, and workshops, all in the field of science-based traceability of commodities, and distributing multimedia educational material in connection therewith; education services, namely, providing public information on education in the field of science-based traceability of commodities; education services, namely, arranging, conducting and providing training, in the form of educational conferences, congresses, classes, seminars, lectures, in-person forums, workshops and exhibitions in the field of traceability and verification of natural resource supply chains.
Class 42
Providing online scientific research information in the fields of identifying commodities, plant and animal species, geological specimen makeup, supply chain management, traceability, illegal trade, deforestation, and origin tracing of natural resources; scientific research and development in the fields of supply chain management, verification, and science-based traceability; developing voluntary standards, namely, developing species identification and source testing standards to combat illegal logging and deforestation; scientific and technological services, namely, testing, analysis, and evaluation of natural resource specimens to identify species and geological makeup and origin in the field of natural resources; scientific study and research in the field of origin traceability and chemistry of plants, animals, and other natural materials; providing on-line non-downloadable software using artificial intelligence for identifying, tracing, and verification of plant, animal, and geological geographic origin data; providing online non-downloadable software for collecting metadata linked to physical samples of plants, animals, and other natural resources; providing public scientific information, all in the field of science-based traceability of commodities.
