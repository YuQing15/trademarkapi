BRONZE SNAKE

Class 35
The bringing together, for the benefit of others, of general consumer goods (excluding the transport thereof), enabling customers to conveniently view and purchase those goods; retail and wholesale of clothing, footwear, headgear and accessories for men, women and children; retail and wholesale services connected with the sale of homewares, luggage, furniture, games, toys, playthings, bedding, cosmetics, jewellery, stationary art and crafting supplies, parts, fittings, and kits for all the aforesaid goods; provision of an online marketplace for buyers and sellers of goods and services; presentation of goods on communication media, for retail purposes; discount services (retail, wholesale, and/or sales promotion services), including online; telephone, online and computerised ordering of goods; ordering services (for others) including online, mail, and telephone order services; administrative processing of purchase orders; intermediary business services relating to the commercialisation of goods; import and export agency services; distribution of goods for advertising purposes; distribution of goods (not being transport services) (agent, wholesale, representative services, by any means); logistics services (business management and organisation of facilities and resources); advertising, marketing and promotional services; administration and management of retail, wholesale, advertising, marketing, and promotions; advertising and marketing services provided by means of indirect methods of marketing communications, namely, social media, search engine marketing, inquiry marketing, internet marketing, mobile marketing, blogging and other forms of passive, sharable or viral communication channels; arranging and conducting of exhibitions for advertising, trade, business and commercial purposes; production of video and sound recordings for advertising, marketing and publicity purposes; rental of advertising space and matter; radio and television advertising; arranging of displays and presentations for advertising purposes; marketing administration, analysis, studies, forecasting, information, and research; organisation, operation, management and supervision of customer loyalty and discount card programmes and schemes; business management and consultancy including management of a retail enterprise for others; business management and assistance including facilitating business contracts and commercial contractual arrangements; business representation services; business administration; business management; office functions; outsourcing and business management of facilities, resources and staff, including call centres and sales staff; business project management for others; business advice, assistance and consultancy relating to franchising; business franchising consultancy and business support services; business franchising services (group purchasing, group advertising); business management advisory services related to franchising; providing information, advice and consultancy in relation to all the aforesaid services; including provision of all the aforesaid services online, via a website, the internet, global computer networks or other computer networks, by electronic means or wireless technology, or accessible by mobile phones or other Internet-enabled devices.
