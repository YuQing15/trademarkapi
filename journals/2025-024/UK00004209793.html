Insurance and reinsurance consulting services, insurance and reinsurance brokerage, financial assessment (insurance and reinsurance); budget preparation services for estimating insurance and reinsurance costs; budget preparation services for estimating costs for insured parties; provision of information on insurance and reinsurance; consulting services in the field of underwriting: insurance, accident insurance, fire insurance, life insurance, marine insurance, medical insurance, property insurance, financial loss insurance, liability insurance of any kind and, in general, insurance authorized for sale by the National Insurance and Surety Commission; quantification of insured losses and in the field of insurance and reinsurance; professional advice on insurance and reinsurance, financial estimates or budgets in relation to insurance and reinsurance in the following areas: accident and miscellaneous risk insurance, fire insurance, life insurance, marine insurance, medical insurance, property insurance, financial loss insurance, liability insurance of any kind and, in general, insurance authorized for sale by the National Insurance and Surety Commission; financial appraisals and expert reports on insurance and reinsurance; insurance and reinsurance assessments; insurance and reinsurance financing; insurance and reinsurance valuation; provision of information related to insurance and reinsurance, as well as the quantification of insured losses through a website; insurance claim quantification and settlement services; financial services, including fiduciary services; processing of financial and insurance claims on behalf of insurers; risk management and financial and insurance advice on the prevention of claims; insurance claim assessments, processing of claims in the field of reinsurance, actuarial services in the field of reinsurance, provision of online information on reinsurance from a computer database or the internet; none of the aforementioned services being in respect of real estate and real estate services.
