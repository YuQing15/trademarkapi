Class 9
Downloadable software for managing financial accounts for vulnerable individuals; downloadable mobile applications for opening and managing bank accounts under appointeeship, deputyship, power of attorney or similar legal authority; downloadable software for carers, law firms and local authorities to access and administer banking services for vulnerable persons; security software for verifying legal authority for account access; electronic banking interface software; software for monitoring financial transactions, issuing alerts, and generating financial reports. .
Class 36
Banking services; provision of bank accounts for vulnerable individuals via a secure digital platform; financial services provided to appointees, attorneys, deputies and guardians for the management of client funds; financial services provided to local authorities, law firms and family members managing the finances of others; processing of financial transactions on behalf of vulnerable individuals; financial administration services; issuing and management of electronic money accounts.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) featuring software for managing and opening bank accounts for vulnerable individuals; platform as a service (PaaS) providing secure banking functionality for use by appointees, deputies, attorneys, carers, law firms, and local authorities; hosting of software platforms for the provision and administration of financial services for vulnerable persons; providing temporary use of non-downloadable software to facilitate financial safeguarding and oversight; data hosting and secure portal services for financial institutions and third parties representing vulnerable individuals. .
Class 45
Legal compliance verification services for the purpose of enabling authorised individuals and organisations to open bank accounts on behalf of vulnerable persons; providing information and advice in connection with the legal authority required to manage financial affairs of others; legal support services for appointees, deputies, attorneys and other fiduciaries; provision of authentication and verification services in relation to legal instruments used for financial account access; consulting services relating to compliance with laws governing financial care of vulnerable individuals.
