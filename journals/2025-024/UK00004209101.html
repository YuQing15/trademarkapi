DearGarden

Class 9
Downloadable mobile applications for AI- and technology-enabled garden design, plant care, gardening inspiration, and education; downloadable software featuring AI-generated garden plans, plant selection advice, care schedules, and personalised gardening recommendations; downloadable software for use in garden and landscape planning; downloadable guides and content for gardening and outdoor care; downloadable plant information resources, style and colour palette inspiration, and suggestions based on user preferences or location.
Class 35
Online retail services connected with the sale of gardening tools, landscaping tools, bricks, paving materials, plants, seeds, garden care products, furniture, compost, accessories, outdoor cooking equipment, garden pots, outdoor décor, lighting, and patio-related products; providing a digital marketplace for gardening and outdoor living goods and services; advertising and marketing of gardening and garden lifestyle products via online platforms; providing consumer information, product comparisons, and reviews related to garden and outdoor living goods.
Class 42
Software as a Service (SaaS) featuring AI- and technology-enabled software for garden design, plant care planning, and personalised gardening advice; garden design tools and visualisation software; hosting of digital platforms for connecting gardeners, sharing gardening insights, and interacting with service providers; plant tracking and analysis software; development and maintenance of web applications for garden and landscape planning; hosting user-generated garden designs and plant logs; filtering and recommendation systems based on user preferences, style, colour palettes, plant types, and location data (including postcode); software for matching gardening needs with third-party services and local contractors; design and development of computer software for gardening and horticultural purposes.
Class 44
Providing gardening information, plant care recommendations, and garden design inspiration via mobile and online platforms; AI- and technology-enabled and user-input-based advisory services for garden planning, plant selection, and layout layering; providing digital plant care reminders and personalised gardening insights through a mobile application; horticultural consultancy offered exclusively through digital and online means; providing educational content, inspiration filtered by styles, colours, and garden types, location-specific gardening suggestions, and lifestyle content and mindfulness prompts related to gardening and outdoor wellbeing; none of the foregoing including physical landscaping, gardening, or horticultural maintenance services.
Class 45
Hosting online social networks and virtual communities for gardeners; providing digital forums, discussion boards, and interactive spaces for users to share gardening content, post updates, ask and answer questions, exchange advice, and comment on garden designs or experiences; enabling online interaction, messaging, tagging, and content sharing between users; facilitating connections and community-led collaboration between gardeners and garden-related service providers.
