EDDIT COM/R

Class 45
Provision of legal services and brand protection solutions focused on safeguarding digital reputation, identity, and intellectual property across forums, social media, communication networks, and digital marketplaces; monitoring and enforcement against impersonation, identity theft, and false affiliation within group discussions, online posts, and digital communities; detection, reporting, and removal of unauthorised, defamatory, or misleading uses of registered trademarks or brand identities in user-generated content, AI-generated or bot-generated content, platform metadata, and other online communication environments; legal and procedural representation for individuals or entities affected by online defamation, brand impersonation, or coordinated misinformation campaigns; provision of compliance, takedown, and enforcement tools for moderating digital abuse, fake reviews, and unauthorised promotional use of protected intellectual property; operation of secure digital identity and brand ownership registries for dispute resolution, community trust building, and enforcement against reputational harm arising from the misuse of trade names in public or private online spaces; intellectual property enforcement and monitoring services; trademark watch services; online brand protection; anti-counterfeiting investigations; domain name monitoring and enforcement; investigation of intellectual property infringement; legal support services relating to brand abuse and misrepresentation; commercial rights enforcement; dispute resolution and compliance advisory services; legal consultancy in the field of brand ownership, IP strategy, and consumer protection.
