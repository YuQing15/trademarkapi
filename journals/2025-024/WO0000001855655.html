The mark consists of the verbal element "IMC MONTAN" that is made in the standard font and Latin letters.

Class 35
Business project management and organization consultancy services, including business management, operation, organization and administration of a commercial enterprise, administrative assistance in responding to calls for tenders, negotiation and conclusion of commercial transactions for third parties, business management of companies, procurement of contracts for others, business management of construction for mining extraction of minerals; business management consulting services, personnel management consultation services.
Class 36
Securities brokerage.
Class 41
Training services relating to increasing manufacturing volumes and improving manufacturing efficiency; education and training of personnel in the field of mining.
Class 42
Provision of expert reports and reports of specialists in the field of mining; provision of preliminary technical and economic studies and reports; research and exploration in the field of geology, including control of drilling process, geophysical exploration, conducting sampling, scientific assessment of resources, geological modeling of resources, consultancy services in the field of exploration, hydrogeology, research in the field of gas and mining industries; consultancy in the field of mining, including preliminary technical and economic assessment of projects for checking resources and reserves, technical analysis services, analysis of operational activities, hydrological research, design of mines; engineering services in the mining industry, including geotechnical services, engineering services in the development of quarries and underground mines, in particular, ventilation, degassing, air conditioning in mines, services in the field of rock mechanics and auxiliary equipment, mining exploration services, drying plant research services, briquetting plant research services; mining industry services, including groundwater monitoring, environmental risk assessment, research in the field of restoration, resource renewal, development of risk control software; mineral enrichment services, including testing, drawing up technological maps, conducting sampling, conducting detailed studies on mineral enrichment; engineering services.
Class 44
Health risk assessment.
Class 45
Legal services in the mining industry; safety risk assessment services.
