LIO

Class 9
Software for monitoring and tracking the health and safety of human beings and animals; software for monitoring and tracking vital signs, including blood pressure, oxygen saturation levels (SPO2), temperature, heart rate and breathing rate; software for monitoring and tracking sleep in human beings and animals; software for monitoring and tracking the movement, location and proximity of human beings and animals; software enabling real time status and alert services; electronic devices for monitoring and tracking the health, safety, movement, location, proximity and sleep of human beings and animals.
Class 10
Medical devices; medical devices for monitoring and tracking vital signs, including blood pressure, oxygen saturation levels (SPO2), temperature, heart rate and breathing rate.
Class 42
Software as a service; software as a service regarding the monitoring and tracking of the health and safety of human beings and animals; software as a service regarding the monitoring and tracking of vital signs, including blood pressure, oxygen saturation levels (SPO2), temperature, heart rate and breathing rate; software as a service regarding the monitoring and tracking of sleep in human beings and animals; software as a service regarding the monitoring and tracking of the movement, location and proximity of human beings and animals; software as a service providing real time status and alert services; information, advisory and consultancy services relating to the aforesaid.
Class 44
Health monitoring and tracking services; monitoring and tracking of vital signs, including temperature, blood pressure, oxygen saturation levels (SPO2), heart rate and breathing rate; monitoring and tracking of sleep;  Medical and veterinary services, namely monitoring and tracking of animals, people, patients and vulnerable people for health purposes via video monitoring equipment; Medical services, namely, monitoring and tracking of prisoners for health purposes via video monitoring equipment;  real time status and alert services relating to all of the aforesaid; information, advisory and consultancy services relating to the aforesaid.
Class 45
Video monitoring, tracking and surveillance services relating to the physical safety of individuals; Video monitoring, tracking and surveillance services for the protection of individuals; Non-medical personal monitoring and supervision services, namely, monitoring and tracking of individuals, including sleep patterns and general well-being indicators, for safety, security, and caregiving purposes; video-based monitoring and tracking of patients, vulnerable individuals, prisoners, and persons in dependent care settings; provision of real-time status updates and safety alerts relating to the foregoing; all provided for personal security, protection, or to meet the social or caregiving needs of individuals and their families; information, advisory and consultancy services relating to the aforesaid.
