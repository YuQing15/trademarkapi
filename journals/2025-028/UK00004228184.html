Class 9
Downloadable mobile applications for vineyard and winery discovery and engagement; software for locating and researching vineyards and wineries; software for route planning and navigation to vineyard and winery destinations; software for accessing wine tasting notes provided by vineyards and wineries and for users to create and manage their own tasting notes; software for researching and planning vineyard and winery trips in advance; software enabling user interaction and sharing of reviews and recommendations; downloadable, searchable and filterable databases featuring wine routes, vineyard and winery profiles, wine styles and varietals, wine-related events, and vineyard and winery amenities; software for providing information about local transportation services; software for delivering subscriber-based content, offers, and discounts from third-party providers.
Class 35
Advertising and promotional services for vineyards, wineries and wine-related events; business information services relating to vineyards, wineries, and wine tourism; promotion of the goods and services of others, namely vineyards, wineries, and local transportation providers; dissemination of promotional offers on behalf of third parties.
Class 39
Providing travel information and route navigation services via a mobile application, particularly in the field of wine tourism; provision of information about travel and transportation options to and between vineyards, wineries, and other related destinations; electronic travel guide services.
Class 41
Providing information about events held at or in association with vineyards and wineries, including but not limited to wine- and food-related experiences, festivals, markets, fairs, and seasonal, cultural, community, or recreational events; online publication of educational content relating to wine, wine styles and varietals, vineyards, wineries, and wine tourism.
Class 43
Providing information about vineyard and winery-based hospitality services, including venue hire for weddings, private events, and receptions; providing information relating to food and drink services at vineyards and wineries.
