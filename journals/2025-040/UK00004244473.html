RAW WINE BAZAAR

Class 35
Arranging and conducting of trade fairs and consumer fairs relating to food and drink; arranging and conducting of trade fairs and consumer fairs relating to wine; organisation, promotion and management of exhibitions relating to food and drink and the service of food and drink for commercial or advertising purposes; advertising, promotional services, placing of advertising, direct mail advertising services, outbound telemarketing campaigns, marketing, all relating to exhibitions; ticket sales relating to exhibitions for commercial or advertising purposes; product launches; business networking services; retail services, including online retail services, connected with the sale of food and drink products; retail services, including online retail services, connected with the sale of wine (including sparkling wine, Champagne and vermouth), fortified wine, fruit wine, beer, perry, cider, non-alcoholic drinks, spirits, liqueurs, brandy, Armagnac; all of the aforesaid services in the field of food and drink.

arranging of competitions and award ceremonies relating to food and drink; arranging of competitions and award ceremonies relating to wine; arranging of competitions and award ceremonies relating to sparkling wine, Champagne, vermouth, fortified wine, fruit wine, beer, perry, cider, non-alcoholic drinks, spirits, liqueurs, brandy, Armagnac;
