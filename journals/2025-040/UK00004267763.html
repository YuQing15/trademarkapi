ASSETPAY

Class 9
Downloadable computer software for blockchain-based payment processing and settlement of tokenized asset transactions; downloadable applications for managing digital wallets for cryptocurrency and tokenized assets; software for secure electronic payment transactions and regulatory compliance in blockchain transactions; software for distributed ledger platforms to record payment, settlement, and tokenized asset liquidity data; computer software featuring smart contracts for automating payment processing, splits, refunds, and tokenization of assets; software for cryptographic security and tamper-proof hashing in blockchain-based payment systems; computer software for managing crypto asset transactions, electronic funds transfers, and tokenized asset liquidity using blockchain technology.
Class 35
Business management services for blockchain-based payment and transaction platforms; provision of online marketplaces for buyers and sellers of tokenized assets, including bullion, property, art, collectibles, inventory, and infrastructure; retail services connected with the sale of digital assets authenticated by non-fungible tokens; business intermediary services for facilitating blockchain-based payment processing, tokenized asset transactions, and merchant onboarding; business data analysis for payment reconciliation, transaction tracking, and tokenized asset liquidity; advertising and promotion of blockchain-based payment and liquidity services; operation of electronic marketplaces for tokenized asset payments and settlements.
Class 36
Financial services, namely, electronic payment processing and settlement services for tokenized asset transactions in fiat currency or cryptocurrency; virtual currency exchange services for tokenized assets; providing a digital currency or digital token for use in payment transactions via a blockchain network; financial management of digital assets for secure blockchain-based payments and liquidity spending; electronic funds transfer services for tokenized assets across global financial systems; regulatory compliance verification for tokenized asset payments, including anti-money laundering, sanctions screening, and cross-border checks; financial consultancy for blockchain-based payment processing, settlement, tokenization, and asset-backed liquidity draws; financial valuation and programmable splitting of transaction proceeds for tokenized assets; issuance of tokens of value for tokenized asset payments.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) featuring software for blockchain-based payment processing, settlement, compliance, and liquidity management for tokenized assets; platform as a service (PaaS) featuring software platforms for managing tokenized asset payments, digital wallets, regulatory compliance, and programmable transaction splits; blockchain-as-a-service (BaaS) for payment processing, settlement, and tokenized asset management; data storage via blockchain for payment transaction logs, audit trails, compliance records, and tokenized asset data; data authentication and certification services using blockchain for payment verification, identity validation, transaction integrity, and tokenized asset spending; technological consultancy in the field of blockchain technology for payment processing, settlement, tokenized asset liquidity, and interoperability across global financial and trading systems; computer programming of smart contracts for blockchain-based payment automation and tokenized asset transactions.
