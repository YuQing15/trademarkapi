Trade Mark Journal No.2025/040 3 October 2025

UK00004230905 8 July 2025 (13,15,20)

The mark comprises a dynamic motion sequence in which a stylised, wall-mounted hand hanger transitions from an open, relaxed posture to a closed configuration through a deliberate, articulated gesture. The fingers and thumb move in coordinated succession, drawing inward to evoke intention, protection, and containment. In the final frame, the thumb folds securely over the curled fingertips in a distinctive overlapping motion, establishing a definitive and recognisable endpoint. The animation’s pacing communicates steadiness and control, reinforcing values of craftsmanship, presence, and restraint. The motion embodies the design language—blending functional elegance with expressive identity.

- No claim is made to colour. - No claim is made to vertical motion of the hand or activation mechanisms visible in the video. - No claim is made to the orientation of the hand or gesture; it may be displayed in vertical, horizontal, or rotated alignments. - The claimed motion is limited to the relative movement of the fingers and thumb in relation to each other and the fixed position of the hand structure. - The mark is limited to the finger and thumb motion as shown in the accompanying video.
