ECCTIS

Class 35
Business advisory services; business research services; computerised data verification services; electronic document verification; benchmarking services; administering of professional [vocational] certifications; providing academic course administration services for academic institutions; administering of professional competency testing; work analysis to determine worker skill sets and other worker requirements; career information and advisory services (other than educational and training advice); advisory services relating to the recognition and comparison of professional qualifications and skill sets.
Class 41
Education services; training services; educational research services; accreditation [certifying] of educational achievement; accreditation of educational services; professional consultancy relating to education; educational certification services, namely, providing training and educational examination; advisory services relating to the recognition and comparison of academic qualifications; provision of language schools and language courses.
Class 42
Software-as-service; platform-as-a-service; computer project management services; software as a service [SaaS] and platforms for use by universities and other educational establishments, students, prospective students and agencies in student applications, enrolment and recruitment and for supporting the student application, enrolment and recruitment process.
Class 45
Personal background verification services; provision of checking services in connection with student visas and immigration and residency status; identity verification and validation services; services for the checking of documents relating to, or for use in, student applications, enrolment and recruitment; legal compliance auditing.
