Templa

Class 9
Downloadable electronic publications, templates and forms; contracts, policies, manuals, checklists, reports and guides; downloadable instructional, training and educational materials; computer software for creating, editing, managing, storing and sharing templates, documents and publications; software platforms; providing subscription access to templates, automated document creation; workflow and compliance tools; downloadable mobile applications relating to the foregoing.
Class 35
Provision of business documentation and templates; business administration support services relating to preparation, management and compliance; subscription-based services providing access to templates, documents and document management platforms; consultancy and advisory services limited to documentation, compliance, governance, policy and process design, management and implementation, including advisory services relating to the use, deployment or optimisation of templates, documents, policies and governance systems; outsourcing services in the field of document preparation and management; provision of information, resources and tools relating to documentation, compliance and governance; information, advisory and consultancy services relating to the foregoing.
Class 41
Provision of training, workshops, webinars and courses in respect of documentation, compliance, governance, policy and process design; publication of training manuals, guides, art, , journals and instructional materials in print and digital format; educational services relating to documentation, compliance, governance and information systems; provision of online resources and e-learning platforms in the field of documentation, compliance and governances.
Class 45
Information, legal support and legal consultancy services, other than the provision of representation in legal proceedings; provision of legal resources and tools; legal document preparation services; paralegal and outsourced legal support services; compliance, regulatory and risk assessment information services; legal, regulatory or policy advisory services limited to documentation, governance, compliance and process design; research services relating to templates, policies, manuals and governance frameworks; subscription-based support services for document and template management; provision of online portals for access to legal, compliance and governance tools; information, advisory and consultancy services relating to the foregoing.
