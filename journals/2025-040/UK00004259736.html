HostiPilotX

Class 9
Computer software; downloadable mobile applications; software for host, venue, and operations management across multiple industries, including hospitality, travel, retail, education, and entertainment; software for booking, reservations, and scheduling services; software for managing host-side service information, metadata, and availability; software for artificial intelligence-driven host assistants; downloadable software for metadata processing and enforcement; software for enabling host-managed payments, ticketing, customer interaction, and service adjustments; software for real-time communication between hosts, venues, staff, and users.
Class 35
Business data management services for venues and hosts; providing business information via metadata systems; consultancy relating to service, event, and reservation management; business management of customer data, bookings, ticketing, and reservations; promotion and organisation of hospitality and event services; business services for the management of metadata relating to guest ordering, reservations, and service delivery.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) featuring software for host, venue, and operations management, including reservations, bookings, staffing, and scheduling across multiple industries; providing online non-downloadable software for AI-driven host assistants, concierge-venue coordination, and service management; platform as a service (PaaS) for metadata processing, conversion, and enforcement; artificial intelligence services for managing host-side operations including reservations, ticketing, pre-orders, staff scheduling, and real-time service delivery across hospitality, travel, retail, education, and entertainment; hosting platforms enabling structured metadata input and communication between hosts, staff, and users; provision of white-labelled software platforms for third-party branding.
Class 43
Hospitality services provided via AI-driven host and venue assistants; reservation services for bars, restaurants, nightclubs, hotels, events, and entertainment venues; providing information in the field of hospitality services, reservations, and event bookings.
Class 45
Software licensing services; licensing of AI-driven host and concierge platforms; licensing of SaaS and PaaS software solutions for hospitality, travel, retail, healthcare, and entertainment; licensing consultancy and advisory services relating to artificial intelligence platforms for service provision; concierge services in connection with hospitality, dining, travel, and event experiences enabling venues and hosts to deliver personalised guest services via AI.
