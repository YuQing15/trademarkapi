Providing health and wellness advisory services; providing health and wellness advisory services in the field of performance nutrition; personalised dietary and nutrition planning; health counselling by means of an AI-powered virtual coach; dietary guidance services; health advice and information services; nutritional advice; providing information about dietary and nutritional supplements; custom health, fitness and nutritional advice; online consultations in the fields of nutrition, health, wellness, diet and exercise; information, consultancy and advisory services for the aforesaid.
