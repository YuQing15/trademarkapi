The Zafar ReGen Method

Class 35
Franchising, namely, providing business management assistance in the establishment and operation of regenerative aesthetic clinics using the Zafar ReGen Method; business consultancy and advisory services in the field of implementing regenerative aesthetic methods and protocols; advertising and marketing services for regenerative aesthetic programs and services.
Class 41
Education and training services, namely, conducting workshops, seminars, and masterclasses in the field of regenerative aesthetic medicine related to the Zafar ReGen Method; providing professional certification programs in the fields of regenerative aesthetics and facial and hair restoration; instruction in the use of proprietary clinical protocols; development and distribution of educational materials, namely, training manuals, video recordings, and online multimedia content in the field of aesthetic medicine.
Class 44
Provision of non-surgical regenerative aesthetic treatments using a proprietary protocol; delivery of facial, scalp, and body regeneration methods, namely for the neck, décolletage, and hands; personalised treatment planning based on structural and biological diagnostics; application of platelet-rich fibrin (PRF), platelet-rich plasma (PRP), and other biologic therapies; collagen and tissue regeneration; hair follicle stimulation; skin and tissue restoration services performed according to a proprietary aesthetic methodology; all of the foregoing provided via specific delivery methods, namely micro-needling, injection therapies, and non-surgical mesotherapy.
Class 45
Licensing of intellectual property, namely, proprietary aesthetic treatment methods, clinical protocols, and regeneration systems.
