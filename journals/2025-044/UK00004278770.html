Mark Description: The logo consists of the words ‘LITTLE ITALY UK’ arranged on three lines. The word ‘LITTLE’ is at the top in green uppercase letters, in a bold sans-serif font. The word ‘ITALY’ is placed in the centre in red uppercase letters, in a thicker bold font to make it the dominant element of the logo. The word ‘UK’ is positioned at the bottom in blue uppercase letters, also in a bold sans-serif font. The design is centred and displayed on a white background. The colours used are green (LITTLE), red (ITALY), and blue (UK), representing the connection between Italy and the United Kingdom.
