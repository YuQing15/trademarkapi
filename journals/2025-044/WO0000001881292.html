The mark consists of the words "Dental tour" in a bold, rounded, lowercase sans-serif font. The wording is presented in a horizontal linear gradient, transitioning from deep violet at the left to purple at the right. The background is white.

The registration of this designation shall give no exclusive right to the use of the word "Dental".

The mark contains the colours purple, violet and white.

Deep violet (#20014A): This color appears at the beginning of the verbal element, starting with the letter "D" in "Dental", purple (#A060F7): This color appears at the end of the verbal element, ending with the letter "r" in "tour".
