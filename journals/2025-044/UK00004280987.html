C.T.E.M. Fashion

Class 35
Business development, marketing, and promotional services within the fashion and creative industries; organisation of trade shows, networking events, and business expos for designers, brands, and manufacturers; consultancy relating to brand strategy, sustainability, and circular fashion models; providing business and market intelligence; promoting collaboration between fashion, technology, and manufacturing sectors; retail promotion and sponsorship services; information, consultancy, and advisory services relating to all the aforesaid.
Class 36
Financial sponsorship, funding, and grant services relating to the fashion, design, and creative industries; arranging financial partnerships, sponsorship programs, and investment schemes to support fashion innovation, education, sustainability, and manufacturing; fundraising and financial consultancy for creative, cultural, and community initiatives; administration of sponsorships and charitable contributions; information, advisory, and consultancy services relating to all the aforesaid.
Class 41
Educational, training, and professional development services; organising and delivering courses, workshops, conferences, exhibitions, and seminars focused on the fashion and clothing industries; planning and producing fashion shows, industry events, and showcases for educational or trade purposes; hosting award ceremonies celebrating innovation, design, and sustainability within the fashion sector; publishing and digital publishing of magazines, articles, and multimedia content; production and distribution of audio-visual materials; providing information, consultancy, and advisory services relating to all the aforesaid.
Class 42
Research, design, and development services relating to materials, textiles, and sustainable manufacturing in the fashion industry; technological consultancy and innovation support in circular fashion and product lifecycle management; creation and implementation of digital tools and platforms for design, retail, and supply chain transparency; providing information, research, and advisory services relating to sustainable technologies and innovation in fashion.
