EPR Rights

Class 9
Downloadable and recorded sound files; digital audio recordings; downloadable music files; downloadable data files containing metadata relating to audio production and authorship; computer software and mobile applications for managing, certifying, and distributing music production rights; computer programs for audio recording, mixing, mastering, and metadata registration; downloadable electronic publications in the field of sound engineering and music authorship.
Class 41
Education and training in the field of music production, sound engineering, and authorship rights; organisation and provision of workshops, lectures, courses, and events relating to audio engineering and creative rights; publication of educational materials, books, and online content; development and certification of educational frameworks and methodologies in sound authorship; cultural activities and events promoting equity and recognition in the music and recording industry.
Class 42
Research, design, and development of technological frameworks for music production and authorship recognition; creation and maintenance of databases for audio metadata management; design and implementation of systems for registering, tracking, and verifying creative contributions in recorded sound; development of data models and digital standards for authorship certification; consultancy and innovation services in the field of audio engineering and rights management.
