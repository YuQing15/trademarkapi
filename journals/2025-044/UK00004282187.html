TAILORPAL

Class 35
Online marketplace services connecting consumers with tailoring, clothing-alteration and garment-repair service providers; Provision of an online business directory featuring tailors, seamstresses and alteration specialists; Business intermediary services relating to the matching of consumers with service providers; Business management and administration of online platforms for tailoring and alteration businesses; Presentation of goods and services on communication media for retail purposes in the field of tailoring, alterations and garment care; Compilation and systemisation of information into computer databases; Advertising, marketing and promotional services for tailoring, alteration and clothing-care businesses; Arranging and conducting of consumer review and feedback services for tailoring and alteration businesses; Providing an online searchable database of tailoring and alteration professionals; Organisation and management of loyalty, incentive and rewards programmes (excluding financial services); Provision of online ordering and scheduling services for tailoring and alteration businesses; Provision of online ordering, appointment-scheduling and customer-relationship management services; Provision of online communication tools for customer engagement and reputation management; Provision of commercial information, business ratings, reviews and directories relating to tailoring, fashion and garment-care services; Business consultancy and support services in relation to digital operations, online presence and client management for tailoring and alteration professionals; Facilitating business networking and partnerships among tailoring, fashion and clothing-service providers.
Class 42
Provision of online non-downloadable software for searching, comparing, and booking tailoring, clothing alteration, and repair services; Software as a service (SaaS) featuring software for use by tailoring and alteration businesses to manage bookings, communications, and payments; Platform as a service (PaaS) featuring computer software platforms for connecting service providers and consumers; Design, development, and maintenance of computer software, mobile applications, and web platforms for online marketplaces; Design, development and maintenance of computer software and mobile applications enabling users to search for, compare and book tailoring, clothing-alteration and garment-repair services; Providing temporary use of non-downloadable software enabling users to book, message, review, and pay tailoring and alteration providers; Hosting of e-commerce and service-marketplace platforms for tailoring, clothing-alteration and garment-repair services; Hosting of an online platform facilitating communication and transactions between consumers and tailoring/alteration providers; Providing temporary use of online non-downloadable software for order tracking, notifications, and history management; Cloud computing services, namely hosting and infrastructure for tailoring-sector marketplace platforms; Design and development of APIs and databases for online service-booking systems; Design and development of software for facilitating digital payments, quotes, and estimates; Design, development and maintenance of technology infrastructure, hosting environments and data storage systems for tailoring and alteration marketplace platforms; Providing temporary use of non-downloadable software for data encryption and secure payment processing as part of online tailoring and alteration marketplace operations; Provision of software tools enabling tailoring businesses to manage customer relationships and operational processes; Provision of software for managing user accounts, order tracking and notifications; Consultancy and technical support services relating to the operation of online marketplace platforms; Providing temporary use of online non-downloadable software for managing customer relationships (CRM), appointment scheduling and workflow automation for tailoring and alteration businesses.
