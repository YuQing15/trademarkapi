Access control systems; electronic purse; downloadable software for management, reservation and purchase of tickets and memberships; ticket dispensing terminals, electronic; electronic ticket examination machines, ticket printers, all the aforesaid for entering restricted areas such as visitor attractions, sports facilities, cultural, training, leisure or entertainment centres; downloadable software applications that interact with devices for managing and controlling access to buildings and facilities such as visitor attractions, sports facilities, cultural, training, leisure or entertainment centres and for locker management; magnetic or encoded identification bracelets, rings, wristbands and key fobs; radio frequency identification bracelets, rings, wristbands and key fobs; encoded or magnetic key card, members cards and access cards; downloadable software for secure electronic payment on the internet; downloadable computer software for processing electronic payments and transferring funds to and from others; authentication software that may be downloaded from a global computer network and/or recorded on computer media for controlling access to restricted areas and for ticketing management; credentials, tickets, coupons and vouchers downloadable from a global computer network; downloadable data files in the nature of digital tickets and access credential; electronic labels; electronic displays for food and beverage self-ordering machines.
