Software, namely, software used for monitoring and tracking medical equipment status and usage in real time, and perform early diagnostics in case of malfunction; software for collecting data on medical equipment in order to schedule and track preventive and corrective maintenance activities of medical equipment; software for managing inventory of medical equipment and parts thereof; software for generating reports on medical equipment performance and maintenance activities, to be used by healthcare professionals and personnel in healthcare facilities, long-term care and rehabilitation centers; software for collecting, monitoring, displaying and transmitting comprehensive data regarding the patient overall well-being and usage of the medical equipment, namely, the patient's comfort settings, weight records, positions, in-bed time history log, providing real time overview of patient status and sending real time alert notifications to healthcare professionals and personnel in healthcare facilities, long-term care and rehabilitation centers.
