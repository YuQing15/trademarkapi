The trademark has a complex structure being composed by word and figurative elements. In particular, the words "QC" and "SPA OF WONDERS" can be read. The sames one are placed on two different levels. The wording "QC" is bigger than the other one. The letter, "SPA OF WONDERS", is in fact created in smaller characters than the aforementioned wording. The first letter "Q" of the wording "QC" is characterized, in its lower part, of a curvilinear line and, in its right side, by the presence of lines of various sizes. Furthermore, the aforementioned letter intersects, in the central part, the letter "C" of the wording in question. All of it highly stylized.
