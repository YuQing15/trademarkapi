The trademark is made up of denominative and figurative elements. In particular, the words “MIDALI” and “TOUJOURS” can be read. These words are placed on the same plane. The first letter “M” of the wording “MIDALI” is larger than the rest of the letters. Similarly, the first letter “T” and the fourth letter “J” of the wording “TOUJOURS” are larger than the rest of the letters. Two small circles are also visible on the second and sixth letters of the wording “MIDALI”. All highly stylised. This brand can be reproduced in all colours, colour combinations and their shades.
