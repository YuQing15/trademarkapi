CrowdCard

Class 9
Downloadable software for managing digital wallet balances, user credits, rewards, and promotional vouchers; software for in-app payments, booking integrations, and automatic refund processing; downloadable software for issuing, storing, and applying gift cards and promotional codes; software for managing stored value systems and in-app transactions; downloadable applications for peer-to-peer credit transfers and e-gifting; downloadable loyalty and voucher redemption software; downloadable SaaS platforms for digital wallet services.

Customer loyalty and incentive program management; administration of rewards, promotional credits, and gift card systems; promotional campaign tracking and voucher redemptions; business services relating to user reward programmes and digital loyalty schemes; marketing services relating to platform credit and user reward systems; customer relationship management using credit-based platforms.

Software as a Service (SaaS) for managing digital wallet balances, rewards, and in-app payments; Platform as a Service (PaaS) for stored value systems and booking integration; design and development of systems for digital payment routing, credit redemption, and automated refund logic; hosting of wallet-based infrastructure for in-app services; development of user-facing platforms for applying and managing promotional vouchers, credit balances, and gift card tools; backend infrastructure for platform wallet functionality and reward system logic.
