PromptSafe

No claim is made to the exclusive right to use the words ‘prompt’ or ‘safe’ individually, apart from the mark as a whole.

Class 9
Downloadable computer software and artificial intelligence software; downloadable software for generating and managing synthetic personas using large language models; downloadable software for developing evaluator models to assess AI-generated outputs for compliance, safety, accuracy, empathy and tone; software for simulating AI conversations for testing and evaluation purposes; computer software for testing and validating the performance of conversational AI systems through interaction with synthetic agents; downloadable toolkits for building and customising prompt safety frameworks; software supporting agentic frameworks and the orchestration of multiple large language models (LLMs); downloadable tools for integration with production systems and real-time AI monitoring; all of the foregoing incorporating scientific methods, technical know-how, and research-based methodologies.
Class 35
Business consultancy relating to the strategic deployment, evaluation and responsible use of artificial intelligence systems; advisory services on implementing synthetic persona testing for risk assessment and regulatory compliance; consultancy in the field of responsible AI adoption, including the use of evaluator models for monitoring system behaviour, tone, empathy and safety; strategic advisory services on integrating AI assistants, agentic frameworks and prompt safety systems into enterprise and production settings; guidance on monitoring AI safety and performance in live environments; advisory services to healthcare, financial and regulated organisations on governance frameworks for conversational AI systems; all consultancy services incorporating scientific methodologies, technical know-how and research-led evaluation frameworks.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) featuring tools for generating and managing synthetic personas using artificial intelligence; provision of non-downloadable software for testing and validating AI systems using persona-based conversation simulation; custom development and configuration of synthetic personas based on clinical, behavioural, psychological and demographic traits; development of evaluator models using large language models for assessing AI system responses across domains including regulatory compliance, safety, accuracy, tone and empathy; provision of platforms for simulating AI-human interactions; development of testing frameworks for conversational AI evaluation; technical support for the deployment and integration of prompt safety systems into production environments; design and implementation of agentic frameworks and orchestrated LLM-based workflows; monitoring and evaluation of AI behaviour in production contexts; consultancy in the field of artificial intelligence and prompt safety systems; all of the foregoing grounded in scientific research, technical know-how, and evidence-based evaluation.
