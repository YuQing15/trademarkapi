The mark consists of an octagon with rounded corners with the four shorter flat sides aligning north, south, east and west. The octagon is comprised of four equal trapezoids and contains a square rotated 45 degrees. Inside the square is another smaller octagon with rounded corners also comprised of four equal trapezoids, but this octagon is rotated 45 degrees and has slightly different proportions. Inside the inner octagon is also a smaller square oriented with its flat sides in north-south and east-west orientations.
