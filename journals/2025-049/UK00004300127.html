IntriMail Tracked

Class 9
Computer software, downloadable software, applications and platforms for mail handling, mail tracking, postal item monitoring, document management and communications management; downloadable electronic publications and materials in the field of mail services, logistics, delivery status reporting and addressing systems; software for providing proof of sending, logging acceptance, generating tracking identifiers and monitoring routing to postal sorting offices; data capture software, barcode scanning systems, QR code systems and reporting tools for postal tracking and mail movement analysis; electronic databases containing postal routing information, tracking data, audit logs and verification records relating to dispatch and processing of mail; recorded and downloadable data and information relating to postal tracking, dispatch verification and sorting office processing.
Class 16
Printed materials and stationery relating to tracked mail; printed labels, envelopes, stickers, addressing media and packaging incorporating tracking codes, barcodes or identifiers for mail routing and postal verification; printed forms, documentation and certificates providing evidence of dispatch or processing of mail items; instructional and training materials relating to tracked mail systems and postal proof of sending.
Class 35
Business management and business administration services; business management assistance and business enquiries; business management reporting services; data processing services; computerised database management; computerised data capture, processing and verification; data cleaning services; data archiving services; document handling services; document scanning and indexing services; electronic storage of scanned and uploaded documents; computerised file management; business support services involving processing incoming and outgoing communications; analysis of business expenditure and budgets, including in relation to mailing costs; statistical studies and provision of statistical and commercial information; compilation, management and provision of tracking data for compliance, audit, evidential and operational reporting purposes; business process outsourcing relating to proof of mailing, dispatch confirmation and postal routing transparency; business records management services providing documentation of sending, processing and sorting events for mail items; advisory, information and consultancy services relating to all the aforesaid, including the provision of such services online from a computer database or via the Internet.
Class 38
Electronic communication services; electronic messaging services including the transmission of email and SMS messages; data transmission and data network services; computer network communications services; transmission and reception of data and information relating to tracked physical mail, email and SMS communications; providing access to computer databases relating to tracking status, routing information and proof of sending; secure electronic messaging services including the identification and authentication of senders, recipients and message content; communication services for the delivery of system alerts, delivery confirmations, read-status updates and routing notifications for physical letters, email and SMS communications; electronic transmission of postal tracking data, dispatch confirmation records and sorting office acceptance information; information, advisory and consultancy services relating to all the aforesaid, including the provision of such services online from a computer database or via the Internet or extranets.
Class 39
Mail tracking services providing evidence of dispatch, routing, acceptance into postal systems and movement to sorting offices; provision of tracking information and proof of delivery status for mail items, physical letters, email and SMS communications; advisory services for locating and identifying the status of items in transit within postal or communication networks; provision of postal services information via websites, portals or online platforms; logistics monitoring services relating to postal handling, sorting, shipment and processing events; verification services confirming the acceptance, processing or routing status of mail within postal networks; information, advisory and consultancy services relating to all the aforesaid, including the provision of such services online from a computer database or via the Internet or extranets.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) for tracking physical mail, email and SMS communications, providing proof of sending, delivery status and routing data and accessing tracking records; hosting of online platforms for monitoring mail movement, sorting office receipt and postal processing; design, development, updating, maintenance and programming of computer software and computerised mail management systems; consultancy, design, testing, research and advisory services relating to computing, software and communications platforms; encryption and decryption of electronic documents and secure data transmission associated with tracked communications; provision of web-based applications for mail handling, tracking, data handling, document management and communications management; design and provision of online data reporting dashboards and verification interfaces; temporary use of non-downloadable software for postal audit, compliance, tracking transparency and evidential data retention; advisory, information and consultancy services relating to all the aforesaid, including the provision of such services online from a computer database or via the Internet.
Class 45
Verification services providing proof of sending, dispatch confirmation and evidence of acceptance of mail into postal handling systems; authentication, certification and evidential documentation relating to tracked postal items for legal, regulatory or contractual purposes; audit support services relating to tracking records, dispatch logs and postal processing history; compliance advisory services relating to documentation and evidential requirements for postal proof of sending and tracking.
