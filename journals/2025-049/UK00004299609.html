ASTROANIMAL

Class 9
Downloadable mobile application software for providing personalized astrological readings, birth chart interpretations, and behavioral guidance for pets using artificial intelligence and machine learning that analyzes pet-specific data including birth date, time, location, breed characteristics, and gender to generate astrological profiles based on planetary positions, zodiac signs, and house placements; downloadable software providing daily, weekly, and personalized horoscope readings for pets; downloadable software for creating and storing pet astrological profiles and birth charts; downloadable software for generating compatibility analyses between pets and their owners, and between multiple pets based on astrological data comparison.
Class 42
Software as a Service (SaaS) services for providing personalized astrological readings, birth chart interpretations, and behavioral guidance for pets using artificial intelligence and machine learning that analyzes pet-specific data including birth date, time, location, breed characteristics, and gender to generate astrological profiles based on planetary positions, zodiac signs, and house placements; providing online non-downloadable software for generating daily, weekly, and personalized horoscope readings for pets; providing online non-downloadable software for compatibility analysis between pets and their owners, and between multiple pets through astrological data comparison; technical consultation for implementing artificial intelligence and machine learning-enhanced applications in pet wellness and behavioral guidance programs.
Class 45
Providing personal advisory and consultation services in the field of pet astrology, personality assessment, and behavioral guidance; providing astrological chart reading services for pets; providing compatibility analysis services between pets and their owners based on comparative astrological chart interpretation; providing compatibility assessment services between multiple pets in household environments; online social networking services for pet owners to share and discuss astrological insights about their pets; all of the foregoing services being provided through analysis of astronomical data and artificial intelligence-based interpretation of astrological frameworks including zodiac signs, planetary positions, and house systems as applied to animals and their human companions.
