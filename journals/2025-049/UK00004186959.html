The trademark "HARDURA" is a combination of "hard" and "durable". In English, "hard" signifies rigidness, while "durable" indicates long-lasting endurance. Incorporating these two words into the trademark name "HARDURA" highlights the exceptional durability and robustness of our stainless steel commercial kitchenware, capable of enduring various challenges in kitchen environments. The trademark comprises three colors: red, blue, and white. White serves as the background color, further emphasizing the minimalist design style of our products. This aligns with the pursuit of efficiency and hygiene in modern commercial kitchens, making our products seamlessly blend into various kitchen settings. Blue symbolizes trust, stability, and professionalism, conveying that our brand is dedicated to providing professional-grade commercial kitchenware and earning the trust and loyalty of our customers. Red, on the other hand, embodies the brand's positive and proactive spirit, as well as its commitment to continuous innovation and progress.
