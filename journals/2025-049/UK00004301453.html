Class 41
Educational services; education and training services in the fields of scientific naturopathy, lifestyle medicine, functional medicine, evidence-based natural health and related integrative health disciplines; provision of online educational materials; organisation and conducting of seminars, workshops, conferences, cookery classes and healthy cooking demonstrations; arranging and conducting public health awareness events, including educational and informational demonstrations of basic non-invasive health checks and well-being practices; development of educational curricula, training programmes and foundational non-accredited courses for volunteers and community health leaders; private services for the evaluation, validation and recognition of educational standards for training programmes in the above fields, including the review of educational content, academic quality assessment and the issuing of private accreditations; professional membership services for practitioners, including continuing professional development.
Class 42
Scientific and technological services; development, maintenance and updating of evidence-based educational standards, technical frameworks and quality guidelines in the fields of scientific naturopathy, lifestyle medicine, functional medicine, evidence-based natural health, nutritional science, mental health, psychoneuroimmunology and related integrative health disciplines; evaluation and validation of academic curricula, training methodologies and competency frameworks in the above fields; research and consultancy relating to educational standards, quality assurance and methodological development; providing scientific information, research reports and technical guidance via electronic platforms; publishing of online non-downloadable and downloadable scientific information, research reports and scientific guides; maintaining and updating databases for scientific and educational reference purposes; consultancy and advisory services relating to scientific research and evidence-based health methodologies; provision of technical expertise for the assessment of compliance with the applicant’s private standards.
