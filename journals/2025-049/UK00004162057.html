Mark 1

The Pet Gundog

Mark 2

Pet Gundog

Class 16
Printed matter;  instructional and teaching material [except apparatus];  books; magazines; printed instructional materials; brochures; training manuals and printed guides featuring gun dog training techniques, canine behavioural therapy, dog toy usage and development, and dog care management; books featuring training for gundog owners; certificates, including gundog training certificates and certificates of participation in training seminars and workshops; printed promotional materials such as flyers, postcards, banners, and posters for speaking engagements, seminars, and events.
Class 41
Education; providing of training; entertainment; sporting and cultural activities; animal training; coaching (training); dog shows; production of training videos; providing on-line electronic publications (not downloadable); training in dog handling; training consultancy; tuition in animal training; training of gundogs; training of dog handlers; training for gundog owners; educational services, namely conducting camps, workshops, classes, clinics and competitions for dogs and dog owners; information, advice and consultancy in relation to all the aforesaid services; public speaking and conference presentations on dog training, canine behavioural therapy and dog care management; organisation and conduct of seminars, webinars, and interactive online training courses; production and hosting of podcasts, live and streaming video content and interactive digital training modules relating to gundog training and behavioural therapy; organisation and management of dog sporting events, agility competitions and dog shows; event management services in connection with training, educational and cultural events for dog owners; and demonstration events of new dog training techniques, dog toy applications, and canine behavioural therapies.

The mark is proceeding because of distinctiveness acquired through use.
