FemHealth360

Class 9
Software and AI technology namely, downloadable and cloud-based software for tracking, analysing, and managing women’s health data; mobile applications for symptom logging, biometric analysis, and wearable device integration; artificial intelligence software for generating personalised health insights and self-advocacy recommendations; data analytics platforms for physiological and behavioural monitoring; software for use with smartwatches, glucose monitors, and health tracking sensors.
Class 10
Medical Devices and Sensor Integration namely, wearable health monitoring devices; diagnostic sensors for non-invasive biometrics; software-integrated devices for tracking heart rate, glucose, blood pressure, hormone levels and other health indicators; electronic devices used in conjunction with AI-powered health applications for early detection and wellness screening; non-invasive tools for women’s health monitoring.
Class 42
Technology and AI Services; Software as a Service (SaaS) for health tracking and symptom analysis; providing temporary use of non-downloadable software for AI-driven health insights; development of algorithms for health risk prediction; design and hosting of platforms integrating biometric, wearable and lifestyle data; scientific research services relating to women's health technology and data analytics.
Class 44
Health and wellness services; health and wellness monitoring services; providing information in the field of women’s health via AI-generated data analysis; personalised health advisory services based on self-reported and wearable data; non-diagnostic support for medical self-advocacy; consultation and education services relating to hormonal health, reproductive health, and chronic symptom tracking.
