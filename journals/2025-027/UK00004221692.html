COOLDOCK

Class 6
Common metals and their alloys; locks and lock goods, also including lock cases, lock cylinders, striking plates, keys and key blanks; doors, gates, barriers and windows; frames, hinges and handles; wheel clamps of metal or mainly of metal; door, gate and barrier closers (not electric); emergency and panic devices for doors and windows; fittings and mountings for doors, gates, barriers and windows as well as other door, gates, barriers and window hardware; wind breaks; loading dock levellers, bridges, ramps and plates; elevating loading dock platforms and lift tables; vehicle restraints; wheel chocks; turnstiles; parts for all the aforesaid goods being goods in class 6; all the aforesaid goods being of metal or predominantly of metal; small items of metal hardware.
Class 7
Machines and machine tools, including machines and machine tools for use in loading dock and terminal operations, loading and unloading machines, loading dock equipment; electric, pneumatic and hydraulic levellers for loading docks; elevating loading dock platforms and lift tables; electric and hydraulic vehicle restraints including restraints for securing vehicles to a loading dock; mechanical, electric, pneumatic and hydraulic drives and controls for doors and gates; electromechanical and electro-hydraulic door operators; loading devices and equipment; mobile and fixed loading platforms and ramps, in particular loading ramps; machine coupling and transmission components (except for land vehicles); electric and electronic operators for doors, gates, barriers and window; parts and components of the aforementioned goods.
Class 19
Loading dock bumpers, not of metal; non-metal loading dock seals; non-metal loading dock shelters; non-metal traffic barrier systems, including non-metal safety barrier systems for loading docks and other industrial gate applications; non-metal safety gates to secure the ledges of pallet drop areas on mezzanines and rack picking systems; non-metal bollards and boundary posts; non-metal machine guarding barriers; non-metal seal systems for loading docks and loading docks levellers; non-metal industrial curtain walls, including non-metal flexible curtain walls; non-metal mounting brackets; non-metal door frames and protective door frames; windbreaks panels or fences; parts and fittings for all the aforesaid goods.
Class 37
Installation, maintenance and repair of locks and locking mechanisms; installation, maintenance and repair of doors, gates, loading superstructures, loading platforms, window fittings and related control and operating systems; installation, maintenance and repair of access control, entrance and security apparatus, systems and installations and related products and devices.
