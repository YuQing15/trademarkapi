G.H. FINANCIALS

Class 35
Business analysis, research and information services; business administration, organization and management consultancy services relating to third party financial, commodity, or trade exchange memberships; providing business advisory services on third party exchange membership requirements; providing administrative business support for organizations and individuals seeking third party membership in financial, commodity, or trade exchanges; providing business assistance and consultancy services in relation to third party exchange membership applications, management, and compliance, including preparation of forms, letters, statements and documents; providing business advice and guidance on selecting appropriate exchange membership types based on a customer’s entity structure and trading behaviour; providing business assistance with third party exchange membership applications by acting as a sponsoring member and clearing member of an application; business advisory services relating to the operation and benefits of third party exchange memberships; compilation and systemization of information related to third party exchange membership programs for business purposes; providing management and administration services of third party exchange seat leases or memberships on a monthly or annual basis.
Class 42
Technical data analysis in the field of financial clearing, settlement, and market access; technological consultancy services in the field of financial risk assessment and trading systems configuration; IT consultancy, advisory and information services in the field of financial clearing, settlement, and market access; technical consultancy and support services relating to the integration of third-party software platforms for use in financial clearing, settlement, and market access; application service provider (ASP), namely, hosting and managing computer software applications of independent software vendors (ISVs); software reseller services; technological services for enabling connectivity with global derivatives exchanges; design and implementation of IT systems for financial market infrastructure; advisory services relating to the integration and configuration of financial technology systems; provision of technical support for third-party software used in financial transaction processing; technical support services relating to financial trading systems and infrastructure; co-location and proximity hosting services for hosting computer servers of others with high-speed network connectivity to financial exchanges; provision of support with the preparation of technical reports and documents for regulatory reporting.
