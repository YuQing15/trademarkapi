Retail or wholesale services and online retail or wholesale services connected with the sale of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages; Advertising consultancy and assistance relating to the beverages industry; Business administration consultancy and assistance in the field of beverages; Business management consultancy and assistance in the field of beverages; Sales, distribution and marketing of alcoholic beverages; Import-export services in the field of beverages; Business accelerator services, namely, providing mentorship, guidance, networking, and resources to brands, startups and entrepreneurs to help grow and develop business and accelerate innovation in the field of beverages; Business consultation and advisory services, namely, assisting brands, startups and entrepreneurs in business strategy, marketing, innovation and growth in the field of beverages; Organising events, trade fairs, competitions, shows and exhibitions for commercial or advertising purposes relating to the beverages industry; Customer loyalty services and customer club services for commercial, promotional and/or advertising purposes; Customer relationship management via chatbot; Providing incentive reward programs for customers through issuance and processing of loyalty points authenticated by non-fungible tokens (nfts) for on-line purchase of goods and services in the field of beverages; Blockchain tokens, digital tokens, downloadable digital files authenticated by non-fungible tokens, digital media, digital files, and digital assets in the field of beverages; Provision of an online marketplace for buyers and sellers of downloadable digital non-alcoholic and alcoholic beverages, authenticated by non-fungible tokens (nfts) .
