The marks consist of the stylised Chinese characters "仙逸堂", accompanied by the English word "XIANYITANG". A seal containing the Chinese character "堂" appears beside the Chinese characters. One mark is in colour, with the seal presented in red and the text in black; the other mark is in black and white.
