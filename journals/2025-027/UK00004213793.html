Class 9
Downloadable computer and mobile applications with multimedia entertainment content; software for use with artificial intelligence in relation with generating music, lyrics, images and multimedia content; virtual reality software used in the field of musical entertainment; downloadable digital images, musical and multimedia files authenticated by non-fungible tokens (NFTs) and dynamic non-fungible tokens (dNFTs) and other digital tokens based on blockchain technology; downloadable applications providing access to virtual, augmented or mixed reality environments used for virtual musical performances or events; downloadable applications for creating, collecting, trading, exchanging, purchasing, renting, sharing, displaying, posting, operating, using, storing, interacting with and/or managing virtual goods, namely computer programs featuring places, digital collectibles (music, music videos, film clips, live performances and motion picture films, concert tickets, toys, dolls, clothing, shoes, fashion accessories, eyewear and sunglasses) and virtual characters (digital animated and non-animated characters, avatars, skins and hairstyle) authenticated by non-fungible tokens (NFTs), dynamic non-fungible tokens (dNFTs) and other digital tokens based on blockchain technology.
Class 41
Entertainment; entertainment services, namely providing an online virtual environment for posting and trading digital collectibles featuring music, music videos, film clips, live performances and motion picture films; entertainment via virtual environments in which users can interact for amusement purposes, for leisure purposes or for musical entertainment purposes; multimedia production, other than for advertising purposes; production and publishing of digital collectibles in the nature of interactive media, images, videos, animations, photographs, music or visual effects; art exhibitions using virtual reality; rental of digital images, musical and multimedia files authenticated by non-fungible tokens (NFTs) and dynamic non-fungible tokens (dNFTs) and other digital tokens based on blockchain technology.
Class 42
Hosting multimedia entertainment content; design, updating, maintenance and rental of musical entertainment software; providing online non-downloadable computer software using artificial intelligence for generating images, lyrics, human speech and text, music, multimedia and digital entertainment content; providing online non-downloadable chatbot software for simulating conversations; software as a service (SAAS) featuring software using artificial intelligence for performing generative text artificial intelligence tasks and natural language processing artificial intelligence tasks and for writing content, summarizing text, document question-answering and simulating natural conversation in the field of musical entertainment; authentication, issuance and validation of digital certificates, user authentication services using technology for electronic trade transactions, user authentication using blockchain-based software technology for cryptocurrency transactions, creation of an online community in which registered users may participate in discussions, form virtual communities and engage in social networking in the field of digital assets, namely non-fungible tokens (NFTs), dynamic non-fungible tokens (dNFTs) and/or other digital tokens based on blockchain technology in relation with the musical entertainment; providing online non-downloadable software for the creation, production and editing of virtual goods (music, music videos, film clips, live performances and motion picture films, concert tickets, toys, dolls, clothing, shoes, fashion accessories, eyewear and sunglasses) and virtual characters (digital animated and non-animated characters, avatars, skins and hairstyle) authenticated by non-fungible tokens (NFTs), dynamic non-fungible tokens (dNFTs) and other digital tokens based on blockchain technology.
