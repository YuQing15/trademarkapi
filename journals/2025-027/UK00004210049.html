Comedy on Prescription

Class 41
Educational and training services relating to mental health and well-being through comedy, laughter and humour; organisation of workshops, classes and seminars in the field of mental wellness, personal development and social prescribing using comedy, laughter and humour; entertainment services, namely live comedy shows, immersive experiences and participatory performances for therapeutic or educational purposes; organisation of community comedy events and cultural activities to improve mental well-being; production and distribution of written, visual, audio and audiovisual materials—including films, podcasts, articles, scripts, digital publications and online content—featuring comedy, laughter and humour for health and wellness education; provision of online non-downloadable content, interactive tools and digital learning experiences using comedy, laughter and humour to support resilience, confidence and connection; Delivery of educational and entertainment workshops using comedy and humour to promote wellbeing, resilience, and social connection. Production of digital and immersive content formats for wellbeing purposes, featuring comedy and laughter. Organisation of live, digital or AI-enabled events focused on wellbeing and inclusion through comedy.
Class 44
Medical and therapeutic services for mental health and well-being incorporating comedy, laughter and humour; psychological therapy services using comedy, laughter or humour for therapeutic or wellness purposes; provision of structured health and wellness programmes using comedy-based interventions as preventative and therapeutic approaches; social prescribing services, namely medical referral of patients to comedy, immersive or community laughter-based activities to improve health outcomes; mental health counselling and wellbeing workshops facilitated by comedy industry professionals, including writers, performers, producers and trained facilitators; advisory and information services relating to the therapeutic, preventative and psychological benefits of comedy, laughter and humour in healthcare; Therapy services using structured humour-based techniques to support emotional recovery and mental health. Wellbeing services incorporating comedy and laughter to address social isolation and promote mental wellness.
