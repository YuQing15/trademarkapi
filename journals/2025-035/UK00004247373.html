LEGACY LOCKER

Class 9
Downloadable digital files and media featuring personal legacies, family histories, photographs, audio and video recordings, and memorial content; downloadable memory albums and digital scrapbooks; downloadable software for managing and storing personal estate information, family memories, digital wills, and encrypted access to legacy content; downloadable electronic publications in the field of estate planning, inheritance, and legacy preservation; video recordings including personal messages, life stories, recipes and anecdotes.
Class 35
Secure online storage of documents, financial and legal records, and digital assets; data processing and data management services in connection with estate planning, legacy distribution, and inheritance; administrative services relating to estate documentation, asset records, and beneficiary access; provision of annual reminders and administrative notifications relating to user accounts and stored legacy materials.
Class 36
Financial planning and advisory services; estate financial planning; financial consultancy relating to inheritance, succession, and digital asset transfer; trust and fiduciary financial services; investment advisory services in the field of estate and legacy management; insurance consultancy relating to life, legacy, or death benefit planning; real estate services including property valuation, property investment consultancy, and conveyancing; financial services relating to wills, trusts, and asset distribution; provision of financial documents for estate planning.
Class 41
Hosting of online memorials and personal legacy tributes; publication of non-downloadable multimedia content relating to family history, personal memories, and legacies; providing online journals, memory albums, and digital scrapbooks; curating digital content for use in family remembrance, storytelling, and heritage archiving; providing educational content and materials in the field of estate planning, digital memory preservation, and legacy documentation; streamed video recordings including video recordings of gift instructions, succession intentions, distribution of wealth, family stories and recipes.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) featuring an online platform for secure digital storage of documents, estate data, passwords, cryptocurrency, NFTs, and family memories; hosting and updating of encrypted software platforms; technical management of access control systems; encryption and authentication services; cloud storage and hosting of multimedia content including photographs, videos, and personal messages; design and operation of web-based tools for personal legacy preservation and memory curation; digital identity verification systems; cloud hosting of video messages and family content; Electronic data storage services.
Class 45
Legal services; legal services relating to wills, estate planning, probate, inheritance, trusts, and succession; legal consultancy in the field of bereavement, inheritance disputes, and estate distribution; preparation of wills; executor services; legal advice relating to fiduciary responsibilities, guardianship, and powers of attorney; verification of identity in the context of digital and physical asset distribution; legal services relating to property ownership and conveyancing; legal consultancy relating to digital asset management after death or incapacity; personal services relating to digital memorial planning and family legacy documentation.
