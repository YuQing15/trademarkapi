ENTERPRISE REMARKETING

Class 9
Downloadable computer software providing auction services, vehicle title transfer services; downloadable computer software providing automobile dealers with information regarding the sale and auction of vehicles, financial information and providing analysis of national and regionalized vehicle value data and vehicle value reports; downloadable computer software for obtaining reports about transaction information and facilitating sale and settlement of wholesale charges, purchases, title transfer services, and analysis of national and regionalized vehicle value data and vehicle value reports, and coordinating inspection services; downloadable computer software for analysing market information.
Class 35
Arranging and conducting auctions for used vehicles; providing automobile dealers with information via a global computer network regarding the sale and auction of vehicles; Providing information over the internet in the field of the vehicle industry and vehicle fleet management industry; administration of membership schemes.
Class 36
Financial evaluation and analysis; Providing financial information by electronic means; Provision of financial information relating to fuel expenditure.
Class 38
Transmission of digital information; Transmission of customer account information relating to vehicle sales via the internet, telephones and wireless devices; telecommunication services, namely, providing electronic message alerts via the internet, telephones and wireless devices; providing email notification alerts via the internet.
Class 42
Online platform services, website hosting, website maintenance and website compilation services, all relating to the sale of automobiles; Providing a website featuring temporary use of an online non-downloadable password-protected software that allows automobile dealers to perform electronic business transactions in an online auction, namely, for accessing financial information, for obtaining online reports about transaction information and for facilitating sale and settlement of wholesale charges, of purchases and of title transfer services.
