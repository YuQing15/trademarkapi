Lewis Trauma Map

Class 41
Provision of education, training, and instruction via prerecorded and live-streamed video; provision of downloadable and non-downloadable educational videos, electronic publications, manuals, and workbooks; creation and delivery of online courses, classes, workshops, seminars, and lectures; continuing professional development (CPD) training; provision of education and training to the public; educational consultancy; publishing of electronic and printed materials; production and distribution of prerecorded and live-streamed video-based educational content in the fields of mental health, psychology, counselling, psychotherapy, trauma recovery, nervous system regulation, and personal development.
Class 44
Psychological services; psychotherapy services; counselling services; trauma therapy services; provision of psychological assessment, diagnosis, treatment, rehabilitation, and aftercare; provision of psychotherapy, counselling, and trauma recovery services incorporating the Lewis Trauma Map method; consultancy and information services relating to mental health, psychological wellbeing, nervous system regulation, and trauma recovery; delivery of therapeutic and psychoeducational services in person, by telephone, and online; professional supervision, mentoring, and training of therapists in the application of the Lewis Trauma Map; advisory and consultancy services relating to the use, adaptation, and authorised delivery of the Lewis Trauma Map in therapeutic and educational practice.
