Class 36
Charitable fundraising; financial grant making; management and monitoring of charitable funds; advisory, consultancy and information services in relation to all the aforesaid.
Class 41
Education and training services including in relation to healthcare and hospice care; organising of sporting, cultural and entertainment events; organising of fundraising events; arranging live music events and performances; provision of informational guides and leaflets; publishing including online publishing; provision of non-downloadable electronic publications; advisory, consultancy and information services in relation to all the aforesaid.
Class 43
Providing temporary accommodation and day care services for hospice patients and residents; providing food and drink catering services to patients, residents, staff and visitors; provision of catering services for charitable fundraising purposes; provision of temporary room hire; advisory, consultancy and information services in relation to all the aforesaid.

Class 45
Spiritual and pastoral guidance and counselling services including such services for terminally ill people and their families; providing bereavement support and counselling; advisory, consultancy and information services in relation to all the aforesaid.
