Class 35
Provision of an online marketplace for buyers and sellers of goods and services; classified advertising services; providing an online commercial information directory; provision of commercial listings and advertisements via on-line searchable databases, including for auto parts and accessories for sale by third parties; compilation of information into computer databases relating to locating products, describing products, rating products, pricing products and comparing prices of products, including for automobile parts and accessories; providing consumer product information; retail, wholesale, import and export agency services in relation to auto parts and auto accessories; advertising, marketing and promotion services; customer data management services; customer relationship management services; online ordering services; administrative processing of purchase orders; business assistance, management and administrative services; inventory management services; database management; compiling information into computer databases.
Class 38
Provision of access to an electronic marketplace [portal] on computer networks; providing access to e-commerce platforms on the internet; providing access to online databases; providing access to portals on the internet; providing access to information on the internet; providing access to computer, electronic and online databases; providing an online forum for the transmission of messages among computer users concerning the use, consumer product information, product pricing, and product characterisations for auto parts and accessories.
Class 42
Provision of online non-downloadable software, software as a service (SaaS), platform as a service (PaaS), and application software in the fields of ecommerce, retail, wholesale, inventory management, logistics, product information, product availability, product compatibility, product fitment, and software design and development; provision of online non-downloadable software, software as a service (SaaS), platform as a service (PaaS), and application software to allow users to perform electronic business transactions via a global computer network, for database management, sales and customer tracking and management, and inventory management; provision of online non-downloadable software, software as a service (SaaS), platform as a service (PaaS), and application software to allow users to create hosted e-commerce stores to sell goods and services online, to manage the content of their e-commerce website, and to manage the inventory of their online stores; provision of online non-downloadable software, software as a service (SaaS), platform as a service (PaaS), and application software for use in the auto parts and auto accessories industry, including for auto parts and auto accessories cataloguing, cataloguing searching, product pricing, and product information; provision of online non-downloadable software, software as a service (SaaS), platform as a service (PaaS), and application software for use in database management, including in relation to auto parts and auto accessories information, fitment data, and vehicle information; application service provider services that provides non downloadable application programming interface (API) software; design, creation, hosting and maintenance of software and websites; design, creation, hosting and maintenance of e-commerce websites; advice, consultancy, and information services relating to the aforesaid.
