A combined trademark is applied for, which is an original composition consisting of a pictorial and verbal elements. The verbal element "INSULALABS" [insulalabs] is an inscription in Latin in a stylized (bold italic) font. The verbal element is made in capital (uppercase) letters and is placed in 2 lines: part of the verbal element "INSULA" is placed in the upper row, part of the verbal element "LABS" is placed below it in the lower row. In front of the verbal element is a pictorial element, which is a filled circle, which is surrounded on the right side by an arc that starts from the top, bends around the circle on the right side, ends at the bottom, turning into a straight line to the left. Thus, the trademark has a complete appearance, distinctiveness, and is well remembered and perceived by the consumer.
