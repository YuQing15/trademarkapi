Class 9
Software for organizing, monitoring, controlling or testing electrical devices in power supply grids, in power supply systems, and of power supply components; software for streamlining and managing workflows of organizing, monitoring, controlling or testing electrical devices in power supply grids or power supply systems; software for organizing, monitoring, controlling or testing data of electrical devices, in particular electrical devices in power supply grids, power supply systems or power supply components, including for example transducer or transformer, current or voltage converters; software for tracking performance and condition of electrical devices in power supply grids, power supply systems, and power supply components for fault detection and diagnostics; software for scheduling inspections and maintenance of electrical devices in power supply grids, power supply systems or power supply components; software for processing and analyzing data including predictive analysis, fault analysis, energy consumption evaluation, and efficiency assessment of electrical devices in power supply grids, power supply systems, and power supply components; software for data acquisition and aggregation of operational data and historical performance databases of electrical devices in power supply grids, power supply systems, and power supply components; software for data documentation, automated reporting, incident and event logging, and performance visualization of electrical devices in power supply grids, power supply systems, and power supply components; software employing data models for data integration and data consistency across electronic devices, software platforms, and computer programs used in the commissioning and maintenance of electrical electric devices in power supply grids, power supply systems, and power supply components; software for enabling interoperability between different formats and protocols of electrical devices in power supply grids, power supply systems, and power supply components; the aforesaid software in particular provided as cloud-based or downloadable software.
