The trademark consists of the word mark “KORAL SECRETS” presented in a modern, clean, and minimalist typeface. The word “KORAL” is written in bold, uppercase letters with a strong and contemporary sans-serif font, symbolizing elegance and confidence. Positioned directly below, the word “SECRETS” appears in smaller uppercase letters, spaced to create a refined and luxurious aesthetic.

The mark contains the colours black and almond.
