The applied-for mark consists of the words "ĐÔNG i". Below is the words "96 VỊ THUỐC BẮC". Above is a stylized crane bird and the stylized lunar eclipse in white, red brown as shown.

The mark contains the colours red brown, white and black.

Red brown: the stylized lunar eclipse; the stylized crane bird; the stylized "i"; white: the stylized lunar eclipse; the stylized body of crane bird; black: the text "ĐÔNG" and "96 VỊ THUỐC BẮC".
