Class 9
Downloadable computer software for prescribing, ordering, filling, and managing prescriptions; downloadable computer software for monitoring usage, dosage and directions for use and refill of prescriptions for patients, physicians and health care facilities; downloadable computer software that enables users to access information and data in the fields of health, healthcare and insurance health plans and for arranging home delivery of prescriptions; downloadable computer software for building and maintaining profiles related to a user's prescription and over-the-counter medications, reviewing details on those medications, receiving automated warnings of potential adverse interactions of newly submitted medications, identifying medications, and adding to a user's profile.
Class 35
Retail pharmacy services as well as mail order services in relation to pharmaceuticals; business administration of medication reimbursement programs and services; business administration of pharmacy reimbursement programs and services; administering a discount program to allow participants to obtain discounts on the cost of prescription drugs and over the counter medications through use of a membership card; online retail ordering services in relation to prescriptions by sms.
Class 38
Telecommunications services, namely, transferring medical data, pharmaceutical data and prescription information by sms; telecommunication services, namely, electronic transmission of data for others in the nature of communicating and routing prescription information for use by pharmacies, medical professionals, and insurance providers in prescribing, ordering, filling, and managing prescriptions by sms.
Class 39
Transport services, namely, transport of prescriptions; transport and delivery of goods; transport and delivery of goods, namely, transport and delivery of prescriptions; pharmacy home delivery services, namely, delivery of pharmaceuticals.
