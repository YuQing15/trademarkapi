Alba Care Group

Class 41
Provision of education and learning services to children and young persons in residential care and supported living; delivery of alternative education programmes for individuals with special educational needs and disabilities (SEND), including emotional, behavioural and mental health needs; education provision for pupils with education, health and care plans (EHCPs); operation of independent or specialist educational settings; therapeutic education and learning support for children and care leavers unable to access mainstream schooling; reintegration education services; enrichment, life skills training, and preparation for further education, training, or employment (ETE).
Class 44
Provision of regulated residential care and supported living services for children and young persons under the age of 18, as well as for care leavers and individuals aged 18 to 25 with continuing support needs following children’s social care involvement; provision of care in settings regulated by Ofsted or equivalent.
Class 45
Provision of safeguarding services for children and young persons under the age of 18, as well as for care leavers and individuals aged 18 to 25 with ongoing vulnerabilities following children’s social care involvement; supervision, advocacy, and risk management services in support of children looked after, care-experienced individuals, and those subject to complex care plans;  support in relation to Deprivation of Liberty Safeguards (DoLS) and non-medical protective interventions; personal and social support services delivered within regulated care settings, including support for those affected by child sexual exploitation (CSE), child criminal exploitation (CCE), trauma, abuse, or significant safeguarding concerns; guidance and monitoring of safeguarding procedures in line with statutory frameworks and regulatory obligations.
