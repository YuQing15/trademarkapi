Taste Intelligence

Class 9
Downloadable software and artificial intelligence systems for generating and delivering personalised content, product recommendations, and preference-based matches in the fields of beauty, fragrance, fashion, lifestyle, and related sectors; downloadable software for operating AI-powered discovery engines, virtual assistants, recommendation systems, and curated commerce platforms; including tools for preference modeling, search optimization, and interactive personalization.
Class 35
Retail services, including online and offline sale of beauty, fragrance, fashion, lifestyle products and services, and related sectors; operation of digital marketplaces and curated commerce platforms; provision of consumer product information, recommendations, and personalised shopping experiences; merchandising, content curation, and product discovery tools; marketing and promotional services via digital, social and offline media channels; business consultancy in the fields of AI-powered personalization and innovative retail models.
Class 38
Streaming and transmission of personalised content, recommendations, and multimedia experiences; digital communication and messaging services; broadcasting of editorial content and product information via digital and traditional media channels; transmission of data for delivering personalised experiences and recommendations; provision of communication platforms and interactive services; telecommunications services for content distribution and community engagement.
Class 41
Publishing and distribution of editorial content, preference-based guides, and creator content via digital and interactive formats; delivery of personalised content, cultural commentary, and product recommendations generated by artificial intelligence or human contributors; provision of online non-downloadable content including articles, video, audio, and interactive media in the fields of beauty, fragrance, fashion, lifestyle, and related sectors; development and syndication of multimedia content across websites, applications, assistants, and social platforms; including AI-powered editorial tools and content personalization engines, and collaborative publishing systems.

Class 44
Provision of personalised fragrance, beauty, and wellness consultation services powered by artificial intelligence; advisory services relating to individual taste and sensory preferences in cosmetics, skincare, self-care, and personal grooming; digital and AI-enabled tools for assessing personal needs and suggesting tailored products.
Class 45
