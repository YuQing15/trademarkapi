Mark 1

Vibe retail

Mark 2

Vibe retailing

Class 9
Computer software and downloadable applications featuring artificial intelligence (AI) for facilitating dynamic interactions through voice and text assistants to enhance retail experiences; interactive software enabling users to navigate retail offerings by providing real-time answers, guidance, and personalized product recommendations; user interface software components specifically designed to augment and improve retail experiences through AI-driven dialogues and interactions; downloadable virtual assistants capable of retrieving, presenting, and organizing product information, retail listings, and retailer details; artificial intelligence software enabling dynamic conversation flows between users and retail services; computer software employing voice recognition, natural language processing, and machine learning technologies to support consumer decision-making within retail environments; downloadable software providing immersive and personalized interactions between consumers, product databases, and retail platforms.
Class 35
Providing online consumer product and retailer information through interactive AI software interfaces; advertising, marketing, and promotional services utilizing embedded AI conversational interactions designed to deliver personalized retail experiences and enhance consumer engagement within online marketplaces; retail consultancy services leveraging AI-driven virtual assistants and conversational interfaces to assist consumers in navigating, evaluating, selecting, and purchasing products across digital retail environments.
