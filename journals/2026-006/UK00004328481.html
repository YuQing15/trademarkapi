ON A MISSION TO END PATIENT FLUSTRATION

Class 9
Downloadable computer software for on-line, patient-initiated, patient authorized, medical profile and medical record aggregation, reconciliation, and transmission services designed to access relevant medical records storage sources, aggregate and reconcile a patient medical profile from such records, share the patient's medical records and health-related information at the direction of the patient, and allow the patient to supplement medical profile, medical record, and health related information; downloadable computer software used for treatment management, payment management, appointment scheduling, and medical record access in the health care field; downloadable computer software for payment management and claim tracking in the field of health care; downloadable computer software for information management, claims eligibility and processing, document management, case management, contract management, workers compensation, occupational health, and employee health; downloadable computer software used for analytical and decision support and designed to enhance patient care and lower health care costs.
Class 35
Medical claims management services, namely, receiving, data entering, and re-pricing of transactions that are originated by physicians, hospitals, and ancillary medical care providers; healthcare management services provided to patients, namely, treatment management, information management, medical record management, payment management, appointment scheduling, and medical record access in the healthcare field; collecting and analyzing health care claims data and medical information for patients for the purpose of enhancing patient care and lowering health care costs.
Class 36
Financial analysis for patients in the field of healthcare; financial management services for patients in the field of healthcare; financial research for patients in the field of healthcare.
Class 42
