Class 9
Downloadable software utilizing artificial intelligence for use in healthcare operations, namely for automating the hiring, screening, credentialing, license verification, and scheduling of medical staff; downloadable software for prescreening patients, coordinating care inquiries, setting and managing appointments, verifying insurance and eligibility, conducting automated follow-up, and collecting patient intake data; downloadable software in the nature of AI chatbots and AI Voice Agents for healthcare websites and platforms to assist with 24/7 virtual intake and staff recruitment.
Class 35
Recruitment and placement of personnel in the healthcare industry, assisted by artificial intelligence.
Class 42
Software as a Service (SaaS) services using artificial intelligence for healthcare organizations to automate and optimize admissions, hiring, staffing coordination, and patient intake workflows; providing temporary use of non-downloadable software for automating caregiver and clinician recruitment, interview scheduling, patient qualification, license verification, appointment reminders, and care coordination; provision of computer software for AI-driven virtual agents for prescreening care inquiries, handling website engagement, and enabling 24/7 interaction between healthcare providers and job seekers or patient families.
