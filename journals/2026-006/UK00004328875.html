Permanent Grade

Class 6
Prefabricated, modular, portable and/or relocatable building units and structures of metal; modular portable units for constructing operating theatres, surgical hubs, wards, intensive care units, and other healthcare facilities; parts and fittings for all the aforesaid goods of metal; ramps and bridging elements, loading ramps, access ramps, walkways and gangplanks of metal; parts and fittings therefor; modular structural frames and connectors of metal.
Class 11
Sanitary installations; sanitary and washroom units; clean air and ultraclean ventilation installations; HVAC installations and components; lighting installations; parts and fittings for the aforesaid goods; all for use in modular or relocatable healthcare buildings including operating theatres; air treatment, purification and filtration apparatus; cleanroom lighting and controlled environment fixtures; sterilization equipment (to the extent classifiable in Class 11); parts and fittings for all the aforesaid goods.
Class 19
Prefabricated, modular, portable and/or relocatable building units and structures not of metal; modular portable units for constructing operating theatres, surgical hubs, wards, intensive care units, and other healthcare facilities; parts and fittings for all the aforesaid goods not of metal; ramps and bridging elements, loading ramps, access ramps, walkways and gangplanks not of metal; cladding, wall and floor panels not of metal; acoustic and hygiene wall and ceiling panels not of metal; parts and fittings therefor.
Class 37
Construction, installation and assembly of modular, portable and/or relocatable buildings; construction, installation and assembly of operating theatres, surgical hubs, wards, diagnostic suites and other healthcare facilities; installation of medical equipment; installation of clean air and ultraclean ventilation systems, HVAC systems and electrical and data systems; maintenance, repair, refurbishment, reconfiguration and relocation of modular and/or relocatable buildings; construction project management; advisory and consultancy relating to construction and installation of modular healthcare facilities; cleaning, servicing and sanitation of modular buildings; hire of construction apparatus, instruments, machines and tools.
Class 40
Custom manufacture and fabrication of prefabricated and modular buildings; custom manufacture of modular operating theatres, surgical hubs, wards and diagnostic facilities; Treatment of air and environmental control media for clinical environments; refurbishment services for modular buildings.
Class 42
Architectural and engineering design services; design and development of modular and relocatable buildings; architectural project management; engineering project management; design of operating theatres, surgical hubs, diagnostic suites, wards and other healthcare facilities; design of HVAC and ultraclean ventilation systems for clinical environments; consultancy relating to compliance with healthcare building and technical standards; Testing, verification and commissioning services for modular healthcare buildings; consultancy on compliance with healthcare standards and guidance; environmental design to support net zero targets and BREEAM/Passivhaus principles as applicable to modular healthcare facilities.
Class 43
Rental and hire of temporary accommodation; rental and hire of prefabricated, modular, portable and/or relocatable buildings; rental and hire of modular operating theatres, surgical hubs, wards and diagnostic facilities; rental and hire of furniture for healthcare buildings.
Class 44
Rental and hire of sanitation and washroom facilities; consulting services relating to the layout and set up of patient care environments within modular healthcare facilities; advisory services relating to infection prevention and control as it pertains to room layouts and finishes within modular healthcare facilities; consultancy on clinical workflows and patient pathways within modular operating theatres and surgical hubs.
