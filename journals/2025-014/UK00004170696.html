Digital Vinyl

Class 9
Downloadable digital music files, albums, compilations, and recordings provided via a digital application and other electronic communications networks; downloadable audio recordings in high-resolution and lossless formats for enhanced sound quality; downloadable digital album booklets, liner notes, and cover artwork accompanying music releases accessible through a digital application; electronic publications related to music, including track credits, production details, and release information available via a digital application.
Class 35
Online retail store services provided via a digital application connected with the sale of downloadable digital music files, downloadable albums and compilations in high-quality audio formats; operation of an online marketplace for independent artists, record labels, and rights holders to distribute and sell digital music files; marketing and promotional services for musicians and digital music releases, including editorial features and artist profiles.
Class 41
Provision of digital music distribution services via a digital application; providing online access to non-downloadable music recordings in high-quality audio formats; music library services, namely the curation and organization of digital music collections for user access and streaming; providing digital album booklets, cover artwork, and supporting information for recorded music via a digital application; offering digital music releases in formats optimized for high-fidelity playback via a digital application.
