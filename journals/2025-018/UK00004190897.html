Class 9

Class 35
Retail services, online retail services, wholesale services, mail order services and temporary pop-up store services relating to dietary and nutritional supplements, protein supplements, protein powders, vitamin and mineral preparations, nutraceuticals, health foods, beverages and drink mixes for wellness purposes, fitness and performance supplements, wearable activity trackers, downloadable digital content including software, mobile applications, videos, guides, and publications in the fields of health, nutrition, wellness and fitness; retail services relating to health tracking devices, fitness equipment, digital health accessories, sportswear, headwear, footwear, gym bags, water bottles, shaker bottles, and other fitness-related merchandise; provision of an online marketplace for buyers and sellers of health, fitness and wellness-related products and services, including access to personal trainers, nutritionists, and wellness professionals; advertising, promotional and marketing services for the promotion of goods and services in the fields of health, fitness and nutrition; loyalty, incentive and bonus program services; dissemination of advertising via online platforms and messaging services; business advisory, information and consultancy services relating to the aforesaid.
Class 38
Telecommunication services, namely the electronic transmission of text, voice, audio, video, and image data via chatbots and messaging networks; providing messaging channels for users to communicate with an AI health coach; transmission of digital files and data through mobile messaging applications; providing user access to global communication networks for interacting with a health and nutrition chatbot.
Class 42
Software as a Service (SaaS) services featuring software for tracking nutrition, fitness and health data and for providing personalised health coaching; Platform as a Service (PaaS) featuring computer software platforms for health and nutrition monitoring; cloud computing services providing data analytics and data visualisation dashboards for users’ health metrics; providing temporary use of online non-downloadable chatbot software for interacting with users via messaging platforms; providing use of online non-downloadable software for enabling the sharing and display of health, wellness and nutrition data between users and healthcare providers, coaches, or personal trainers; electronic data storage and hosting of databases in the field of health and wellness; design and development of computer software and AI algorithms for health tracking and coaching.
Class 44
Providing information and advice in the fields of nutrition, health, wellness and fitness via a website and via chat-based communication platforms; nutrition counselling and weight management consulting provided through an AI chatbot and online dashboard; providing personalized health and wellness coaching (non-downloadable) including diet planning and fitness guidance; providing health information to users by electronic means, including via mobile messaging applications; wellness monitoring services and lifestyle counselling for health improvement; facilitating access to personal trainers, nutritionists, and healthcare professionals for wellness advice and coaching.
