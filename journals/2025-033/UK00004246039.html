Class 35
Organization and management of customer loyalty programs; Administration of loyalty programs featuring discounts or incentives; Customer loyalty services for commercial, promotional, and advertising purposes; administration of frequent flyer programs enabling redemption of points, miles, or other benefits; Promoting the goods and services of others through incentive award programs, in which points are awarded for purchases from participating vendors and may be redeemed for merchandise, travel, lodging, or dining; Promoting the goods and services of others by providing consumer information and distributing benefit cards associated with point accumulation systems, vouchers, or other value based mechanisms offering rewards, discounts, or additional benefits; Advertising services, namely providing advertising space in periodicals; Business administration services in airports.
