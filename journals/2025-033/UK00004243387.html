Class 38
Providing electronic messaging services enabling communication between musicians, DJs, and fans via mobile and web platforms; transmission of audio clips, short video previews, and live broadcast updates over a global computer network; hosting interactive messaging and broadcast features for artists to send real-time status updates and announcements to fans; facilitating artist-to-artist direct messaging and fan-to-artist communication through controlled premium messaging systems; providing platforms for RSVP notifications, event alerts, and location-based show discovery communications; enabling digital communication services related to fan referrals, donations, and real-time show updates; offering online chat, broadcast, and notification services tailored to live music events and fan engagement.
Class 41
Organizing, promoting, and hosting live and virtual music performances and shows featuring unsigned musicians and DJs; providing online platforms and tools for musicians and DJs to create, publish, and manage event listings, ticketing, and RSVPs; facilitating fan engagement experiences including early access to shows, exclusive content, and interactive broadcast updates; offering fan membership programs with premium perks such as backstage access, direct artist messaging, and exclusive merchandise offers; hosting educational and entertainment content related to music performances, artist discovery, and fan community building; providing platforms for music streaming previews, digital drops, and promotion of artist discographies; managing loyalty and referral programs to reward fans and musicians for participation and promotion of events. .
