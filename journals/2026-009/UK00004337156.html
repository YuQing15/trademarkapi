MEMBRANE.SOCIAL

Class 9
Downloadable computer software and mobile applications for enabling users to participate in social networking, content sharing, digital interactions, messaging, chats, video and audio sharing, feeds, surveys, feedback collection, and attention-based exchanges; downloadable software for collecting, generating, validating, and analysing human responses, preferences, and behavioural signals; downloadable software for campaign management, response collection, analytics, reporting, and decision support; downloadable software for enabling human-in-the-loop input for artificial intelligence, machine learning, and automated decision-making systems; downloadable software for managing user engagement and reward-based interactions.
Class 35
Advertising services; market research and survey services; conducting surveys and collecting human responses and behavioural data for business, commercial, and strategic purposes, including optimisation and evaluation of digital systems; business data analysis relating to advertising campaigns, messaging, decision-making, and survey responses; administration and management of advertising, research, and insight-generation campaigns.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) featuring software for creating, hosting, configuring, delivering, validating, measuring, and analysing social networking features, user-generated content, video and audio content, messaging, chats, feeds, digital interactions, surveys, and campaigns; providing online non-downloadable software for collecting and analysing human responses, preferences, and behavioural signals; providing online non-downloadable software for managing user engagement, response data, analytics, and content interactions; hosting of digital platforms for social networking, messaging-based, content-sharing, and attention-based interactions between users and third parties for informational, research, advertising, and system-training purposes; providing human-in-the-loop feedback, preference data, and calibration signals for use in artificial intelligence, machine learning, automated, and decision-support systems.
