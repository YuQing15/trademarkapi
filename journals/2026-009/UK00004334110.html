SwapTix

Class 9
Computer software for enabling peer-to-peer ticket exchange and resale; application software for listing, selling, purchasing, reserving and booking tickets for entertainment, sporting and cultural events via an online platform; downloadable mobile applications for accessing and managing ticket marketplaces; downloadable software platforms for facilitating ticket exchanges and sales; software for creating, issuing, managing, transferring and verifying electronic tickets, including QR code-based tickets, whether issued directly by the platform or in partnership with event organisers or ticket vendors; authentication software for verifying the legitimacy of event tickets; software for facilitating secure online payment and validation of ticket transactions; downloadable electronic databases and publications relating to ticket sales, ticket management and user transactions.
Class 35
Provision of an online marketplace for the purchase, sale, exchange and resale of tickets for entertainment, sporting and cultural events; business management, administration and organisation services relating to ticket sales, exchanges and bookings carried out via an online platform; facilitating ticket sales, bookings, exchanges and transfers through an online platform or marketplace; advertising, marketing and promotional services related to event ticketing and ticket exchange platforms; arranging and conducting promotional competitions, prize draws and giveaways relating to event ticketing; provision of consumer information relating to event dates, venues, and ticket availability; market analysis and reporting relating to event ticket sales; information, consultancy and advisory services relating to the aforesaid services.
Class 41
Providing an online platform for the reservation, booking, exchange and management of tickets for entertainment, sporting and cultural events; facilitating ticket reservations, bookings and sales via an online marketplace; enabling users to purchase, reserve, redeem, transfer, or manage tickets for live events through an online platform; information, advisory and consultancy services in connection with online ticketing platforms and event attendance.
Class 42
Providing temporary use of online non-downloadable software for peer-to-peer ticket exchange, resale, booking, and management; design and development of computer software and online platforms for ticket sales, exchanges and electronic ticketing; design and hosting of websites for ticket marketplace services; provision of software as a service (SaaS) for facilitating ticket sales, exchanges, bookings, and validation; technological services in the field of data storage, encryption and security of ticket transactions; integration of ticketing software with third-party payment providers and event management platforms; technical support, consultancy and advisory services in connection with the aforesaid services.
