retail services in relation to catering equipment, kitchen equipment, bar equipment, cookware, bakeware, tableware, glassware, barware, cutlery, crockery, kitchen utensils, chef’s tools; retail services in relation to food and drink packaging, takeaway and delivery containers, disposable packaging and cutlery, food and drink carriers and containers, and labels for takeaway goods; retail services in relation to toiletries, cleaning preparations, articles for cleaning purposes; retail services in relation to paper hygiene products, napkins, hand towels, tissue roll, tissues, toilet roll, paper pads, and pens; retail services in relation to workwear, protective clothing, chef’s clothing, waitstaff uniforms, aprons, tunics, hats, caps, hair nets, waistcoats, ties, t-shirts, shoes, clogs, and oven and freezer gloves and mitts; retail services in relation to storage containers and food safe containers; retail services in relation to cocktail equipment, wine coolers, ice buckets and tools, measurers and pourers, glass and bottle skips, beer mats, drip trays, floor mats, bar games, and ashtrays; retail services in relation to signage, labelling products, food safety identification items, thermometers, protective and safety equipment, personal protective equipment namely clothing, helmets, goggles, face masks, gloves and coveralls, first aid kits, fire safety equipment; retail services in relation to outdoor tables and chairs, high chairs, booster seats, baby changing units, table lamps, candles and candle holders, blankets, food presentation stands, food service equipment, food storage equipment, cake displays, trays, trolleys, tills and till rolls, menu holders, storage lockers, money counters, pest control equipment, refrigerators, freezers and chillers, ice machines, food preparation benches, kitchen sinks and parts and fittings therefor;
