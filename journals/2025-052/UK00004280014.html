Integriti Systems

Class 9
Downloadable and cloud-based software; all relating to business process mapping, workflow optimisation for service professionals, client-journey management, document preparation and document automation for professional services, training delivery and performance tracking, internal operations support for service-based businesses, estate-planning workflow and form-filling assistance; all of the aforesaid being for use within professional service firms; none of the aforesaid relating to security systems, alarms, access control, monitoring, surveillance, logistics, warehousing, robotics, supply-chain management, telecommunications, networking, infrastructure management, cybersecurity, encryption, identity-verification, authentication, industrial systems, engineering systems, or consumer-focused applications outside professional services.
Class 35
Business consultancy; business process analysis; all relating to professional service firms, estate planning consultants, legal service providers, financial advisers, accountants, mortgage brokers; none of the aforesaid services relating to logistics, warehousing, manufacturing, industrial operations, recruitment, staffing, HR outsourcing, or the operation or management of security systems.
Class 42
Software as a Service (SaaS); design and development of artificial intelligence software; provision of temporary use of online non-downloadable software for business workflow management, all relating to workflow automation for professional service firms, client-journey optimisation, online delivery of business training and certification, internal operational systems for estate-planning and advisory firms, process documentation and systems architecture for service-based businesses; none of the aforesaid services relating to security, surveillance, alarm, access-control or monitoring systems, robotics, industrial automation, warehouse automation or supply-chain management systems, telecommunications software or platforms, cybersecurity, encryption, identity-verification or authentication services, or development of systems for physical hardware or physical infrastructure.
