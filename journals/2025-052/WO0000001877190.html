Office of origin: Italy

Date of International Registration:13 May 2025
Date of designation in the UK: 13 May 2025

The trademark consists of an imprint depicting the wording IDEA SHOPPING in fancy characters, the two portions being placed one over the other, flanked on the left by a letter I in fancy characters which is folded back onto itself so as to trace a substantially quadrilateral figure evoking the shape of a stylized shopping bag, there being on the upper side of this figure a semicircle to evoke the bag's handle and, inside it, a letter S in fancy characters.
