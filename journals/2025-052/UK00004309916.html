THE BLUE PULSE

Class 9
Software for generating, recording, managing, verifying, displaying and storing data for sustainability and impact use cases, including but not limited to non-tradable digital impact certificates; downloadable sustainability and impact data packs; software systems for cryptographic verification, evidence authentication, impact tracking, data provenance and structured ESG data capture and reporting, including data relating to marine, coastal and ocean-based impact projects, blue economy initiatives, nature-based solutions and alignment with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), for use by organisations, project owners, sponsors and corporate stakeholders in relation to sustainability, CSR and impact accountability.
Class 35
Business consultancy and advisory services relating to environmental, social and governance (ESG) strategy, sustainability frameworks, corporate social responsibility (CSR) programmes and impact accountability; business support services for impact documentation, sponsor reporting and ESG disclosures; business analysis, benchmarking and information services relating to sustainability performance, marine, coastal and ocean-based impact projects, blue economy initiatives, nature-based solutions, verified impact outcomes and organisational accountability; business management assistance for organisations, project owners and sponsors in relation to impact governance, alignment with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and verified impact reporting.
Class 41
Education, training and capacity-building services relating to sustainability, environmental, social and governance (ESG) reporting, impact verification and accountability; provision of workshops, tutorials, seminars and educational programmes relating to marine, coastal and ocean-based impact projects, blue economy initiatives, nature-based solutions, project compliance and environmental stewardship; education and training relating to alignment with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and verified impact reporting.
Class 42
Software as a Service (SaaS) featuring software for sustainability and impact verification, ESG data processing, digital evidence management, cryptographic hashing, issuance and management of non-tradable digital impact records, sustainability analytics, data provenance and automated verification of environmental and social claims; provision of online non-downloadable software platforms for collecting, processing, validating and reporting environmental and social impact data, including data relating to marine, coastal and ocean-based impact projects, blue economy initiatives, nature-based solutions and alignment with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), for use by organisations, project owners, sponsors and corporate stakeholders.
Class 45
Verification, validation and authentication services relating to environmental, social and sustainability impact; verification of environmental and social claims; auditing and authentication of impact data, evidence and documentation relating to marine, coastal and ocean-based impact projects, blue economy initiatives and nature-based solutions; issuance of non-tradable verified impact certificates and records confirming verified actions and outcomes; provision of compliance verification services relating to ESG disclosures, CSR reporting, sustainability accountability and alignment with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs); creation and maintenance of tamper-evident audit trails supporting verified impact claims.
