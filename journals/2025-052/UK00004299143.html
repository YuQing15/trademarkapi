Class 35
Charitable services namely organising and developing programmes that aim to improve the lives of young people; public advocacy and providing information to promote awareness on the subjects of social welfare and the need for reform of the care system in relation to children; promoting public awareness of reform and alternatives to deprivation of liberty orders.
Class 36
Charitable fundraising services relating to children’s social care.
Class 41
Education services (including assessments and tutorials) related to children with complex needs within the social care system including; providing programmes featuring prevocational training, life skills training, parenting skills training, education and literacy training, and vocational training; providing vocational guidanceto high-risk young people seeking employment; organisation of group seminars; workplace training; presentations; online publications and teaching aids; providing education and care-related accommodation.
Class 44
Mental health services related to children with complex needs within the social care system; providing integrated mental health support to professionals, families and children and providing professional supervision for staff working with children.
Class 45
Youth counselling services, including providing life skills counselling and parenting skills counselling concerning intra-family relationships to high-risk young people; case management services, including coordinating personal and social services in the nature of employment counselling, life skills counselling, and parenting skills; lobbying services aimed at promoting the interests of children in social care.
