UK00004285200 28 October 2025 (9,35,36,38,41,42)

The mark consists of an abstract "X" shape formed by a series of interlaced contour lines. The contour shape features a vertical gradient transitioning from the colour Pantone 2995 C applied to the lower parts to the colour Pantone 2726 C applied to the upper parts. The overall design maintains a cool blue to blue-purple tone with clean white line details throughout the contour structure.
