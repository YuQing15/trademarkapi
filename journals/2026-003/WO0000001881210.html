Class 9
Software, in particular application software, cloud-based software, middleware, or system software, for optimization of the operation of plants for industrial gas production, in particular for the production of oxygen, nitrogen, noble gases, hydrogen, carbon monoxide, carbon dioxide, olefins, liquified natural gas, in particular for real-time optimization of operation; software for analyzing the operation of plants for industrial gas production, in particular for remote real-time analysis of the plant using measured operational data of the plant; software for simulating the operational status of plants for industrial gas production using measured or calculated operational data for optimizing the operation of plants, in particular using digital twins; software for monitoring the efficiency, safety, operational stability, quality, production volume and emissions of plants for industrial gas production, in particular for real-time monitoring; software for monitoring and optimizing the operation of individual components or individual processes of a plant, of whole plants or of several plants for industrial gas production in the form of an open or closed control loop using operational data of components, in particular for use of operational data of components derived in real time for the diagnosis of the status or determination of the remaining lifetime of the components; software for recording operational data of plans for industrial gas production and analysis of the operation of plants and components of plants based on the recorded data; software for the automated operation of plants for industrial gas production, in particular the remote operation of plants; software for the automated operation of plants for industrial gas production, in particular for the remote control of plants; software platforms for controlling the operation of plants for industrial gas production, in particular via user interfaces; interface software for controlling plants for industrial gas production via user interfaces; aforesaid software with algorithms based on artificial intelligence, in particular for optimization of the operation of plants for operational modes that are not yet implemented.
Class 37
Installation and maintenance works for plants for industrial gas production, in particular for the production of oxygen, nitrogen, noble gases, hydrogen, carbon monoxide, carbon dioxide, olefins, liquified natural gas, for optimization of operation of the plants; installation services for sensors and data recording and data transmission units in plants for industrial gas production.
Class 42
Engineering services for optimizing the operation of plants for industrial gas production, in particular for the production of oxygen, nitrogen, noble gases, hydrogen, carbon monoxide, carbon dioxide, olefins, liquified natural gas; development and implementation of software for data recording, data analysis and control of plants for industrial gas production; control, in particular remote control of operations of plants for industrial gas production; remote monitoring of operations of plants for industrial gas production; remote monitoring of the operation of individual components of a plant for industrial gas production using operational data of components, in particular for use of operational data of components derived in real time for the diagnosis of the status or determination of the remaining lifetime of the components; remote determination of the cause of a failure or operational fault of a plant for industrial gas production, determination and providing of solutions for troubleshooting.
