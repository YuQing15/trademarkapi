REEFverse

Class 9
Downloadable computer software and mobile applications for adopting and monitoring corals; software for booking coral-planting or diving experiences; software for recording coral health data, analysing biodiversity information, and visualising reef restoration progress; downloadable dashboards, data visualisations, and digital certificates relating to coral reef restoration and environmental monitoring; downloadable software and digital dashboards for viewing and managing biodiversity or environmental or nature credits linked to coral reef restoration and conservation activities.
Class 42
Providing online non-downloadable software as a service (SaaS) for coral adoption, reef monitoring, and environmental restoration management, including features using artificial intelligence for data analysis and visualisation; design and development of computer software for tracking coral growth, biodiversity assessment, and ecological data analytics; providing online platforms enabling users to adopt and track corals, book coral-planting experiences and diving activities, and participate in environmental restoration programmes; scientific and technological research in the field of coral reef ecology; environmental monitoring and reporting services relating to marine ecosystems; providing online platforms and software tools for tracking, analysing, and reporting biodiversity or environmental credits associated with coral reef restoration projects.
