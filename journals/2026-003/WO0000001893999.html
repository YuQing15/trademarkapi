The mark consists of a design of three hexagons with one above and two below. In the top hexagon is the outline of a Greek Revival building superimposed over a representation of a globe with lines of longitude and the equator. In the lower left hexagon is a circle with eight protruding rectangles enclosing three human figures. In the lower right hexagon is a series of solid round dots, some of which are connected by vertical lines. Out of the last dot on the right there is a horizontal line that bisects into two smaller lines which also bisect into two smaller lines. At the junction of each bisect is a solid, round dot. To the right of this the letter-string "ISCT" appears above the words "INSTITUTE OF TRAINING & DEVELOPMENT".

Class 41
Education services, namely, providing in-person classes, workshops, and seminars in the field of gene and cell therapies and cell product development and manufacturing; providing online training in the field of gene and cell therapies and cell product development and manufacturing.
