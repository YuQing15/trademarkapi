The mark consists of a 3-second sequence. First, a wedge-shaped ‘pestle’ appears vertically in the image from top to bottom, with the wide end facing upwards. Next, the ‘pestle’ moves vertically slightly downwards and the shape of a ‘mortar’ or bowl slowly forms on the lower left side of the ‘pestle’, running from the left and then under the tip of the ‘pestle’ to the right.
