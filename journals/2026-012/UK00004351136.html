The mark consists of a stylized solid red hexagonal graphic on the left, and adjacent literal elements on the right. The red hexagonal graphic includes nested internal red line elements forming a symmetrical stepped structure, with a hollow hexagonal blank area at its exact center and a notched opening on its right side. To the right of the graphic, the top line features the three stylized simplified Chinese characters "九零创" in bold solid black, and directly below the Chinese characters is the wording "JIU LING CHUANG" in solid black uppercase font.
