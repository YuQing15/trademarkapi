software as a service (SaaS) services featuring software for monitoring, analyzing, and controlling electric-grid conditions, demand-response events, and power-system reliability; software as a service (SaaS) services featuring artificial intelligence (AI) software for real-time orchestration, scheduling, and power capping of computing workloads, battery systems, and other electrical infrastructure to meet grid constraints and computing performance targets;
