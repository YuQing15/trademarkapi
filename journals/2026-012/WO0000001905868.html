KIMICALAB figurative trademark. The trademark consists of the designed wording KIMICALAB written in a fancy blue block font, with all letters lowercase except the initial letter K, which is capital letter. The wording is characterized by a graphic element consisting of a black dot positioned directly above the second letter I, intersected by a black ellipse that in turn intersects the adjacent letters.

The mark contains the colours black and light blue
