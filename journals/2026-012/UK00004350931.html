I AM SAFE

Class 9
Downloadable mobile applications for personal safety and safeguarding; wearable electronic devices and smart badges for emergency alerting and personal security; wireless communication devices for transmitting emergency alerts and location information; personal safety alarm devices; electronic monitoring devices for safeguarding and personal protection; computer software for emergency notification, location tracking, and incident reporting; downloadable software for communication between wearable safety devices and mobile phones.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) featuring software for personal safety monitoring and safeguarding; providing temporary use of non-downloadable software for receiving, managing and responding to emergency alerts; hosting platforms for communication between wearable safety devices and mobile applications; design and development of computer software relating to personal safety and safeguarding systems.
Class 45
Personal safety monitoring services; providing personal security and safeguarding services via electronic devices and mobile applications; emergency response coordination services; monitoring of emergency signals and alerts generated by personal safety devices.
