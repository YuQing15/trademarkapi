Trade Mark Journal No.2026/012 20 March 2026

UK00004350361 6 March 2026 (9,42)

The mark consists of the word 'seeker' in standard characters. It is used for artificial intelligence-based software for recruitment and hiring purposes, including downloadable software (Class 9) and software as a service (SaaS) (Class 42) for analysing, processing, and rewriting resumes; analysing, rewriting and optimising job descriptions; and algorithmically matching and ranking job candidates based on skills, experience and job requirements. The mark may be used in color or black and white. No other limitations are claimed.

Class 9
Downloadable computer software using artificial intelligence for recruitment and hiring; downloadable software for analysing and processing resumes; downloadable software for rewriting resumes and job descriptions; downloadable software for ranking and matching job candidates based on skills, experience and job requirements.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) featuring artificial intelligence software for recruitment and hiring; providing temporary use of non-downloadable software for analysing and processing resumes; providing temporary use of non-downloadable software for rewriting resumes and job descriptions; providing temporary use of non-downloadable software for matching and ranking job candidates based on skills, experience and job requirements.
