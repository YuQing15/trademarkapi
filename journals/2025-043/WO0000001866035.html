The trademark is made up of denominative and figurative elements. In particular it is composed of the words "ERNESTO", "SOLARI" and "INSURANCE BROKER" placed on three different levels. The wording “SOLARI” is larger in dimensions than the remaining words. Similarly, the first letter "I" of the wording "INSURANCE" as well as the first letter "B" of the wording "BROKER" are larger in size than each of the remaining letters. Two rectangular geometric figures are placed on the right side of the wording “ERNESTO” and visible on the top part of the second wording “SOLARI”. Each figure is characterized by ornamental lines of various sizes and shapes. The words "INSURANCE BROKER" is inserted within a fantasy imprint. All highly stylized.
