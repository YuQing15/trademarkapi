The Dash Stop

Class 35
Retail services, wholesale services, distributorship services, and online retail services connected with the sale, provision, supply, and distribution of food, beverages, dairy products, frozen foods, bakery products, confectionery, chocolates, snacks, crisps, bread, eggs, milk, and ice creams; retail services relating to tobacco, snus, nicotine pouches, e-cigarettes, vapes, nicotine products, and smoking accessories; retail services relating to general household goods, kitchen products, cleaning products, laundry products, waste bags, and domestic essentials; retail services relating to clothing accessories including gloves, scarves, and hats; operation, management, and provision of self-service and automated retail services, including the operation of automated kiosks, vending machines, ATMs, coffee machines, slush machines, InPost lockers, Royal Mail lockers, PayPoint terminals, financial services terminals, and payment collection services; retail services offered via online platforms, websites, mobile applications, and e-commerce systems, including provision of goods and services through third-party delivery platforms such as Deliveroo, Uber Eats, Just Eat, or other similar delivery or courier services; retail services connected with prepared foods, takeaway foods, deli counters, and catering services, including delivery of such goods to consumers via online ordering, mobile applications, and third-party platforms; printing services, including photocopying, document printing, and related printing services; and all ancillary services, support services, and related services in connection with the sale, distribution, provision, promotion, delivery, printing, deli, and app-based fulfillment of the aforementioned goods and services.
