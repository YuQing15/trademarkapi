Class 35
Business administration assistance and management services relating to the operation and control of property rental bookings, including the scheduling, organisation, and coordination of property maintenance and cleaning services; provision of administrative support services connected to the management of concierge service coordination, handling of guest communications via electronic and telephone means, and management of property bookings, check-ins, and check-outs; provision of business data analysis and analytics services, including the collection, processing, and dissemination of business intelligence relating to hospitality operations, property occupancy, guest reviews, and accommodation services performance; marketing and promotional services for property rental businesses, hospitality providers, accommodation hosts, and related service providers, including the development and implementation of advertising campaigns, digital marketing initiatives, and customer acquisition programs; operation, management, and facilitation of online marketplaces and platforms enabling users to book, schedule, and pay for maintenance, cleaning, leisure, and concierge services for use in connection with properties and accommodation.
Class 36

Class 39

Class 41
