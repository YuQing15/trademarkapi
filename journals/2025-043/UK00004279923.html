Trade Mark Journal No.2025/043 24 October 2025

UK00004279923 17 October 2025 (37,38,40,41,42)

Class 37
Provision of installation, maintenance, and repair services for event technology systems, including audiovisual, lighting, staging, sets, rigging, scenic, and exhibition installations; Fitting and assembly of event infrastructure such as stages, rigging, truss systems, drapes, display stands, and temporary power and control systems; Integration of audiovisual, lighting, and electrical systems for both permanent and temporary use at venues and live events; Maintenance and technical servicing of audio, video, lighting, and electronic event equipment; Consultancy and project management in relation to event system installation, maintenance scheduling, and infrastructure setup; All of the aforesaid services being provided in connection with the planning, organisation, or production of corporate functions, private events, concerts, exhibitions, theatre performances, festivals, and other live or broadcast productions.
Class 38
Provision of communication and broadcasting services for events, including transmission of live and recorded audio, video, and multimedia content; Rental and configuration of telecommunications and signal distribution equipment, including systems for teleconferencing, videoconferencing, and live streaming; Real-time broadcasting and web streaming of live events over digital and online platforms; Establishment and management of communication infrastructure for live, hybrid, and virtual events; Technical support and consultancy relating to event communication networks, signal management, and broadcast system setup; All of the aforesaid services relating to the organisation and production of conferences, corporate functions, exhibitions, entertainment shows, festivals, and other live or online events.
Class 40
Custom fabrication, assembly, and treatment of materials for use in event production; Manufacture of bespoke audiovisual installations, scenic structures, display elements, and exhibition components; Material treatment services including carpentry, metal fabrication, CNC machining, painting, finishing, and treatment of textiles and drapes; Production-related services such as duplication of audio and video recordings, custom branding and printing for events, and temporary power generation; Consultancy and advisory services relating to fabrication methods, material selection, and production processes for event environments; All of the aforesaid services provided in connection with the preparation and delivery of corporate events, exhibitions, concerts, theatre productions, festivals, and live entertainment.
Class 41
Event production and technical operation services; Planning, management, and execution of live and recorded events, including technical direction and production of audiovisual, lighting, and staging systems; Hire and rental of production equipment, namely sound, lighting, video, staging, rigging, scenic, draping, power, and control equipment; Provision of recording and content creation facilities including audio and video studios, editing suites, and post-production services; Photography and videography services for live and corporate events; All of the aforesaid services rendered in connection with corporate functions, exhibitions, theatre productions, festivals, concerts, entertainment shows, and broadcast or streamed events.
Class 42
Design, technical planning, and consultancy relating to event and entertainment systems; Design and development of audiovisual, lighting, and control system layouts for live productions, exhibitions, and installations; Technical integration of hardware and software for event operation and production; Development and implementation of software for event control, signal processing, and digital content management; Consultancy relating to technical system design, event technology planning, automation, and safety management; Onsite support, system configuration, and equipment training services for production staff and technicians; All of the aforesaid services provided in connection with the design, setup, and delivery of corporate, private, and public events, exhibitions, festivals, theatre productions, and live entertainment.
