HOOTAi

Class 9
Downloadable computer software and mobile application for mental health, wellness and neurodivergence support; downloadable self-help tools; downloadable guided reflection programs; downloadable mindfulness and resilience exercises; but not including any such goods relating to entertainment, NFTs and/or teleconferencing.
Class 41
Provision of courses of instruction in self awareness; Conducting workshops and seminars in self awareness; Educational assistance services for persons with individual needs; Teaching of meditation practices; Meditation training; nonmedical personal coaching; all relating to mental health, wellness, neurodivergence support and personal development, but not including any such services relating to entertainment.
Class 42
Updating of smartphone software; Smartphone software design; Update of computer software; Computer software integration; Computer software development; all relating to  the delivery of AI-driven mental health, wellness, self- help and neurodivergence support tools;   but not including any such services relating to entertainment, teleconferencing and/or document-sharing.
Class 44
Provision of information and advisory services relating to mental health and wellness; provision of  on-line self-help and resilience purposes for medical purposes.
