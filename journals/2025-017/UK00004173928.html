Class 41
Entertainment services; betting, gaming, and gambling services; sports betting services; provision of online and offline sports betting services; lottery services; provision of online and offline lotteries, draws, and games of chance; organisation, operation, and provision of competitions, prize draws, sweepstakes, and contests; organisation of online gaming tournaments and betting events; provision of online gaming platforms and betting platforms for sports, lotteries, and games of chance; provision of non-downloadable gaming and betting software via online platforms; interactive gaming and gambling services provided online from a computer network or via mobile applications; providing virtual environments in which users can interact for recreational, gaming, and betting purposes; electronic betting and gaming services via a global computer network; entertainment services relating to the provision of betting, gaming, and gambling; provision of gaming and lottery information via websites and mobile applications; consultancy and advisory services relating to gaming, betting, and gambling; Provision of entertainment services incorporating gamified reward mechanisms, including interactive entertainment experiences related to gaming, betting, and lottery activities; organising and presenting entertainment events and challenges where audiences earn points or rewards based on participation or performance within gaming and betting environments; entertainment-based distribution of non-monetary prizes or incentives as part of game play or audience interaction; offering digital entertainment content that features integrated reward structures designed to enhance user engagement; provision of entertainment and gaming services that incorporate promotional incentives, including free bets, free plays, and bonus entries. Organisation of entertainment and sports events; arranging of contests and games for entertainment purposes; providing online computer games and mobile games; production of video and interactive media relating to gaming, betting, and lotteries; provision of information, news, and commentary in the field of gaming, sports betting, lotteries, and entertainment competitions; entertainment services provided via decentralized platforms, blockchain-based networks, and peer-to-peer networks, including blockchain-based games of chance, decentralized betting services, and NFT-linked games; provision of entertainment services linked to cryptocurrency and tokens, namely, games, lotteries, and betting platforms utilizing cryptocurrency and digital tokens; entertainment services incorporating virtual, augmented, and mixed reality experiences related to gaming, betting, lotteries, and reward-based competitions. Entertainment services incorporating cryptocurrency, crypto coins, digital tokens, and non-fungible tokens (NFTs), including the use of such digital assets as prizes, entry fees, in-game currency, rewards, loyalty points, and other units of value in connection with gaming, betting, lotteries, competitions, contests, and entertainment-based loyalty and rewards programs; all of the aforesaid services also provided via online, mobile, centralized, decentralized, blockchain, and web-based platforms.
