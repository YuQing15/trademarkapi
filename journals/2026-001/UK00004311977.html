The trademark consists of a distinctive, stylized design featuring a monogram composed of the letters “C” and “H”, which together form a unified and modern emblem representing the brand name CHELVAA HOMES. The design is presented in a balanced, geometric form, with the letter “C” shaped as a bold and rounded semicircle, open toward the right side. The rightward opening of the “C” leads seamlessly into the horizontal bar of the letter “H,” creating a visually connected and cohesive symbol. The letter “H” appears in a clean, minimalist style composed of a strong vertical bar intersected by a horizontal bar extending from the “C.” This integration creates the impression of a continuous, harmonious structure, symbolizing stability, craftsmanship, and sophistication. The monogram is prominently displayed above the wording “CHELVAA HOMES”, which appears in uppercase letters in a sans-serif typeface, ensuring clarity and contemporary visual appeal. The lettering is evenly spaced to provide a sense of balance and professionalism, which complements the geometric qualities of the monogram. In certain versions of the mark, the monogram appears in a bronze or golden-brown color, while the wording “CHELVAA HOMES” appears in either black or white, depending on the background context. One version features the monogram and wording placed on a black background, with the monogram in bronze/golden brown and the wording in white. Another version features the monogram in the same bronze/golden-brown color and the wording in black, set against a white background. These variations demonstrate contrast and adaptability while preserving the unique characteristics of the mark. The bronze or golden-brown color used for the monogram conveys a sense of luxury, quality, durability, and refined aesthetics, aligning with values commonly associated with home development, construction, and real estate services. The use of a bold, rounded “C” paired with the structured form of the “H” symbolizes both comfort and strength—two qualities central to the identity of CHELVAA HOMES as a housing, property development, or real estate brand. The overall design maintains modern simplicity while still being distinctive and memorable. The mark as a whole is intended to function as a visual representation of the company’s values, including reliability, elegance, innovation, and architectural integrity. The stylized monogram is capable of being used independently as a brand symbol, while the full combination with the name “CHELVAA HOMES” provides clear brand identity. The design allows for scalability, usability on various media, and broad application across marketing materials, signage, packaging, digital platforms, and business documentation. The applicant may claim the colors bronze/golden brown, black, and white as features of the mark. The color bronze/golden-brown applies specifically to the stylized “CH” monogram. The colors black or white may apply to the text “CHELVAA HOMES,” depending on the background or presentation context. Alternatively, if the applicant chooses not to claim color as a feature of the mark, then the mark may be registered without color limitation, and any colors shown in the attached representation are understood to be for illustrative purposes only. The literal element of the mark consists of the wording “CHELVAA HOMES.” The applicant makes no claim to the exclusive right to use the term “HOMES” apart from the mark as shown, as this term may be descriptive of real estate, construction, or housing services. However, the stylized monogram “CH” and the overall specific arrangement, font style, color presentation, and geometric composition are claimed as distinctive features of the trademark. The mark may be used in connection with services in Class 36 and/or Class 37, depending on the applicant’s business scope, including but not limited to real estate development services, real estate brokerage, property management, housing construction, building development, renovation, and general contracting services. The stylized presentation and monogram serve to uniquely identify the company’s goods and services, ensuring that consumers can distinguish the applicant’s offerings from those of others in the marketplace.
