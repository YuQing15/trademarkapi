Class 9
Downloadable and cloud-based computer software and mobile applications for collecting, consolidating, analysing and displaying data from multiple online platforms, including but not limited to music-streaming services, social-media networks, live-performance listing sites, marketing systems and sales or distribution platforms; software using human-centred artificial-intelligence (AI) models that generate analytics derived from artists’ own performance, audience and engagement data; machine-learning and data-visualisation tools enabling creative professionals to interpret and act upon their own multi-source datasets. None of the foregoing relating to the creation, recording, publishing or retail distribution of music, nor to the provision of consumer music-streaming services or audio recordings. .
Class 35
Commercial data analysis; business advisory services for artists, managers, record labels and digital-content creators.
Class 41
Provision of information, training and consultancy in the fields of music performance, production, marketing and analytics; educational services relating to the ethical and human-centred use of AI in the creative industries; organising workshops, webinars and online learning materials that teach artists how to interpret insights derived from their own data across multiple platforms; publishing of electronic resources featuring performance and marketing analytics for creative professionals. .
Class 42
Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) featuring platforms that consolidate and analyse data from multiple online music-streaming, social-media, live-performance, marketing and distribution sources; design, development and maintenance of computer software for data analytics and human-centred artificial-intelligence applications; consultancy and research in the field of ethical AI and data-driven insight generation; providing temporary use of online non-downloadable software that allows artists and managers to derive actionable insights from their own aggregated data. None of the foregoing relating to the creation, recording, publishing or retail distribution of music, nor to the provision of consumer music-streaming services or audio recordings. .
