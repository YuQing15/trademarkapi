Trade Mark Journal No.2025/031 1 August 2025

UK00004237056 21 July 2025 (21,25,35,41,42)

Satsuma

Class 21
Household and promotional items, namely: mugs, drinking vessels, water bottles, and flasks bearing branding associated with digital currencies and educational initiatives; Reusable drinkware and utensils used for marketing and awareness campaigns in the field of decentralised finance and Bitcoin education.
Class 25
Clothing, headgear, and footwear, namely: T-shirts, hoodies, sweatshirts, jackets, hats, and caps featuring branding related to digital currency education and strategy; Branded apparel for use in promotional, educational, or consultancy contexts, including merchandise associated with financial literacy and decentralised blockchain-based finance.
Class 35
Business consultancy and advisory services, namely: Strategic business consulting in the field of digital currencies and blockchain-based financial systems; Business planning and treasury strategy consultancy for companies holding or intending to hold digital assets, including Bitcoin; Business management services relating to the integration of digital currencies into company operations; Provision of commercial information and advice for consumers and businesses relating to the use and management of decentralised digital assets; Market analysis and client business research in the field of financial technology and virtual currencies; Arranging and conducting business networking events and conferences for professionals in the digital finance and cryptocurrency sectors; All the aforesaid services excluding marketing services, advertising, public relations, affiliate marketing.
Class 41
Educational and training services, namely: Delivery of live and online courses, workshops, and seminars in the field of digital currencies and blockchain-based finance; Coaching and instruction in Bitcoin custody, strategy, and adoption for individuals and businesses; Development and publication of instructional materials, training manuals, and digital resources relating to financial applications of digital currencies; Provision of non-downloadable online videos, podcasts, and tutorials in the field of decentralised finance and digital assets; Educational and training services relating to the financial use, storage, and strategy of digital currencies.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) and technology consultancy services, namely: Provision of online platforms for analysing, visualising, or managing digital currency reserves and treasury strategies; Development and maintenance of software tools for Bitcoin custody modelling, pricing, and strategic planning; Technical advisory services relating to the implementation of blockchain-based infrastructure within businesses; Hosting of digital tools and data-driven dashboards for decision-making in decentralised finance environments; All the aforesaid services excluding development of advertising platforms, or marketing tools.

Satsuma Trading Ltd
