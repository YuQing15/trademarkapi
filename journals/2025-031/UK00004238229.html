Class 9
Wearable digital electronic communication devices; wearable activity trackers; portable and wearable digital electronic devices for sensing, detecting, recording, organising, transmitting, visualising, reviewing, and receiving digital text and data; wearable electronic devices incorporating sensors capable of being attached to bracelets, wristbands and watch straps; wearable electronic devices capable of sensing, detecting, monitoring, recording, transmitting and communicating digital data over the internet and short-range wireless and other computer and electronic communication networks; wearable electronic devices comprised of software for alerts and reminders, and for sensing, detecting, monitoring, recording, organising, transmitting, visualising, reviewing, and receiving digital text and data; sensors for scientific use to be worn by a human to gather human biometric data; sensors; computer and application software for alerts and reminders, and for recording, organising, transmitting, reviewing, and receiving digital text and data; computer and application software for smartphones and mobile and portable electronic devices in the fields of biometric and health tracking and monitoring; computer application software for smartphones, smart devices and mobile devices to support personal wellness, general healthcare and nutritional plans; computer software capable of connecting wearable electronic devices and activity trackers with smartphones and mobile and portable electronic devices to record, organise, transmit, visualise, review and interpret digital text and data in the fields of biometric and health tracking and monitoring; parts and fittings for all the aforesaid goods.
Class 10
Personal and medical diagnostic appliances and devices used for medical purposes; wearable medical measurement apparatus and instruments; general health-related instruments and apparatus for sensing, detecting, monitoring, storing, relaying, tracking, measuring and displaying biometric and blood glucose data; wearable medical devices for continuous monitoring of glucose levels in the human body; wearable medical devices and their accessories particularly sensors, transmitters and receivers, all for medical purposes; blood glucose meters being wearable devices for detecting and monitoring glucose levels directly in blood and/or interstitial fluid in the human body; wearable medical electronic devices; blood glucose meters and monitoring devices and apparatus; non-invasive blood testing apparatus and equipment; parts and fittings for all the aforesaid goods.
