Class 38
Telecommunications; transmitting, distributing and broadcasting images, sound and data; providing access to and making available electronic (communications) networks, wireless or otherwise, dashboards, websites, portals, electronic databases, chat rooms, social networks, search engines, forums and online communication facilities; providing access to and making available an online platform on the Internet, including for the purpose of creating an overview of social contacts and a social safety net; provision of access to and provision of an interactive online platform on the Internet, including for the purpose of creating a directory of social contacts and a social safety net; provision of access to content; electronic transmission of data; providing advice, consultancy and information regarding the aforementioned services.
Class 41
Education; training; entertainment; sports and cultural activities; organizing educational events and meetings; organizing and holding seminars, webinars, lectures, workshops, courses, symposia, conferences, congresses, exhibitions, tutorials and (video) training courses, including in the field of providing insight into social networks of individuals; writing blogs; publishing and distributing printed matter, including books, magazines, periodicals, newspapers, leaflets, brochures, publications, teaching materials, educational materials and other printed matter, whether or not in digital form, as well as electronic publications and audio-visual and multimedia productions, including in the area of providing insight into people's social networks; providing advice, consultancy and information regarding the aforementioned services.
