Telecommunications services; mobile telecommunication services; broadband telecommunications services; broadcasting services; streaming and downloading of audio, video, audiovisual and multimedia content, data and information whether via the internet, communications networks, wireless telecommunications networks or by other means; transmission and delivery of audiovisual and multimedia content via the internet; providing video on-demand transmission of audio, video and audiovisual content, data and information; streaming of data; streaming of audio and video tethered downloads; video streaming services via the internet or any other communication network featuring films and movies; Internet protocol television (IPTV) transmission services; broadcasting and/or transmission of radio and/or television programmes and/or films; satellite, DTT, cable, DSL and broadband broadcasting and/or transmission of audio and/or audio visual programming; transmission of audio, video and/or audio visual programming (by any means); transmission of video films; broadcasting and transmission of content including television programmes, films, movies, sports, documentaries and entertainment to platforms including televisions, personal computers, wearable devices, mobile devices and tablet computers; broadcasting and communications by means of or aided by computer; transmission of audio, video and/or audio visual programming by Internet protocol (IPTV); telecommunications services over the Internet including but not limited to services provided using voice over Internet protocol (VOIP); telecommunication and/or communication and/or broadcast and/or transmission of audio visual content; television screen based information broadcasting and retrieval services; communications services by satellite, television and/or radio; news agency services; provision of access to news, current affairs and sports information; television broadcasting services incorporating interactive services for viewing guides and intelligent automated selection for programme recordal; providing interactive television viewers (including those watching on their mobile telephones, PDAs, tablet computers or PCs) with access to information, data, graphics, audio and/or audio-visual content from a restricted group of Internet websites or portals; broadcasting and transmission of interactive television, interactive games, interactive news, interactive sport, interactive entertainment and interactive competitions; video on demand and near on demand telecommunication and/or communication and/or broadcast and/or transmission services; streaming delivery of video on demand streams to viewers; providing access to movies, videos, sports, entertainment and television programmes to viewers on demand and near on demand; provision of a connection between websites and television viewers via an interactive television portal; interactive television portal services providing access to Internet websites for television viewers via their television receiving equipment including digital television receiving equipment; factual information services relating to television broadcasting; online video on demand or near on demand telecommunication and/or communication and/or broadcast and/or transmission services; communication services for transmitting, caching, accessing, receiving, downloading, streaming, broadcasting, sharing, displaying, formatting, mirroring and transferring text, images, audio, video and data via telecommunications networks, wireless communications networks, and the internet; providing on-line portals and platforms for entertainment in the field of movies, television, audiovisual works, music, audio works, arts, books, theatre, literary works, sport, recreational activities, leisure activities; providing information on telecommunications and broadcasting; broadcasting services relating to Internet protocol TV; provision of access to Internet protocol TV; advice and consultancy services in relation to the above.
