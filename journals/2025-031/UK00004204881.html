DearComic

Class 9
Downloadable personalized digital comic books generated by artificial intelligence; downloadable personalized comic content generated by artificial intelligence; mobile applications for creating and generating customized comics, animated stories, and story scripts using artificial intelligence; downloadable software for customized AI-based storytelling; downloadable personalized animated comics and motion-based visual stories generated by artificial intelligence; downloadable personalized story scripts generated by artificial intelligence; downloadable personalized interactive content and gamified digital experiences featuring comic characters, generated by artificial intelligence; downloadable personalized animated films and short-form visual media generated by artificial intelligence; downloadable storyboarding tools; downloadable design templates for comic creation.
Class 42
Software as a Service (SaaS) services featuring software for generating personalized digital comic books and personalized story scripts using artificial intelligence; providing temporary use of online non-downloadable software for creating personalized comics, personalized story scripts, and personalized animated stories using artificial intelligence; providing AI-based character generation and customization services; Provision of software for collaborative storytelling and multi-user content generation; hosting an online platform for users to create, store, manage, and share their AI-generated personalized digital comics, stories, and related visual content; providing an application programming interface (API) for AI-based personalized content generation and integration; Provision of software for generating personalized interactive storytelling and personalized gamified visual experiences using artificial intelligence.
