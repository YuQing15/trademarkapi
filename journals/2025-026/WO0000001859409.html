The mark consists of a stylized design and literal elements, featuring an abstract representation of the letter "S" composed of two curved, leaf-like or crescent shapes that form the "S" monogram. The top shape curves downwards, and the bottom shape curves upwards, with both shapes being smooth, rounded, and symmetrical. The entire mark is in black and white, with the design element appearing in black.
