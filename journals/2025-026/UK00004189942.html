Devil's Advocates

Class 9
Downloadable audio, video, and multimedia content featuring commentary, discussion, and analysis of cultural, political, economic, and social issues; downloadable podcasts and video recordings focused on public discourse; digital publications and multimedia files for public information and entertainment purposes. Expressly excluding educational materials related to legal instruction, academic coursework, or vocational training. Legal topics may be discussed in relation to public discourse and current events, but not for the purpose of legal education or training.
Class 41
Provision of live and online events focused on public affairs, economic, cultural, political, and philosophical discourse; organisation of speaker panels, public debates, and civic forums for entertainment and commentary purposes; hosting of public-facing discussions aimed at fostering open expression and critical engagement with contemporary issues. Expressly excluding legal education services, vocational training, and academic tuition. Legal issues may be addressed where relevant to cultural, political, or public affairs discourse, provided such coverage is not intended as legal instruction or education.
