Class 9
Downloadable software and mobile applications for pet adoption, rehoming, fostering, and pet-owner matching; downloadable databases and tools for managing pet profiles, health records, and adoption status; software for tracking pet location, behaviour, or health; software using artificial intelligence for matching pets with suitable adopters or foster homes; software for facilitating communication, scheduling, and payments between users of pet-related services.
Class 25
Clothing, footwear, headwear; apparel and apparel accessories relating to pet adoption.
Class 35
Advertising and promotional services in the fields of pet adoption, rehoming, fostering, and pet-related services;   providing and online marketplace featuring pets for adoption, rehoming, or fostering; promotion of third-party shelters, rescue centres, and individuals; connecting users with pet service providers including groomers, walkers, and sitters; retail services connected with the sale of pet food, pet accessories, toys, grooming products, and pet care supplies.
Class 36
Facilitating online payments and financial transactions in the fields of pet adoption, rehoming, fostering, and pet-related services; collection and processing of adoption fees and donations for pet rescue organisations or individuals; referral and brokerage services for pet insurance and related financial products.
Class 38
Providing online communication tools for pet owners, adopters, rehomers, and service providers; electronic messaging and notification services; audio, video, and chat transmission through mobile applications and websites; enabling digital meet-and-greet or video calls between users and pet providers.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) and platform as a service (PaaS) for pet adoption, rehoming, fostering, and pet-care management; creation and hosting of online communities for sharing pet profiles, success stories, and care advice; hosting of online platforms and mobile apps for pet-related services; development and maintenance of pet-focused websites and applications; cloud storage services for pet records and user data; provision of non-downloadable software for managing communications and user profiles in the context of pet services.
Class 45
Online social networking services for pet lovers, adopters, rehomers, fosterers, and rescue organisations; services for matching pets with suitable adopters, rehomers, or fosterers provided through the use of artificial intelligence and algorithmic systems. providing identity verification and trust-building tools for users of pet-related services; forums, feeds, and profile-based interactions in the context of pet adoption, rehoming, and fostering.
