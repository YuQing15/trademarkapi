MADOBIA

Class 9
Downloadable computer software for use as a learning management system (LMS) for creating, managing, administering, tracking, and delivering educational courses and training programs; Downloadable mobile application software for use as a learning management system (LMS); Downloadable software incorporating artificial intelligence (AI) for adaptive learning, personalised content delivery, and automated skill-gap analysis; Downloadable software for authoring, importing, and distributing digital learning content that complies with the Sharable Content Object Reference Model (SCORM) and the Experience Application Programming Interface (xAPI); Downloadable diagnostic software tools for assessing training requirements and generating customised course recommendations.
Class 41
Educational services, namely, providing online non-downloadable courses, classes, and instructional content in the fields of business management, leadership development, workforce training, and applied artificial intelligence; Providing digital learning resources and training modules via a proprietary web-based platform; Providing non-downloadable online interactive self-assessment tools for evaluating training needs and recommending tailored educational content; Providing online educational testing, certification, and progress tracking associated with learning programs delivered through a proprietary Learning Management System (LMS); Hosting and organising webinars in the field of business education and technology training; Hosting and organising educational seminars, conferences, and symposiums in the fields of professional development and leadership.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) services featuring non-downloadable software for use as a Learning Management System (LMS) for hosting, managing, tracking, and delivering online educational courses and training programs for others; SaaS services featuring software for authoring, importing, organising, and distributing digital learning content that complies with the Sharable Content Object Reference Model (SCORM) and the Experience Application Programming Interface (xAPI); SaaS services featuring software incorporating Artificial Intelligence (AI) for adaptive learning, automated content recommendation, personalised learning paths, performance analytics, and predictive modelling of training outcomes; SaaS services providing multi-tenant access for organisations to administer enterprise-level training environments, manage user enrolment, monitor learner progress, and standardise workforce training delivery; SaaS services for integrating learning systems with Human Resource Information Systems (HRIS), Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS), and compliance management platforms; Providing temporary use of non-downloadable software for digital credentialing, certification issuance, and badge management; Providing temporary use of non-downloadable software for facilitating remote skills assessments and psychometric testing; Platform as a service (PaaS) featuring computer software platforms for developers and businesses to build, customise, and deploy modular training and educational content within a hosted learning management framework.
