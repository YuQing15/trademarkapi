LITTLEKIN

Class 35
On-line retail store services featuring subscription boxes containing pet supplies and other pet-related goods and offers for pet-related services; consumer membership program services in the field of pet products and services; promoting the goods and services of others by providing a web site featuring information, reviews and advice about products, services, practices and trends, all being in relation to pets.
Class 41
Provision of information relating to pet training, pet training in the field of behavioral modification, and pet exercise via the internet; providing on-line non-downloadable publications in the nature of articles, newsletters, and blogs relating to pet care, pet health, pet pregnancy and pet sitting services.
Class 44
Provision of information relating to pet health, pet nutrition and feeding, pet mental and physical health development, and pet pregnancy as it relates to pet health via the internet; provision of information relating to pet related services, namely, veterinary and grooming services, via the internet.
