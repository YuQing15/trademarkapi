The mark consists of the image of a quadrilateral that is internally red, except for the presence of a first segment and a second segment, both white. The first segment has one end lapping one vertex of the quadrilateral and the other end lapping the opposite vertex of the same quadrilateral. The second segment has one end that touches a vertical side of the quadrilateral and the other end that touches the opposite vertical side of the same quadrilateral. The first segment and the second segment intersect each other. To the right of this image, we find the letters ATK in capital letters and in red.

The mark contains the colours red and white.
