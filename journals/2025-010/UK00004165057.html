Instrumentals Club provides original instrumental music and nature visuals designed to promote relaxation, mental wellbeing, and emotional healing. Through digital media, streaming services, sync licensing and curated video content, Instrumentals Club aims to transform passive screen time into a calming, therapeutic experience for users in healthcare settings, waiting rooms, care homes, and beyond.

Class 9
Downloadable and streaming digital music recordings; video recordings featuring instrumental music and nature visuals; downloadable mobile applications for streaming instrumental music and visual media.
Class 38
Broadcasting and streaming of audio-visual content; transmission of digital music and videos over the internet; provision of access to multimedia content via online platforms.
Class 41
Entertainment services, namely, providing online non-downloadable music and videos featuring instrumental music and nature visuals; music production services; curation of content for mental wellbeing and relaxation.
