Class 35
Healthcare consulting services, namely, business management consulting in the field of healthcare, and business and management consulting for healthcare providers and related businesses; commercial lobbying services in the healthcare field; providing business consultancy on public policy on behalf of businesses in the healthcare industry facing policy or regulatory changes that present barriers to market entry, business efficiency or slow rates of return on investments, and commercial lobbying services; data collection, processing, compilation, systemisation and management in the field of healthcare for business purposes, namely, financial analysis, budget impact and cost effectiveness for congressional budget office and office of management and budget impact models; business consulting in the field of public and private payer coverage; consulting in the field of healthcare coding, business pricing and management services.
Class 36
Insurance information in the field of private and public payer coverage.
Class 42
Technical advisory services relating to data processing and computer services for the analysis of data involving data relating to healthcare financial analysis, budget impact and cost-effectiveness models, assessment of cost impact of proposed legislation on medicare expenditures, analysis of national and local coverage determinations; technical advisory services relating to data processing and computer services for the analysis of data for alternative outcomes; technical advisory services relating to data processing and computer services for the analysis of data in support of litigation, due diligence or transaction specific client needs.
Class 45
Political lobbying services; legal advocacy services, namely, advocating for health policy alternatives necessitated by policy or regulatory changes that raise barriers to market entry, challenge business efficiency or viability, threaten to reduce rates of return for investments, or slow customer adoption of health items or services.
