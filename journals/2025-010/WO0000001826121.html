Yellow, beige and brown- visible parts of the body of the bear; blue - the bears’ eyes; grey - the bear’s nose; red - Kubuś logotype; white - t-shirt the bear is wearing; navy blue - trousers the bear is wearing; white and green - shoes the bear is wearing.
