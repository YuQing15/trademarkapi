LUCENT

Class 35
Retail and wholesale services, including online, in relation to beauty products, cosmetics, skincare, haircare, fragrances, and personal care preparations; business management and administration of beauty salons, spas, and wellness centres; marketing, advertising and promotion of beauty and wellness services; provision of business advice, consultancy, and support services relating to the operation of beauty salons and spas; loyalty, incentive, and membership programme services relating to beauty and wellness services; arranging and conducting trade shows, exhibitions, and events for business and promotional purposes in the field of beauty, skincare, and wellness.
Class 41
Education and training services in the field of beauty, skincare, cosmetics, hair care, and wellness; organisation of workshops, courses, and masterclasses relating to beauty, personal care, and cosmetic treatments; provision of certification and accreditation for beauty professionals and therapists; publication of educational materials and content in the field of beauty and wellness; provision of online non-downloadable videos, tutorials, and digital content in relation to beauty and wellness; organisation of events, exhibitions, and conferences for educational purposes in the beauty industry; mentoring and coaching services for beauty and wellness professionals.
Class 44
Beauty salon services; hairdressing services; cosmetic treatment services; skincare treatment services; provision of non-medical facial and body treatments; spa services; aesthetic treatment services; nail care services; make-up application and consultancy services; massage services; holistic beauty therapy services; wellness and self-care services; aromatherapy services; provision of information and advice relating to beauty, skincare, and cosmetic treatments;  provision of information relating to beauty, skincare, and cosmetic treatments via online platforms and social media; consultation services relating to skincare, cosmetics, and personal care; medical and dermatological skincare services; provision of non-surgical aesthetic treatments.
