The mark contains the colours blue, red and white.

The first three (3) letters of the word element are blue and the last two (2) are red; to the left of the letters there are two overlapping circles: the lower one is blue, the upper one is red, and a white line separates them.

International priority date claimed: 16 July 2024 (Poland)

Services related to agriculture, horticulture and forestry; information and advisory services on agriculture, horticulture and forestry; advisory services on the use of fertiliser products, advisory services involving field data on agriculture, horticulture and forestry in relation to fertiliser products.
