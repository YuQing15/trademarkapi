The Trade Mark consists of the term “SEVENMINDS” represented in a stylised font, in lowercase letters, specifically, “SEVEN” appears in grey (HEX #9C9E9F) and “MINDS” appears in cyan (HEX #2D8AAA). An abstract round shape with an irregular line dividing it in two also appears to the left of the sign, with the upper part represented in cyan (HEX #2D8AAA) and the lower part in grey (HEX #9C9E9F). The colours Cyan (HEX #2D8AAA) and grey (HEX #9C9E9F) are claimed as part of the mark.
