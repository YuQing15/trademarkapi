CUSTODIFY

Class 9
Computer software; downloadable software; mobile applications; recorded and downloadable computer programs; software for tracking, managing, verifying and evidencing custody of vehicles, trailers, movable assets and equipment; software for generating legal evidence records; software for processing, analysing, recording and displaying custody events; compliance software; software for driver identification, driver assignment and custody status determination; risk analysis software; downloadable electronic data files featuring custody timelines, audit records, evidence packs, verification data, liability information and chain-of-custody logs; downloadable application programming interfaces (APIs), software libraries and software development kits (SDKs) for integration with fleet management systems, telematics systems, logistics platforms, insurance systems and compliance systems.
Class 39
Information services relating to transport, fleet management, logistics operations, driver assignment and asset handover; tracking and monitoring of vehicles, trailers, equipment and movable assets for logistical, mobility and operational purposes; providing transport-related information, including custody duration, handover timelines, driver usage history, asset allocation and location status; consultancy and advisory services relating to fleet operations, logistics coordination and vehicle utilisation.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS); platform as a service (PaaS) featuring software for tracking, managing, recording, verifying and evidencing custody of vehicles, trailers, movable assets and equipment; providing temporary use of non-downloadable software for generating and verifying chain-of-custody records; hosting of digital platforms for automated liability assignment; providing use of online non-downloadable software for generating evidence packs, custody timelines, audit logs, driver identification and custody analytics; hosting of application programming interfaces (APIs), software development kits (SDKs) and integration tools for fleet, logistics, mobility, insurance, compliance and legal-tech applications; design, development, maintenance and updating of computer software; cloud hosting services; data storage, data modelling and data analytics services relating to custody, liability and transport operations; non-downloadable software; non-downloadable application programming interfaces (APIs), software libraries and software development kits (SDKs) for integration with fleet management systems, telematics systems, logistics platforms, insurance systems and compliance systems.
Class 45
Legal compliance services; preparation, verification and authentication of legal evidence; providing information relating to legal liability, custody verification and regulatory compliance; forensic analysis of custody data for legal, regulatory or insurance purposes; authentication, validation and verification services relating to digital records, audit logs, custody timelines and evidence packs; consultancy relating to assignment of liability, driver responsibility, asset custody, legal compliance and evidentiary standards; providing chain-of-custody certification services; advisory and information services relating to legal responsibility in vehicle, asset and equipment usage.
