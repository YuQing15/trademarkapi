STUDIO NIRA

Class 38
Streaming of audio, video and audio‑visual content over the Internet via websites, mobile applications and digital platforms relating to yoga, fitness and pilates instruction; subscription based and video‑on‑demand transmission services featuring yoga and physical fitness classes, training and instruction; electronic transmission and streaming of digital media content via global computer networks; providing users with access to libraries of on demand audio visual content; information, advice and assistance relating to the aforesaid; providing access to online forums, bulletin boards and chat rooms including interactive and real time communication facilities for the transmission of messages, blogs and user generated content relating to yoga, fitness and wellness; web messaging services; electronic transmission of instant messages and data; providing access to online computer databases, all relating to yoga, fitness and pilates instruction.
Class 41
Online educational and instructional services in the field of yoga and physical fitness; providing live interactive and recorded yoga and fitness classes by means of Internet streaming via websites, mobile applications and digital platforms; providing non-downloadable instructional videos and audiovisual content featuring yoga, exercise and wellness accessed on a subscription and on demand basis; providing online courses and training programs in the field of yoga; instructional information, videos and audiovisual content relating to yoga, physical fitness and wellness provided via a website and mobile applications; providing online journals and blogs featuring yoga and fitness content; production and publication of online text, images, audio and audiovisual materials relating to yoga and fitness, as part of educational and instructional services, via a website and mobile applications.
