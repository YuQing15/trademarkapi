The mark consists of a device on top of the stylized words “spice services”. The device consists of a leaf shaped element in green olive colour. The upper part of the leaf is shaped on top as upper lips. Within this low part of the device, an inner curved shape like a chili pepper is in dark red colour and could also be interpreted as a smile. The word “spice” appears in dark red lowercase letters, and the word “services” appears in olive green lowercase letters.

Olive green and dark red.
