PROHIRE

Class 35
Business management; business administration; business information services; business management of a vehicle fleet for others, business management of a transportation fleet for others; business advice, management and assistance relating to franchising; provision and operation of motor vehicle dealership services; retail services in respect of the sale of motor vehicles; marketing, advertising, and publicity services; organizing, arranging or conducting competitions, demonstrations, displays, events, trade fairs, exhibitions, festivals or presentations for advertising or marketing; dissemination or distribution of advertising material or advertising matter; provision of on-line advertising space; operation and management of gift card, discount card and customer loyalty schemes; information, advisory and consultancy services relating to the aforesaid services.
Class 36
Financial services for the leasing of vehicles, provision of finance for leasing; financial and insurance services; finance services for the purchase, hire and rental of motor vehicles.
Class 37
Motor vehicle repair, maintenance, and servicing; diagnostic tuning, reconditioning, painting, polishing, restoration, and cleaning of motor vehicles; motor vehicle inspection services; advisory and information services relating to the aforesaid services.
Class 39
Automobile leasing, rental and commercial fleet management services; car, van, lorry, trailer, cargo container and truck leasing and rental services; all of the aforementioned services including petrol, diesel, alternative fuel vehicles and electric vehicles; provision of support, information, advisory and consultancy services relating to petrol, diesel, alternative fuel and electric vehicles.
Class 41
Education services; training and tuition services; organising events and competitions, arranging, and conducting of colloquiums, conferences, congresses, seminars, symposiums and workshops; arranging and conducting of courses of instruction relating to, or connected with, petrol, diesel, alternative fuel and electric vehicles and vehicle fleet management; publication of educational video content; provision of non-downloadable content; production and publishing of films or video recordings on the leasing of automobiles, trucks, trailers, cargo containers and trucks; information and advice on leasing automobiles, trucks, trailers and cargo containers; information, advisory and consultancy services relating to the foregoing; providing on-line publications (not downloadable).
