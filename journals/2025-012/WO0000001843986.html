Education; providing of training; entertainment; sporting and cultural activities; arranging and conducting seminars, courses, workshops, congresses, training, lectures, education and other events, including for managers in the pharmaceutical industry, including in the field of business development, market forecasting, market penetration, risk analysis, strategy and brand development; compiling, publishing, circulating and lending of books, journals, newspapers, magazines, articles, press releases, leaflets, brochures, information bulletins and other printed matter and periodicals, and audiovisual and multimedia products, including for the pharmaceutical industry; providing non-downloadable electronic publications from a global computer network or the Internet; publication of electronic books and journals online; publication of texts, other than publicity texts; translation and interpretation; consultancy and information regarding the aforesaid services; the aforesaid services also provided via electronic networks, such as the Internet.

Science and technology services, including research and design relating thereto, including design and development of teaching material for courses, workshops, training and training courses including for the pharmaceutical industry; consultancy and information regarding the aforesaid services; including the aforesaid services provided via electronic networks, including the Internet.
