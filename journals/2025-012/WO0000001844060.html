The mark consists of three rectangles in gradually increasing heights below a stylized representation of two fish to the left of the words "SEAFOOD METRICS" with the word "Seafood" in lower case letters directly above the letters "ME" in the word "Metrics" in initial caps.

Registration of this mark shall give no right to the exclusive use of the words seafood metrics.

Class 35
Promoting collaboration within industry groups to make improvements in the fields of seafood supply chains and fishery and aquaculture sustainability; promoting public awareness of the need for environmental conservation and preservation of the oceans, marine life, and associated ecosystems; providing business, commercial and consumer information in the fields of seafood supply chains and fishery and aquaculture sustainability via a website; providing business, commercial, and consumer information in the fields of seafood supply chains and fishery and aquaculture sustainability; providing independent ratings of the status of fish stocks, fisheries, and aquaculture zones to businesses in the seafood industry for purposes of improving sustainability; providing business, commercial and consumer information in the fields of seafood supply chains and fishery and aquaculture sustainability via a database.
Class 41
Providing online non-downloadable electronic publications in the nature of reports and charts in the fields of seafood supply chains and fishery and aquaculture sustainability.
