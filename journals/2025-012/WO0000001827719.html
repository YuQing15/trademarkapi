Class 36
Charitable fundraising services for public health philanthropy, namely, providing grants for research in smoking and toxic tobacco cessation, in the effectiveness of smoking and tobacco cessation interventions integrating behavioral, educational, and pharmacological approaches, and in tobacco control, tobacco harm reduction, and the use of reduced risk tobacco products; charitable fundraising services for public health philanthropy, namely, providing grants for science and technology research and solutions relating to accelerating an end to smoking; providing grants for research in the fields of public health economics and assessment of policy impact, and environmental issues created by reduced risk products-related waste materials; providing grants in the area of agricultural transformation, namely, the use of agricultural science; providing grants to support the provision of classes, trainings, seminars, workshops, convenings, virtual education, and information relating to global tobacco control and cessation, tobacco harm reduction, agriculture, agricultural transformation, agricultural science, smoking cessation, nicotine, the continuum of risk, and the impact of smoking cessation and tobacco harm reduction on health; providing scholarships and funding for capacity building in the areas of smoking cessation, tobacco harm reduction, agricultural transformation, and agricultural science.
Class 41
Providing seminars, workshops, convenings, panel discussions, conferences, webinars, virtual education, meetings, and information regarding global tobacco control and cessation to smokers, users of toxic tobacco products, health care providers, the general public, and other stakeholders; capacity building in the areas of smoking cessation, tobacco harm reduction, agricultural transformation, and agricultural science; conducting in-person and on-line seminars and meetings regarding the impact of smoking cessation and tobacco harm reduction on health; providing educational services relating to smoking cessation, tobacco harm reduction, agriculture, agricultural transformation, agricultural science, nicotine, and the continuum of risk.
Class 42
Global tobacco control research and analysis relating to smoking cessation and tobacco harm reduction; providing information in the fields of public health economics, environmental issues created by reduced risk products-related waste materials, agricultural transformation, and agricultural science.
