The trademark consists of an imprint depicting a stylized label with a stylized contrasting double frame, in the upper part of which, and partially protruding, there is a stylized oval with stylized frame inside which are the letters DM in fancy characters, and inside said label are the wordings DUCA MOSCATI in fancy characters arranged on two lines in which the wording DUCA is of greater dimensions.
