The mark consists of a stylized, solid silhouette of a bull’s head facing forward, featuring wide, upward-curving horns. A distinct circular ring hangs from the nose of the bull. Below the bull head icon appears the text 'IRON BULL' in a bold, uppercase, sans-serif font.
