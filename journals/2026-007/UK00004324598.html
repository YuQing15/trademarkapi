SHEERLUXE

Class 41
Publishing, reporting, and writing of texts; photographic reporting; news reporting; publishing of magazines; publishing of an online magazine; publishing of an online magazine featuring news, editorials, opinions, reports, trend analysis and any other type of commentaries in relation to haute couture (high fashion), high street fashion, clothing, footwear and headgear, beauty care, skincare, make-up, hair and nails, travel, culture, local culture, popular culture, current affairs, film, music, books, podcasts, restaurants and bars, food and drink, careers, parenting, health and wellness, weddings, sex and relationship advice, sports and fitness, real estate, interior design, decorating, furniture, kitchen, bathroom and living room furniture and accessories; entertainment, educational and cultural services by providing non-downloadable playback of audio, visual and audio-visual recordings, publications and content; provision of entertainment, gaming, cultural, sporting and educational content, information, advice and assistance via a website; providing non-downloadable music, audio, visual and audio-visual recordings and content; production of videocasts or podcasts; provision of entertainment, news or commentaries via videocasts or podcasts; talent shows, fashion shows and style shows; organisation of conferences, exhibitions and competitions; conducting auditions; arranging contests; publication of magazines, including online fashion magazines, featuring models created using artificial intelligence (AI) software; any of the aforesaid provided online via the Internet, via online social media platforms, mobile device communication apps or any other digital or electronic platform; information, advisory or consultancy services relating to any of the aforesaid.
