The mark consists of an orange box with six black and white curved stroke lines, alternating in color from black, to white, to black, to black again, to white and to black, the stripes begin from the lower right side of the box and continue to the upper left side of the box and with three of the lines extending outside the left border of the box, directly under the orange box are the words "Air Tiger Express" in black color with only the first letter of each word capitalized, approximately half of the letter "i" and all of the letters "g", "e", and "r" of the word "Tiger" are positioned beneath the orange box, the serifs on the left of the capitalized letter "E" of the word "Express" is positioned right under the lower right corner of the orange box.

The mark contains the colours black, white and orange.
