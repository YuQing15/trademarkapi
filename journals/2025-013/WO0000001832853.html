The mark consists of the stylized blue wording "MINH PHU SHARING GOOD FOOD", with "MINH PHU" appearing above "SHARING GOOD FOOD". To the left of this wording is a series of red triangles with curved sides forming an arch design in the shape of a stylized shrimp. Appearing below is the wording "Rice Shrimp" in red, device of a shrimp in red and three rice flowers in yellow. Putting beneath is the wording "NATURALLY DELICIOUS" in yellow and a curve in yellow. The whole elements are placed on a white background.

Registration of this mark shall give no right to the exclusive use of the words "SHARING GOOD FOOD"; "Rice Shrimp"; "NATURALLY DELICIOUS".

The mark contains the colours orange, blue, red, yellow and white.

The color orange appears on the curve, arcs. The color blue appears on the wording "MINH PHU", "SHARING GOOD FOOD". The color red appears on the wording "Rice Shrimp" and the shrimp. The color yellow appears on the wording "NATURALLY DELICIOUS", the rice flowers and the curve. The color white appears on the background of the mark.
