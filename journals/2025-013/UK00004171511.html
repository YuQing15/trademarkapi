SatPort Infrastructure

Class 38
Telecommunications services, namely the provision of infrastructure to satellite operators and business users to enable communication links between earth and satellites; satellite communication services; satellite transmission and receiving services; data transmission services over telecommunication networks; provision of earth-to-satellite transmitter and receiver installations; provision of satellite earth stations; satellite network communications; transmission of signals and data via fibre optic, wireless, cable and satellite networks; telecommunications network management services; rental and leasing of telecommunication installations for the transmission and receiving of signals and data via fibre optic, wireless, cable and satellite networks; rental and leasing of satellite earth stations; providing third party users with access to telecommunication infrastructure; consultancy and advisory services in connection with aforementioned services.
Class 42
Infrastructure as a service (IaaS) services for use in the field of satellite communications, featuring computer software platforms for syncing, storing, archiving and backing-up data to cloud servers, for managing, networking, collaborating within and providing remote access to databases, for sharing data, data processing and analysing data and for administration of computer local area networks management of computer applications and computer hardware and computer application distribution; infrastructure as a service (IaaS) services for data transmission and data reception, control and processing of information received at satellite ground stations; infrastructure as a service (IaaS) services, namely, providing remotely accessible electronic data storage; technical consultancy for the production, manufacture and finishing of installations, in particular satellites, ground stations and related installations, apparatus and technical project studies; technical consultancy, implementation and operation of information and communications solutions and services in the fields of satellite ground stations, satellite ground segments, control centres, satellite communications and telecommunications; operation of satellite ground stations, satellite ground segments and control centres and components thereof; consulting and contracting services for the development and use of satellite ground stations; providing temporary use of non-downloadable software for satellite network and device security.
