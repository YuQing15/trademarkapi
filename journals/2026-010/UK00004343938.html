TOOLIVERSE

Class 35
Advertising services; online advertising services; provision of advertising space on websites; providing business information relating to software and digital tools via an online directory; providing consumer information relating to software and digital tools, namely ratings, reviews and comparison information; price comparison services relating to software and digital tools; providing user rankings for commercial or advertising purposes relating to software and digital tools; providing user reviews for commercial or advertising purposes relating to software and digital tools; compilation and provision of information into online databases relating to software and digital tools; providing commercial information and advisory information for consumers and businesses relating to the selection and use of software and digital tools; web indexing for commercial or advertising purposes; lead generation services.
Class 41
Providing online electronic publications, not downloadable; providing online publications in the nature of newsletters, articles, guides and glossaries in the field of software, artificial intelligence tools and digital productivity; providing educational information relating to software and artificial intelligence tools via online publications; publication of texts, other than publicity texts, relating to software and artificial intelligence tools; providing information in the field of education relating to software and artificial intelligence tools; production of podcasts in the field of software and artificial intelligence tools; arranging and conducting of workshops and educational forums in the field of software and artificial intelligence tools.
Class 42
Providing temporary use of online non-downloadable software for searching, filtering, discovering, comparing and evaluating software and digital tools; software as a service (SaaS) for aggregating, analysing and presenting user review data relating to software and digital tools; software as a service (SaaS) for generating and displaying scoring, ranking and summary outputs derived from aggregated review data; platform as a service (PaaS) featuring computer software platforms for providing searchable directories and comparison functionality relating to software and digital tools; providing online non-downloadable software for publishing and displaying editorial selections, awards and recommendations relating to software and digital tools; providing search engines for the internet for searching and discovering software and digital tools; artificial intelligence as a service (AIaaS) for analysing and scoring software and digital tools.
