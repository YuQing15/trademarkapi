ULTIBED

Class 1
Chemical binders for use in soil stabilisation; chemical compositions for treating, stabilising and strengthening soil, excavated spoil, aggregates and recycled materials; chemical products for use in ground remediation; chemical additives for improving the structural performance of soils and construction materials; polymer based stabilising agents; cementitious and pozzolanic chemical blends for treatment of soils and sub bases; dust control preparations; adhesive substances for industrial purposes relating to the treatment or stabilisation of earth, soil, aggregates or construction materials.
Class 19
Treated soil, spoil, rock, sand, gravel, debris and excavated material for use as a building or civil engineering material; stabilised soil and aggregate replacement materials for construction and civil engineering; non metallic building materials incorporating chemical binders; backfill materials; sand, gravel, concrete, cement; crushed stones, rock, gravel, slag, recycled aggregate and/or concrete; silt, clay, recycled aggregate; sub-base materials for roads, pavements, footways and construction platforms; non metallic materials for ground stabilisation and reinforcement; premixed materials for reinstatement, civil engineering and utilities works; recycled construction materials produced using chemical soil binders.
Class 37
Soil stabilisation services; soil treatment and ground improvement services; ground remediation services; civil engineering services relating to the strengthening or stabilising of soil or sub bases; reinstatement of excavations and civil engineering works; construction services incorporating treated soils, binders or aggregate replacement materials; advisory and consultancy services relating to soil stabilisation, ground remediation or reinstatement.
Class 40
Treatment of soil, spoil or excavated materials using chemical binders; recycling and processing of excavated materials for use in construction; recycling and processing of sand, gravel, concrete, cement, stones, rock, gravel, slag, aggregate, silt, and clay for use in construction; custom treatment of soils and aggregates; material treatment services relating to soil stabilisation and ground improvement.
