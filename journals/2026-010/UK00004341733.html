Class 9
Downloadable computer software; downloadable mobile applications; downloadable web applications for career exploration, job search, recruitment, applicant tracking, candidate screening and skills assessment; downloadable electronic publications; downloadable guides, handbooks, articles and reports in the field of careers, occupations, education and recruitment; downloadable datasets and databases featuring job descriptions, occupational information, salary data, skills frameworks and labour market statistics; downloadable audio and video recordings, podcasts and multimedia content relating to careers, occupations and employability; computer software for administering psychometric, aptitude and skills tests; computer software for data analytics and career matching.
Class 35
Employment agency services; personnel recruitment; personnel placement; internship and graduate placement services; job board services; dissemination of job advertisements online; classified advertising services relating to jobs and careers; provision of business information and consultancy relating to recruitment, workforce skills, employability and labour market trends; compilation, systemisation and management of information into computer databases; data processing services; market research and data analysis relating to occupations, sectors, salaries, skills and hiring trends; organisation and conduct of career fairs and employer showcases for commercial or advertising purposes; recruitment process management; applicant tracking services; talent pooling services; employer branding services.
Class 38
Providing access to online portals, forums and communities for careers, occupations and recruitment; providing user access to searchable databases; electronic transmission of job postings, curricula vitae, application data and user profiles; streaming of audio and video content relating to career guidance and occupational information; web messaging and notifications relating to job opportunities.
Class 41
Career information and guidance services; vocational guidance; coaching and mentoring; workshops, seminars and webinars relating to careers, employability and workplace skills; publishing services; provision of online non downloadable publications, guides and articles featuring job profiles, occupational pathways, course options and professional development; development and delivery of training programmes, e learning, micro credentials and skills development courses; arranging and conducting career fairs for educational purposes; educational testing and assessment of skills, competencies and employability; provision of online quizzes and learning tools.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) and platform as a service (PaaS) featuring software for career exploration, job matching, recruitment workflow, applicant tracking, skills assessment and data visualisation; hosting of websites, databases and online platforms for the publication and sharing of job profiles and occupational information; design and development of computer software; maintenance and updating of software; application programming interfaces (APIs) for labour market and occupations data; providing temporary use of online non downloadable software for psychometric, aptitude and skills testing; data analytics and algorithmic matching services.
