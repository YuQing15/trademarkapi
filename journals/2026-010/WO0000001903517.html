Class 10
Mobility aids, included in this class, for the disabled; electrically, hydraulically or mechanically driven hoists for the disabled, namely bathtub lifts, lifting devices for persons, being boarding aids; technical assistive equipment for the disabled, namely walking aids, including forearm crutches, trolley walkers, rollators, walking frames, arm restraints, special cushions, seat cushions, toilet seats that can be fitted to wheelchairs, headrests, leg supports; assistive furniture, for the disabled, for baths and toilets, namely shower chairs of metal, shower seats, bathtub seats and inserts, shower and bathtub handles, attachments, armrests, handles and other holders for toilets, toilet chairs of metal or wood; accessories for the disabled for work and leisure, namely arthrodetic chairs, wall standing devices, righting seats, righting aids, turntables, telephone holders and medical fleeces; aids for physical therapy, namely hand and finger devices and devices for activating the hand and finger muscles, training equipment for activating the trunk and back muscles; inflatable mattress covers and vacuum seats, for medical purposes; back cushions for preventing pressure sores; special furniture for medical use; medical aids, namely seats, chairs and stools for the physically disabled and for persons with disabilities; hoists for persons with disabilities; cushions for medical purposes; special furniture for medical and therapeutic purposes, namely sick-beds, physical exercise apparatus, for medical purposes; medical pads and bed pads; technical aids for the disabled, namely rolling walkers, rollators, rollators (walking frames); aids for dressing and undressing, namely devices for putting on and removing stockings, shoe horns, button fasteners; housework aids for the disabled, namely key aids, universal holders, door handle extensions; eating and drinking aids for the disabled, namely special tableware, special cups, cutlery holders, cutting boards.
