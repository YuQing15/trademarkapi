Trade Mark Journal No.2026/010 6 March 2026

UK00004342498 18 February 2026 (9,35,42)

The mark consists of a stylized, abstract geometric design formed by continuous curved lines creating a layered structure. The design evokes the appearance of a fingerprint, symbolizing uniqueness and personal identity. At the same time, the inner negative space forms the silhouette of a human figure, representing an individual digital persona. The overall composition also suggests the letter “A”, referencing the name AVATID. The mark conveys the concept of digital identity, individuality, and the integration of human presence within a technological environment.

Class 9
Downloadable and recorded software; mobile applications; software using artificial intelligence for digital identity creation, avatar representation, appointment scheduling, automation and service management.
Class 35
Online marketplace services; business directory services; platform services connecting consumers with service providers; advertising and promotional services provided via digital platforms.
Class 42
Providing temporary use of non-downloadable software; Software as a Service (SaaS); Platform as a Service (PaaS); development, design and hosting of artificial intelligence software and digital identity platforms.
