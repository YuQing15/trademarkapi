Software for candidate assessment, applicant evaluation, skills testing, and recruitment workflow management. Downloadable and non-downloadable software for screening candidates and automating recruitment processes. Cloud-based software for talent analytics, competency modelling, and predictive hiring insights.

Platform-as-a-service (PaaS) for hosting candidate assessment tools, scoring engines, and recruitment analytics.
