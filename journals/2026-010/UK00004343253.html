Apex Trading

Class 38
Providing online forums and digital platforms for communication, discussion and information exchange; transmission of information, messages and content via online communities; electronic bulletin boards and chat room services; providing access to online discussion groups and educational communities; streaming and broadcasting of audio, video and multimedia content via the internet; podcast broadcasting services; transmission of educational content via digital communication networks; provision of access to digital platforms for the sharing of educational material; none of the aforesaid services relating to financial transaction execution or regulated financial services.
Class 41
Education; providing education and training in the fields of financial markets, trading, investing, risk management and macroeconomics; online education services; provision of online courses, seminars, workshops, masterclasses and tutorials; educational services provided via the internet, digital platforms and virtual environments; publication of educational content, articles, blogs, research papers and training materials; arranging and conducting educational conferences, live events and webinars; providing non-downloadable educational videos and audio recordings; educational discussion groups and study groups; mentoring and coaching services for educational purposes; providing online educational communities for learning and discussion; provision of educational information and analysis relating to financial markets; teaching and training services relating to trading psychology, portfolio construction and decision-making; continuing professional education, Providing educational simulations and paper-trading demonstrations for training purposes; none of the aforesaid services constituting regulated financial advice. .
