The door to more

Class 9
Downloadable computer software for enabling transmission of photographs, videos, and messages; Computer software for the collection, organising, transmission, storage, and sharing of data and information; Computer software which facilitates online services for social networking, building social networking applications, and for allowing data retrieval, upload, download, access, and management; Computer software to enable uploading, downloading, accessing, posting, displaying, tagging, blogging, streaming, linking, sharing, or otherwise providing electronic media or information via computer and communication networks; Computer software platforms for content hosting, distribution, and monetisation.
Class 35
Online subscription services for the purpose of allowing individuals to subscribe and access content uploaded by members of the service for entertainment purposes; Business management and administration services related to online content hosting and distribution platforms; Marketing and promotional services for content creators.
Class 38
Telecommunications services, namely, electronic transmission of data, messages, graphics, images, and information; Providing access to interactive communication services, including blogs and chatrooms; Peer-to-peer photo and video sharing services, namely, electronic transmission of digital files among internet users; Providing online forums for communication on topics of user interest; Electronic exchange of messages via chat lines, chatrooms, and internet forums.
Class 41
Providing entertainment services, namely, a website featuring non-downloadable videos, photographs, images, audio, and text via a global computer network; Electronic publishing services for others, including user-generated content; Providing online databases in the field of entertainment and community interest groups; Hosting and distribution of digital content for entertainment purposes.
