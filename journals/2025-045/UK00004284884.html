Gift Wellness, Not Things

Class 35
Retail and online retail services connected with the sale of candles, essential oils, incense, diffusers, bath and body products, skincare preparations, crystals, journals, yoga mats, meditation cushions, books, clothing, nutritional supplements, and home decor items relating to wellness and mindfulness; presentation of goods on communication media for retail purposes; provision of consumer product information relating to wellness and selfcare; marketing, advertising and promotional services in the fields of wellness, mindfulness, personal development and lifestyle; organisation of promotional campaigns and customer loyalty programmes relating to gifting and wellness; business management and administration services for wellness brands; provision of online marketplaces for buyers and sellers of wellness and lifestyle products.
Class 41
Provision and booking of wellness, mindfulness and lifestyle experiences; organisation, arrangement and booking of classes, workshops, retreats, and events in the fields of yoga, meditation, breathwork, sound healing, energy work, mindfulness, journaling, movement therapy, self-care, stress management, mental health and personal growth; provision of online and in-person educational programmes and training courses relating to wellbeing, mindfulness, emotional wellbeing, yoga, and lifestyle improvement; providing digital and physical activities designed for relaxation and personal enrichment; publication of content and information relating to wellness, mindfulness and lifestyle improvement, including via online platforms, social media and mobile applications; coaching and mentoring services for personal and emotional development; consultancy and advisory services relating to all the aforesaid services.
