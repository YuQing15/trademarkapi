The Valve Whisperer

Class 37
Repair, maintenance, servicing, overhaul, refurbishment, freeing and restoration of valves, penstocks, actuators, gearboxes, pumps, compressors, and associated mechanical, hydraulic, or pneumatic equipment; on-site and off-site repair, maintenance and servicing of industrial machinery, fluid handling systems, pipelines, and pressure equipment; installation, repair, maintenance, and adjustment of flow control devices, actuated valves, industrial gates, sluices, and mechanical process control assemblies; cleaning, rehabilitation, and refurbishment of seized, corroded, or malfunctioning valve and flow control systems; provision of advice, information, guidance, and consultancy in relation to all the aforesaid services; all the aforementioned services provided in relation to water, wastewater, oil and gas, energy, chemical processing, pharmaceutical, healthcare, manufacturing, and public infrastructure.
Class 42
Engineering services; technical consultancy, advisory and inspection services relating to valves, penstocks, actuators, gearboxes, flow control systems, and associated industrial equipment; condition assessment, testing, analysis, evaluation, and inspection of mechanical, hydraulic, or pneumatic valves and systems; preparation of technical reports, recommendations, and specifications in relation to performance, serviceability, safety, and suitability of valves, penstocks, and associated equipment; design, supervision, quality assurance, and oversight of maintenance, repair, or refurbishment operations for valves and flow control systems; provision of information, consultancy, guidance, and advisory services in relation to industrial valves and flow control systems in any industrial or public service sector including but not limited to water, wastewater, oil and gas, energy, chemical processing, pharmaceutical, healthcare, manufacturing, and public infrastructure; preparation and review of risk assessments, method statements, and technical documentation relating to all the aforesaid services.
