ConnexusMed

Class 35
Business consultancy relating to cross-border healthcare systems; advisory services relating to healthcare system navigation; business networking services in the field of cross-border healthcare systems; consultancy relating to healthcare system processes; all of the aforesaid being limited to the provision of informational and interpretive guidance about the structure and operation of healthcare systems in China for international audiences; none of the aforesaid being medical services or medical advice; and excluding arranging healthcare, recommending or referring healthcare providers, acting as an intermediary for healthcare services, and excluding travel booking.
Class 41
Online publication of non-downloadable articles relating to healthcare systems in China; providing online informational and educational content relating to the structure and operation of healthcare systems in China for international audiances; publication of analytical reports and briefings; providing online non-medical educational information relating to cross-border healthcare understanding; digital publishing services; publication of text-based content via a website; all of the aforesaid being informational and educational only and excluding medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, clinical services, referral, or arranging care.
