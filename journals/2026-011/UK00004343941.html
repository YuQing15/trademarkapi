HANDYSAFE

Class 35
Provision of an online marketplace for buyers and sellers of home repair and maintenance services; business management and administration of a vetting and accreditation scheme for tradespeople; commercial information and advice for consumers in the choice of handyperson and home maintenance professionals; personnel recruitment and management of independent contractors; background screening and business verification services for tradespeople.
Class 37
Handyperson services [maintenance and repair]; building maintenance and repair; installation of home safety equipment; installation of grab rails and mobility aids; property maintenance; minor electrical and plumbing repair services; emergency home repair services; consultancy and information services relating to home maintenance and repair.
Class 42
Software as a Service [SaaS] featuring software for the vetting and verification of service providers; Platform as a Service [PaaS] featuring a digital interface for coordinating handyperson services via messaging applications; hosting of a digital platform for identity verification and document authentication; electronic data storage of insurance and accreditation credentials.
Class 45
Identity verification services; security vetting of personnel; background screening of tradespeople for safety and trust purposes; provision of information relating to the safety and security of home environments; consultancy services relating to the verification of professional credentials and insurance compliance.
