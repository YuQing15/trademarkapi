MARCH CHEMICALS

International priority date claimed: 23 August 2024 (European Union Intellectual Property Office (EUIPO)) (019070903)
Class 1
Chemicals for use in agriculture, horticulture, forestry, the agrochemical industry and for domestic use; plant growth regulators, plant growth regulating preparations, microorganisms for use in stimulation of plant growth, natural and synthetic plant growth media, plant-growth promoters containing microorganisms, plant growth regulating compositions, preparations and substances; plant-growth regulators containing microorganisms for use in agriculture, horticulture, forestry, the agrochemical industry and for domestic use; substances for controlling the growth of aquatic plants; substances for promoting plant growth; substances for regulating growth in plants; chemicals for use in herbicides; chemical additives to insecticides; chemical additives to rodenticides, molluscicides and fungicides; chemicals for herbicides; chemicals for insecticides; chemicals for fungicides; all the above for use in agriculture, horticulture, forestry, the agrochemical industry and for domestic use.
Class 5
Fungicides, weed killers, herbicides, insecticides, biological fungicides, and parasiticides for killing vermin, all for medical, agricultural, horticultural and for domestic use; aquatic herbicides; biological herbicides; all the above for use in agriculture, horticulture, forestry, the agrochemical industry and for domestic use.
Class 35
The bringing together, for the benefit of others, in a shop, store, wholesale outlet or on-line website of a variety of insecticides, fungicides, herbicides, molluscicides, rodenticides and plant growth regulators, all for use in agriculture, horticulture, forestry and agrochemical industries and for domestic use, to enable customers to conveniently view, compare, select and purchase these goods; management of retail and wholesale outlets and of on-line shops; business analysis, research and information services; on-line advertising in computer networks; commercial or industrial management assistance; business information services; data processing, in particular data recording; import and export services; advertising and promotional services; business administration advice; business management advice; all the pre-mentioned being in relation to goods for use in for agriculture, horticulture, forestry, the agrochemical industry and for domestic use.
