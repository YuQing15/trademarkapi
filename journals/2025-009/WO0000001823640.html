The mark consists of the letter J reproduced in an original type font; beneath the letter J, there appears a geometric hexagonal shape. The vertical portion of the letter J and the hexagonal shape below are characterized in red/orange color, while the ending curved part of the letter is denoted in blue color, as per attached specimen.

The mark contains the colours Red/orange and blue.
