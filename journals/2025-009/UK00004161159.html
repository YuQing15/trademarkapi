POSSIBILITIES MADE REAL

Class 17
Nonwoven polymer plastic sheeting used as an insulating or vapor barrier for building construction; sheets of plastic fabric in the nature of coated polyolefin fabric used in construction to reduce air penetration in buildings; semi-processed foams or films of plastic; flexible plastic films, namely, greenhouse films for insulation purposes; plastic flexible polyethylene sheeting for agriculture purposes to cover animal feed and crops; plastic consumer sheeting, namely, plastic sheeting for use as drop cloths; plastic drop cloths; plastic film for commercial and industrial use, namely, silage film; polymer film for use as a moisture barrier providing barrier protection against moisture, oxygen, light or gases, or resistance against puncture, namely, medical packaging; plastic sheeting for use as weather proofing, water-proofing, insulation and moisture barriers, and as a weed barrier or mulch cover for landscaping use; house wrapping material in the nature of plastic sheeting for insulating houses; plastic sheeting for use as a weed barrier or mulch cover for landscaping use; plastic sheets in the nature of molded-in barrier liners marketed to packagers for hot fill, aseptic fill and retort closure manufacturing applications; plastic construction film, namely, plastic film for industrial use for industrial packing purposes and insulation purposes; Plastic film for industrial and commercial packing use, namely, personal care product films, and agricultural products film.
Class 24
Non-woven fabrics of man-made fibers and filaments in roll form suitable for making household furnishings and apparel and for industrial uses. Woven and non-woven fabrics for use in hygiene, filtration, healthcare, janitorial, and industrial applications; fabrics for use in making diapers, incontinence pads, feminine products, wound dressings, prep pads, bandages, surgical swabs, medical wipes, sterilization wraps; air-through bonded or thermobonded non-woven textile fabrics for use in the manufacture of medical and personal hygiene products; non-woven fabrics in the form of rolls for use in manufacture. Non-woven fabrics for use in filtration and healthcare area. Airlaid, spunlace and wetlaid nonwoven fabric made from natural and synthetic fibers for use in the manufacture of industrial and consumer goods, namely, filtration products, tea bags, coffee bags, food packaging, food pads, adhesive tapes, wallcovers, electrical paper, absorptive hygiene products, cleaning wipes, personal care products, napkins and healthcare products.
