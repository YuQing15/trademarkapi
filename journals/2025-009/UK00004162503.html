The mark consists of the uppercase letters "SD" in a stylized format, with the words "SUGAR DEVIL" written beneath it in uppercase using the Poppins font. The positioning, size, and color of the text may vary. Claim of Exclusivity: Exclusive rights to the combination of the letters "SD" and the words "SUGAR DEVIL" in the stylized format presented in the mark. Protection extends to the overall design, typography, and arrangement of elements as depicted. Color is not claimed as a feature of the mark. Scope of Protection: This trademark registration seeks to prevent unauthorized use of the letters "SD" and the words "SUGAR DEVIL" in any confusingly similar style, design, or format that could mislead consumers.
