Class 9
Computer software and mobile device software applications, namely for listing, searching and renting temporary lodging, for accessing information on temporary lodging, for providing reviews and feedbacks about listers and hosts of temporary lodging, for establishing communication between insurers, disaster victims and hosts, for managing disaster requests for lodging, for the bidding by hosts on insurance contracts and lodging requests.
Class 42
Providing software as a service, namely providing others with computer software and mobile device software applications in the field of disaster victim lodging rental and lodging reservations; providing software as a service, namely providing others with computer software and mobile device software applications featuring the listing, searching and rental of temporary lodging; providing software as a service, namely providing others with computer software and mobile device software applications for accessing information on temporary lodging, for providing reviews and feedbacks about listers and hosts of temporary lodging, for managing disaster requests for lodging, for the bidding by hosts on insurance contracts and lodging requests; providing of temporary use of online non-downloadable computer databases and online searchable databases featuring information, listings and announcements about rental and leasing advertisements; providing an online booking platform for the reservation of lodging for disaster victims.
Class 43
Providing on-line information concerning disaster victims temporary lodging reservations and temporary lodging rentals; providing temporary lodging reservation and temporary lodging rental services; providing temporary lodging reservation services for disaster victims; reservation services for the rental of lodging among property owners, insurance companies and disaster victims seeking lodging.
