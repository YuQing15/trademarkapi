UK00004162657 19 February 2025 (9,36,38,42)

USF

Class 9
Downloadable computer software platforms for deployment, transfer, and management of digital currency and cryptocurrency; downloadable computer software for managing cryptocurrency transactions using blockchain technology.
Class 36
Cryptocurrency exchange services; Cryptocurrency exchange services featuring blockchain technology; Cryptocurrency payment processing; Cryptocurrency trading services; Providing financial information in the field of cryptocurrency.
Class 38
Communication and electronic transmission for others of financial information, namely, communicating and routing trade information involving orders, entry and execution services, to others via a satellite and global computer network; payment transmission services, namely, providing electronic transmission of electronic payment data via a satellite and global computer network; transmission of encrypted communications using satellites; information, advice and consultancy relating to all the aforesaid services.
Class 42
Computer services, namely, acting as an application service provider in the field of information management to host computer application software for the purpose of featuring application programming interface (API) software for facilitating and managing interactions between digital currency and cryptocurrency; Platform as a service (PAAS) featuring computer software platforms for the efficient and secure sending, transferring, receiving, and storing of digital currencies and data; Providing technology information in the field of cryptocurrency; Providing user authentication services using blockchain-based software technology for cryptocurrency transactions; Technological consulting in the field of cryptocurrency; design and development of satellites, subsystems for satellites and components for satellites.
