PATIENT INFORMATION FORUM

Class 35
Opinion polling; public opinion surveys; business organisation and administration; organisation, operation and supervision of membership schemes for producers and providers of health care information and support; statistical analysis and reporting; organisation, operation, supervision and arranging of subscriptions to information packages; maintaining a registry of certified trusted information creators in the field of healthcare information; record keeping and administration in relation to certification services; information, advisory and consultancy services relating to the aforesaid.
Class 36
Charitable fundraising services; fundraising and sponsorship; information, advisory and consultancy services in relation to the aforesaid.
Class 41
Providing training and educational examination for certification purposes; information, advisory and consultancy services relating to the aforesaid.
Class 42
Certification [quality control] of health information and support; provision and administration of an examination and certification scheme to draw and set standards, and verify and certify that information provided in connection with health and healthcare is credible and trustworthy; provision of information, support, technical support and advisory services, all relating to the examination and certification of techniques, systems and practices and services connected with the provision of health information and support and in respect of the provenance, quality and accessibility of the health information and support provided; testing and inspection services to monitor compliance with certain requirements, standards, certification criteria and certification guidelines connected with the provision of health information; quality control services to monitor compliance with certain requirements, standards, certification criteria and certification guidelines connected with the provision of health information; auditing and evaluating participants in the certification scheme for certification purposes; certification services relating to all the aforesaid services; information, advisory and consultancy services relating to the aforesaid.
