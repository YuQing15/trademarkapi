ERGOCHAIR

Class 10
Medical apparatus; orthopaedic articles; therapeutic and assistive devices adapted for persons with disabilities; lumbar support apparatus; furniture adapted for people with mobility difficulties; chairs adapted for people with mobility difficulties; apparatus to support postural or disability requirements; body support systems, devices, parts, and fittings thereof .
Class 20
Furniture; custom-built furniture; office chairs; custom-built office chairs; leg rests; foot stools; arm rests; furniture adapted for people with mobility difficulties; chairs adapted for use by people with mobility difficulties; children's furniture; children's bespoke furniture; chair back rests, footplates, and arm rests; furniture parts; chair accessories including mounted laptop stands, wheels, neck rests, head rests, lumbar support apparatus; parts and fittings for the aforementioned.
Class 35
Office functions; collection of data; collection and systematisation of business data; collection and systematisation of information into computer databases; collection of data for the subsequent design of bespoke furniture; retail services connected with the sale of furniture, bespoke office chairs, furniture parts and accessories including mounted laptop stands, wheels, neck rests, head rests, and lumbar support apparatus; retail services connected with the sale of medical apparatus, orthopaedic articles, therapeutic and assistive devices adapted for persons with disabilities, furniture adapted for people with mobility restrictions; retails services connected with the sale of motor drive units [other than for land vehicles] electric motors, drive mechanisms, drive mechanisms for attaching to wheeled chairs to enable them to be driven, drive mechanisms for attaching to wheeled chairs to enable them to be driven and steered.
Class 42
Design of furniture, including computer-aided design; computer-aided design of bespoke furniture; computer software design; system for the design of furniture; information services relating to furniture design; software tool for the assessment of body measurements in relation to the design of bespoke furniture.
