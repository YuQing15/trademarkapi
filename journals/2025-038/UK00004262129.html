The mark consists of the word “SHOWBILL” in bold, uppercase black letters, with a stylised “S” featuring a curved theatrical flourish. The letters are set in a modern sans-serif typeface. The letters “UK” appear in a handwritten script in the upper right corner of the final letter “L”. Below the word “SHOWBILL” is the tagline “Your Front-Row Guide to the Stage” in sentence case. The entire design appears on a yellow background in the shape of a horizontal theatre ticket, incorporating a stylised Union Jack motif in the top-left corner.
