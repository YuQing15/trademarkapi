HERITAGE PASSPORT

Class 9
Computer software and mobile applications for recording, verifying and displaying provenance and ownership of collectibles, including vehicles, watches, jewellery, artworks, coins and other valuables; downloadable digital certificates; encoded identification tags including QR codes, NFC tags and barcodes; electronic databases recorded on computer media; blockchain-based software platforms for authentication and certification.
Class 16
Printed certificates of authenticity; printed passports and booklets relating to collectibles, including vehicles, watches, jewellery, artworks, coins and other valuables; stickers, labels and decals for identification and authentication; printed instructional and informational materials relating to provenance and heritage.
Class 35
Registry and record-keeping services relating to provenance and ownership of collectibles; compilation and systemisation of information into computer databases; providing an online marketplace for buyers and sellers of collectibles and related goods; business consultancy in the field of provenance and authentication of valuables; provision of commercial information relating to collectible assets.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) for provenance verification and certification; design, development and hosting of computer databases and digital platforms; blockchain-based verification services; electronic storage of ownership and provenance records; development of authentication and identification technology; hosting platforms for digital certificates of authenticity; Authentication services relating to provenance, history and ownership of collectibles, including vehicles, watches, jewellery, artworks, coins and other valuables; verification of digital and printed certificates of authenticity; expert authentication and validation services for valuables.
Class 45
Advisory services relating to intellectual property rights and authenticity of collectible assets.
