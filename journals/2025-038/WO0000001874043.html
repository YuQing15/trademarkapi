The mark consists of five intersecting lines forming a leaf design.

Class 9
Downloadable software in the nature of a mobile application for enabling users to access telemedicine services, for use in connection with obtaining a medical marijuana card, providing information on medical marijuana and medical marijuana card requirements, locating medical marijuana dispensaries, and checking inventory and availability of medical marijuana products for pickup; downloadable podcasts in the field of cannabis and medical marijuana.
Class 35
Advertising, marketing and promotion services in the field of medical marijuana dispensaries and medical practices that provide medical screenings for obtaining a medical marijuana card; promoting the goods and services of others by providing a website featuring the ratings, reviews, and recommendations on products and services for commercial purposes; providing the ratings, reviews and recommendations on products and services for commercial purposes posted by users via a web site; providing consumer information in the field of marijuana dispensary inventories and locations.
Class 41
Educational services, namely, conducting online seminars, classes, conferences and workshops in the field of cannabis and medical marijuana; on-line publication of journals or diaries [blog services] in the nature of articles in the field (s) of cannabis and medical marijuana; entertainment services, namely, providing entertainment via podcasts in the field of cannabis and medical marijuana.
Class 42
Software as a service (SaaS) services featuring software for providing a database of information regarding cannabis strains, cannabis dispensaries and their inventories and locations; software as a service (SaaS) services, namely, hosting software for use by others for enabling consumer users to search for, identify, and inspect services and goods offered by local service providers, and for use in providing recommendations, ratings and reviews about services and goods offered by local service providers; software as a service (SaaS) services, namely, hosting software for use by others for facilitating and managing online ordering, inventory tracking and delivery services; software as a service (SaaS) services, namely, hosting software for use by others for allowing users to create and manage online storefronts.
Class 44
Advisory services related to health and obtaining a medical marijuana card; health assessment services, namely, providing screening and recommendation for medical marijuana card; health care services, namely, providing telemedicine to provide screening and recommendation for medical marijuana card; medical services; medical screening; mobile healthcare services; providing medical information relating to medical marijuana card requirements and for the connection of patients to medical professionals to obtain screening and recommendation for medical marijuana card via an internet web site for medical professionals and medical patients; providing medical information via a web site; providing information on medical conditions and the health benefits of medical marijuana via a web site; providing on-line information, news and commentary in the field of health and wellness relating to medical marijuana; provision of health care and medical services by health care professionals via the Internet or telecommunications networks; telemedicine services.
