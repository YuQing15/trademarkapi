The mark consists of the word 'MODON' in a stylized Latin script. The first letter 'O' is depicted as an open circular shape with a dot above it, resembling the appearance of the Arabic letter "noon". The letter 'D' is also stylized with a curved design that gives it a unique appearance similar to the Arabic letter (dal). The three middle letters are abstracted from the original English letters to read "MODON" in Arabic from right to left. The mark is presented in black and white, with no claim made to color.
