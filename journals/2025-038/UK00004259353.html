MERCATO METROPOLITANO

Class 35
Business consultancy relating to retail market places for the sale of food and drink products and the provision of food and drink services; retail services relating to food and drink; the bringing together, for the benefit of others, of a variety of goods and services, enabling customers to conveniently view and purchase those goods and enjoy those services from retail stalls and shops, all for the sale of food and drink or the provision of food and drink services; organization and conducting of commercial events; event marketing.
Class 36
Real estate services; rental of retail units; rental of units for the provision of food and drink.
Class 41
Education; education services relating to food and drink; providing of training relating to cookery; cookery classes; spirit and wine courses; wine tastings; arranging and conducting entertainment, sporting, educational and cultural activities and events; arranging and organising parties and events; musical performances; organizing music concerts; musical entertainment services; information, consultancy and advisory services relating to the aforesaid.
Class 43
Services for providing food and drink; food and drink catering; take-away food services; provision of food and drink for corporate hospitality; hospitality services being food and drink services; consultancy services relating to food preparation; rental of furniture; rental of linens; rental of table settings; rental of equipment for the provision of food and drink; café, cafeteria and restaurant services; information, consultancy and advisory services relating to the aforesaid.
