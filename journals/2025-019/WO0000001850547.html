Electronic warfare systems for airborne, ground, and ship applications including manned and unmanned platforms, namely, an electronics system with cyber and electronic warfare capabilities comprised of computer hardware and downloadable software for providing warfighters with the ability to sense, identify, and transmit energy and signals across; computer hardware and downloadable and/or recorded software for use in unmanned aerial vehicles and drones; computer hardware and downloadable and/or recorded software for use in operating directed energy and microwave weapons; downloadable and/or recorded computer software that uses artificial intelligence for use in manned and remotely-operated unmanned aerial vehicles; downloadable and/or recorded software using artificial intelligence for detecting, identifying, tracking, and disabling unmanned aerial vehicles; downloadable and/or recorded computer software for sensing, receiving, monitoring, decoding, decrypting, and redirecting electromagnetic signals; downloadable and/or recorded software using artificial intelligence for disabling electromagnetic signals and devices; downloadable and/or recorded software using artificial intelligence for disabling electronics; computer hardware and downloadable and/or recorded software for use in directed energy weapons in the nature of infrared lasers capable of delivering significant thermal energy at a distance, high powered microwave and RF transmitters capable of disrupting radio receivers and other electronic devices, and visible lasers capable of temporarily and/or permanently disrupting optical sensors; apparatus, instruments, and equipment for electronic warfare, namely, electronic jamming devices, namely, electronic transmitters for jamming signals and interfering with and preventing the transmission and reception of electromagnetic signals; apparatus, instruments, and equipment for electronic warfare, namely, computer hardware and downloadable and/or recorded software for attacking and interfering with, disrupting, disabling, denying functional access to, seizing control of and destroying computers, computer networks, computer software, and computer network resources of an adversary; apparatus, instruments and equipment for electronic warfare, namely, receivers and transmitters for attacking and interfering with, disrupting, disabling, denying functional access to, seizing control of command, control, and communications networks, and computer network resources of an adversary; radio frequency transmitters and receivers; electronic sensors for use with electronic warfare systems, namely, for sensing undetectable radio frequency energy; electronic transmitters and electronic receivers for use with electronic warfare systems, namely, for transmitting and receiving undetectable radio frequency energy; wireless transmitters and receivers.
