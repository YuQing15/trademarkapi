OPTOGUIDE

Class 9
Downloadable and cloud-based software platforms for clinical decision support; mobile and web applications for use in optometric triage, ophthalmic diagnostics, referral generation, and optical health assessment; artificial intelligence software for the analysis of patient symptoms, clinical data, and ocular imaging including posterior and anterior eye structures; computer software for managing, reviewing, and interpreting ocular health records, visual field assessments, and clinical outcomes; downloadable software for machine learning, image processing, and data modelling in the field of vision science and healthcare; software tools for use in diagnostic assistance, anterior and posterior segment image analysis, visual field interpretation, clinical education, and optimisation of patient care in optometric and ophthalmological settings.
Class 42
Design and development of software as a service (SaaS) for clinical decision support, optometric triage, and vision care diagnostics; hosting of platforms and infrastructure for the analysis of ocular health data, patient records, and medical imaging; provision of non-downloadable software over the internet for artificial intelligence-assisted health applications; white-label development of hosted software platforms for clinics, optical groups, and healthcare institutions; software as a service for clinical workflow support, referral tools, and structured triage decision-making.
Class 44
Provision of clinical decision support services in the field of optometry and ocular health; provision of triage, eligibility assessment, diagnostic assistance, and referral pathway generation tools for healthcare professionals; dissemination of clinical information, patient management guidance, and structured care pathways via digital platforms; provision of consultancy services relating to visual health, image interpretation including anterior and posterior segment imaging and visual field testing; provision of artificial intelligence-enabled analysis of ocular symptoms, medical imaging, clinical records, and diagnostic tests for use by vision science professionals, optometrists, and ophthalmic practitioners.
