SigraFi

Class 9
Downloadable computer software for issuance, management, transfer, custody and redemption of tokenised or digital bonds; downloadable mobile applications for use as a cryptocurrency wallet; downloadable software for blockchain‑based financial transactions; downloadable smart contract software for use on distributed ledger networks; computer software for processing electronic payments and for transferring funds in connection with tokenised bond purchases; authentication software for controlling access to digital wallets and bond‑management tools.
Class 35
Business management and administration services relating to tokenised and digital bonds; marketing, promotional and advertising services for tokenised bond products; advisory and consultancy services in the field of business management of tokenised bond issuances; provision of business information in the field of tokenised finance; organisation of trade shows, conferences and webinars in the field of cryptocurrency and digital asset financing; database management services; data processing services; provision of an online marketplace for buyers and sellers of tokenised bonds.
Class 36
Financial services, namely issuance, underwriting and distribution of tokenised and digital bonds; financial analysis, valuation and management of tokenised bond instruments; investment advisory services in the field of tokenised finance; escrow services for tokenised financial instruments; custody and transfer of cryptocurrency and digital tokens; issuance, sale and redemption of a native cryptocurrency token and related digital assets; financial transaction via blockchain; electronic payment services; provision of financial information and analytics relating to tokenised bonds.
Class 42
Platform as a service (PaaS) featuring software for issuance, management, transfer and redemption of tokenised and digital bonds; software as a service (SaaS) featuring blockchain‑based financial transaction and smart contract management tools; design, development, customisation and implementation of computerised blockchain and distributed ledger systems; technical support, maintenance and integration services for blockchain‑based applications; provision of application programming interfaces (APIs) for tokenised bond issuance and cryptocurrency wallet integration.
