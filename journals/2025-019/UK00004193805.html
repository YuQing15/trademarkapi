ESIMORA

Class 9
Downloadable telecommunications and telephony software; software and applets for embedded universal integrated circuit cards (eUICC); software and applications for mobile devices for enabling, provisioning, activation and management of mobile network connectivity, eUICC/eSIM profiles and Mobile Station International Subscriber Directory Numbers (MSISDNs); software for Voice over IP (VoIP) and voice calling; software for distributing data plans; software for managing secure digital identities; authentication software for managing users in communication systems; software featuring virtual goods, namely eSIM cards and virtual SIM cards; data, information, audio, video and other multimedia, all being downloadable from the Internet for telecommunications and telephony applications.
Class 35
Retail and wholesale services, via stores and via global computer networks, for international data plans for travellers; retail and wholesale services, via stores and via global computer networks, for memory cards, telephone cards, cash cards, prepaid telephone cards, electronic chip cards, SIM cards and eSIM cards; online retail and wholesale services for the sale of in-house and third-party eSIM cards and virtual SIM cards.
Class 38
Telecommunications services; services for providing connectivity through the issuance, provisioning, activation and configuration of SIMs and eSIMs; services related to mobile network connectivity, including enabling and managing access to global telecommunications networks, provisioning and management of Mobile Station International Subscriber Directory Numbers (MSISDNs), Voice over IP (VoIP) and voice calling, distribution of data plans, and managing secure digital identities; provision of access to telecommunications networks, global computer networks and connections with computer databases and the Internet; transmission of voice, data, graphics, images, signals, multimedia and messages via telephony, cable, digital, electronic, optical and satellite networks; international data transmission; telephony services for international data plans for travellers; providing communication services through the use of phone cards or debit cards.
