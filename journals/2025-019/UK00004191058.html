KITCHWOOD

No claim is made to the exclusive right to use the words ‘kitchen’ or ‘wood’ apart from the mark as a whole.”

Class 21
Wooden cutting boards, chopping boards, serving boards, kitchen utensils, cooking spoons, spatulas, ladles, salad servers, rolling pins, bowls, plates, serving trays, coasters, kitchenware, tableware, mixing spoons, scoops, mortar and pestles, cake stands, measuring spoons, measuring cups, cheese boards, utensil holders, tea trays, spice racks, fruit bowls, butter dishes; all the aforementioned goods made wholly or principally of wood.
Class 35
Online retail services, wholesale services, and distributorship services relating to kitchenware, cookware, household utensils, and home goods, namely cutting boards, chopping boards, kitchen utensils, cooking spoons, bowls, plates, trays, and tableware, all primarily made of wood; retail services provided via online platforms, websites, and e-commerce marketplaces relating to wooden kitchen and home products; advertising and marketing services in the field of wooden kitchenware and homeware; import and export services relating to wooden household and kitchen goods.
