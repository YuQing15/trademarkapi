Anodes, cathodes, being parts of electrolyzers; machines for producing gas by electrolysis, namely hydrogen electrolysers; electrolysis cells being parts for hydrogen electrolyzers; cell stacks made from electrolytic cells as parts of electrolyzers; electrolyzers for hydrogen production.
